## Section Overview

### Typography

**Greeting Headline:**
- Font: Playfair Display or similar serif (elegant)
- Size: 32-42px (desktop), 24-32px (mobile)
- Color: Primary brand color (dark blue/green)
- Weight: Semi-bold

**Message Body:**
- Font: Open Sans, Roboto, or similar sans-serif
- Size: 16-18px (desktop), 14-16px (mobile)
- Line height: 1.6-1.8
- Color: Dark gray (#333 or #444)
- Weight: Regular

**Signature:**
- Font: Satisfy, Dancing Script (cursive) or elegant script
- Size: 24-28px
- Color: Brand color
- Actual signature image preferred

### Colors & Styling

**Background:**
- Light option: White or very light gray (#F8F9FA)
- Gradient option: Subtle brand colors (green to blue)
- Pattern option: Subtle Islamic geometric patterns at low opacity

**Borders/Dividers:**
- Top: 3px solid brand color
- Bottom: 1px solid light gray
- Quote marks: Large, light, decorative

**Spacing:**
- Section padding: 80px top/bottom (desktop), 40px (mobile)
- Content padding: 40px all sides
- Paragraph spacing: 20px between paragraphs

## Content Guidelines

### Tone & Voice

**Characteristics:**
- **Warm & Welcoming:** Like a kind elder or mentor
- **Authoritative but Approachable:** Credible without being intimidating
- **Inspirational:** Motivating and encouraging
- **Authentic:** Genuine, not marketing-speak
- **Islamic:** Natural integration of faith language
- **Professional:** Maintains dignity and formality appropriate to role

### Language Requirements

**Must Include:**
- Islamic greeting (Assalamu Alaikum)
- Reference to Islamic values/principles
- Mention of Smart Academy's unique value proposition
- Personal connection or relatable experience
- Forward-looking vision
- Invitation to engage further
- Closing with du'a (prayer)

**Must Avoid:**
- Excessive jargon
- Overly sales-oriented language
- Making comparisons that diminish other institutions
- Empty promises
- Statistics overload
- Impersonal corporate-speak

### Length Guidelines

**Standard Welcome:** 400-600 words (2-3 minutes reading time)
**Brief Welcome:** 150-250 words (1 minute reading time)
**Full Welcome (with modal):** 800-1000 words

## Accessibility Features

### Required Elements

**Visual:**
- High contrast text (minimum 4.5:1 ratio)
- Sufficient font size (minimum 16px body)
- Clear paragraph breaks
- Adequate line spacing

**Technical:**
- Proper heading hierarchy (H2 for main heading)
- Alt text for Principal's photo
- Descriptive link text (no "click here")
- Keyboard navigable
- Screen reader friendly
