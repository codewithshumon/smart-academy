### Testimonial Card Design

**Structure:**
```
┌────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  ❝ Large opening quote mark            │

│                                         │

```

### Typography

**Quote Text:**
- Font: Georgia, Merriweather (serif for elegance)
- Size: 18-22px (desktop), 16-18px (mobile)
- Line Height: 1.6-1.8
- Color: Dark gray (#2F4F4F)
- Style: Italic (optional)
- Quote marks: Large, decorative, brand color

**Name:**
- Font: Montserrat, Open Sans (sans-serif)
- Size: 18-20px
- Weight: Bold (700)
- Color: Dark gray (#212529)

**Relationship/Role:**
- Font: Open Sans
- Size: 14-16px
- Weight: Regular (400)
- Color: Medium gray (#6C757D)

**Location:**
- Font: Open Sans
- Size: 14px
- Weight: Regular
- Color: Light gray (#ADB5BD)

### Visual Elements

**Quote Marks:**
- Large opening quote mark (❝)
- Brand color (#2C5F2D or #0077B6)
- Font size: 60-80px
- Opacity: 50-70%
- Positioned top-left

**Photos:**
- Circle or rounded square
- Size: 80-100px diameter
- High-quality, professional
- Grayscale with color on hover (optional)
- Border: 3px solid brand color

**Rating Stars:**
- Color: Gold (#FFD700) for filled
- Color: Light gray (#DEE2E6) for empty
- Size: 18-20px each
- Always show 5 stars (filled based on rating)

### Color Scheme

**Background Options:**
- White cards on colored background
- Light gray cards (#F8F9FA)
- Subtle gradient (white to light brand color)

**Card Styling:**
- Border-radius: 12px
- Shadow: 0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)
- Hover: Lift shadow (0 8px 12px)
- Padding: 30-40px

## Content Guidelines

### Testimonial Selection Criteria

**Quality Standards:**
- Authentic and specific (not generic)
- Includes concrete details or examples
- Shows genuine emotion
- Highlights Smart Academy's unique value
- Appropriate length (100-200 words ideal)
- Permission granted for publication
- Photo available (preferred)

**Diversity Requirements:**
- Multiple perspectives (parents, students, teachers)
- Various grade levels represented
- Different backgrounds (economic, geographic)
- Both academic and Islamic focus
- Male and female voices
- Long-term and recent families

### Collection Process

**How to Gather Testimonials:**

1. **Survey Method:**
   - Annual satisfaction survey
   - Open-ended questions
   - Permission checkbox for publication
   - Contact information for follow-up

2. **Interview Method:**
   - Schedule recorded interviews
   - Professional transcription
   - Edit for clarity (maintain authenticity)
   - Get approval before publishing

3. **Written Submission:**
   - Testimonial submission form on website
   - Clear guidelines provided
   - Review and editing process
   - Follow-up for details if needed

4. **Video Testimonials:**
   - Professional recording
   - Good lighting and sound
   - Edited for length (1-2 minutes)
   - Transcription for accessibility

### Editing Guidelines

**Allowed Edits:**
- Correct grammatical errors
- Remove redundancy
- Shorten for conciseness
- Add context if unclear
- Translate Bengali to English (with permission)

**Not Allowed:**
- Change meaning or intent
- Add false information
- Remove negative points (address separately)
- Make sound overly promotional
- Use without permission

### Length Guidelines

**Homepage Display:**
- Short version: 2-3 sentences (50-75 words)
- Expandable to full version
- Link to detailed story (optional)

**Full Testimonials:**
- 150-250 words ideal
- Up to 400 words for compelling stories
- Break into paragraphs for readability

## Accessibility Features

### Required Elements

**Visual:**
- High contrast text (4.5:1 minimum)
- Readable font sizes
- Clear focus indicators
- Icon alternatives (text + icon)

**Technical:**
- Semantic HTML (blockquote, cite)
- ARIA labels for carousel
- Alt text for photos
- Keyboard navigation
- Screen reader friendly

**Example ARIA:**
```html
<div class="testimonials-carousel" 
     role="region" 
     aria-label="Testimonials from Smart Academy families">
  
  <blockquote class="testimonial" role="article">
    <p>As parents, we struggled to find a school...</p>
    <footer>
      <img src="rahman.jpg" alt="Mr. and Mrs. Rahman">
      <cite>
        <span class="name">Mr. & Mrs. Rahman</span>
        <span class="role">Parents of Aisha, Class 7</span>
      </cite>
      <div aria-label="Rating: 5 out of 5 stars">★★★★★</div>
    </footer>
  </blockquote>
  
  <button aria-label="Previous testimonial">&larr;</button>
  <button aria-label="Next testimonial">&rarr;</button>
</div>
```

## Legal & Privacy Considerations

### Consent Requirements

**Before Publishing:**
- Written consent from testimonial provider
- Photo permission (separate checkbox)
- Right to edit for clarity (disclosed)
- Right to remove upon request
- Anonymous option available

**Consent Form:**
```
Testimonial Release Form

I, [Name], grant Smart Academy permission to:
☐ Use my testimonial on the website and marketing materials
☐ Use my photo alongside the testimonial
☐ Edit my testimonial for length and clarity (maintaining original meaning)
☐ Use my full name and relationship to Smart Academy
☐ Keep my testimonial anonymous (use first name only or "Parent of Student")

I understand:
- I can request removal at any time
- My testimonial may be used in print and digital materials
- Smart Academy will not significantly alter the meaning of my words

Signature: _______________ Date: _______________
```

### Privacy Protection

**Options for Privacy:**
- First name only
- "Parent of Class 7 student"
- "Community Member from Ramganj"
- Generic photo or illustration
- Voice disguised in video testimonials
