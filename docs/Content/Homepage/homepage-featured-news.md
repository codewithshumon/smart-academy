## Content Management

### Content Entry Template

**For Each News Item:**
```yaml
title: "News Headline Here"
category: "Academic Excellence"
date: "2026-01-05"
author: "Smart Academy Communications"
featured_image:
  url: "/images/news/science-olympiad-2026.jpg"
  alt: "Students receiving awards at Science Olympiad"
excerpt: "Brief 2-3 sentence summary of the news..."
content: |
  Full article content goes here...
  Multiple paragraphs supported.
tags: 
  - science
  - competition
  - achievement
related_links:
  - title: "Our STEAM Program"
    url: "/academics/steam"
  - title: "Student Achievements"
    url: "/achievements"
status: "published"
featured: true
```

### Publishing Workflow

**Process:**
1. Content creator drafts news article
2. Upload images (optimized)
3. Add metadata (category, tags, etc.)
4. Submit for review
5. Editor approves/requests revisions
6. Publish with scheduling option
7. Share on social media
8. Archive after 6 months (move to news archive)

### Update Frequency

**Homepage Featured News:**
- Update at least weekly
- Show 3-6 most recent items
- Automatically pull from news database
- Manual feature/pin option for important stories

**News Archive:**
- All published news accessible
- Organized by month/year
- Searchable
- Category filtering
