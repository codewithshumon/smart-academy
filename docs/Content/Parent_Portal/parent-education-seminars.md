**Document Version:** 1.0

## Overview

Parent Education Seminars are comprehensive, in-depth educational programs that go beyond workshops to provide parents with deep understanding of critical topics related to education, child development, and Islamic parenting.

---

## Seminar vs. Workshop

### Key Differences

**Workshops:**
- Practical, skill-focused
- 1.5-2 hours
- Strategies and tools
- Immediate application
- Specific topics

**Seminars:**
- Comprehensive, knowledge-focused
- 3-4 hours or multi-day
- Deep understanding
- Long-term application
- Thorough exploration

## Seminar Format

### Multi-Session Seminars

**Some Seminars Span Multiple Sessions:**
- **Format:** 2-3 sessions, 2 hours each
- **Schedule:** Typically consecutive weeks
- **Benefit:** Deeper exploration, time to practice
- **Follow-up:** Application and review

### Intensive Seminars

**Full-Day Seminars:**
- **Format:** Single day, 5-6 hours
- **Schedule:** Saturday or school holiday
- **Includes:** Multiple sessions, breaks, lunch
- **Benefit:** Comprehensive coverage in one day

## FAQ

**Q: What's the difference between seminars and workshops?**
A: Seminars are more comprehensive (3+ hours), workshops are practical skill-building (1.5-2 hours). Seminars provide deeper understanding.

**Q: Do I need to attend all seminars in a series?**
A: No, each seminar stands alone. However, attending series provides comprehensive understanding of topic.

**Q: Are seminars appropriate for parents of all grade levels?**
A: Some are specific to ages/grades, others are relevant to all. Check description for details.

**Q: Can I bring my spouse or another family member?**
A: Yes! Both parents/guardians welcome. Encouraged for both to attend.

**Q: Is there childcare available?**
A: Not typically. Plan accordingly for childcare.

**Q: What if I can't afford the fee?**
A: No one turned away for inability to pay. Contact us confidentially.
