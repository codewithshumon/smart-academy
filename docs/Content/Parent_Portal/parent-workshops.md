# Parent Workshops

## Educational Workshops for Parents

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026

---

## Overview

Smart Academy offers regular workshops designed to help parents support their children's education and development. These practical, research-informed sessions provide strategies, tools, and resources for effective parenting.

---

## Workshop Philosophy

### Why Parent Workshops?

**Benefits to Parents:**
- Learn effective parenting strategies
- Understand child development
- Support learning at home
- Connect with other parents
- Access expert knowledge

**Benefits to Children:**
- Better support at home
- Consistent approaches between home and school
- Improved academic outcomes
- Stronger character development
- More confident parents

**Islamic Perspective:**
- Parenting is sacred trust (Amanah)
- Seeking knowledge is obligation
- Learning to raise children according to Islamic principles
- Community of learning and support

## Workshop Categories

### Academic Support Workshops

**Purpose:**
Help parents support their children's academic learning and success.

**Topics:**

**1. Supporting Reading at Home:**
- Importance of reading
- Reading strategies by age
- Selecting appropriate books
- Making reading enjoyable
- Supporting struggling readers
- Islamic literature and stories

**2. Mathematics at Home:**
- Everyday math activities
- Helping with homework
- Building math confidence
- Math games and activities
- Problem-solving strategies
- Islamic contributions to mathematics

**3. Homework Help Without Tears:**
- Creating homework routines
- Effective homework support
- When and how to help
- Dealing with homework struggles
- Balancing support and independence

**4. Technology and Learning:**
- Educational apps and websites
- Managing screen time
- Online safety
- Using technology for learning
- Digital citizenship basics
- Balancing technology with Islamic values

**2. Raising Muslim Children in Modern World:**
- Maintaining Islamic identity
- Navigating modern challenges

**3. Teaching Islamic Values:**
- Everyday Islamic character building

- Teaching prayer and Islamic practices
- Discussing Islamic topics with children
- Creating Islamic home environment

**4. Quran in Family Life:**
- Integrating Quran into daily life
- Family Quran time
- Memorization support strategies
- Understanding and teaching Quran
- Living according to Quran

## Workshop Structure

### Typical Workshop Format

**Duration:** 1.5 - 2 hours

**Schedule:**
- **Welcome and Introductions (10 min)**
- **Presentation (45-60 min)**
- **Interactive Activity (20-30 min)**
- **Q&A Session (15-20 min)**
- **Resources and Takeaways (10 min)**

**Materials Provided:**
- Handouts with key points
- Resource lists
- Activity materials
- Reading recommendations
- Contact information for follow-up

## Registration

### How to Register

**Online Registration:**
- Available on parent portal
- Select workshop(s)
- Provide contact information
- Submit registration

**Email Registration:**
- workshops@smartacademy.edu.bd
- Include name, workshop title, contact

**Phone Registration:**
- Call main office during school hours
- Provide name and workshop

**In-Person:**
- Sign up at main office
- Register at PTA meetings

### Workshop Details

**Location:**
- Smart Academy campus
- Specific rooms announced with registration

**Time:**
- Weekends (typically Saturday morning)
- Or as announced for specific workshop

**Cost:**
- Most workshops: Free for Smart Academy parents
- Some specialized workshops may have fee (announced)
- Materials included

**Childcare:**
- Not typically provided
- Plan accordingly for childcare
