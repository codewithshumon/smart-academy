## Overview

The Smart Academy Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) is an organization of parents, teachers, and staff working together to support the school, enhance educational opportunities, and build a strong school community.

---

## What is the PTA?

### Purpose and Mission

**Our Mission:**
To foster collaboration between parents and teachers, support the educational and Islamic development of students, enhance school programs and facilities, and build a vibrant, supportive school community.

**Islamic Foundation:**
- Working together for the sake of Allah
- Service to the community as Sadaqah
- Consultation (Shura) in decision-making
- Unity and cooperation
- Supporting education as sacred trust (Amanah)

### What We Do

**Support School:**
- Volunteer for events and activities
- Fundraise for school improvements
- Provide parent perspective on issues
- Support school programs and initiatives

**Enhance Education:**
- Organize educational programs for parents
- Support classroom activities
- Fund special projects and resources
- Enhance school facilities

**Build Community:**
- Host social events and gatherings
- Welcome new families
- Foster connections between families
- Celebrate our diverse community

## PTA Structure

### Leadership

**Executive Committee:**
- **President:** Leads PTA, presides over meetings, represents PTA to administration
- **Vice President:** Supports President, presides when President absent
- **Secretary:** Records minutes, maintains records, handles correspondence
- **Treasurer:** Manages PTA finances, maintains financial records

**Class Representatives:**
- Represent each class or grade level
- Communicate between PTA and parents
- Gather parent feedback and concerns
- Coordinate class activities

**Committee Chairs:**
- Head various PTA committees
- Plan and execute committee activities
- Report to Executive Committee

### Committees

**Event Planning Committee:**
- Plan and coordinate school events
- Organize volunteers
- Manage logistics
- Coordinate with school administration

**Fundraising Committee:**
- Plan fundraising activities
- Coordinate campaigns
- Manage donation drives
- Organize fundraising events

**Parent Education Committee:**
- Plan parent workshops and seminars
- Identify educational topics
- Coordinate speakers and resources
- Organize training sessions

**Facilities Enhancement Committee:**
- Identify improvement needs
- Plan enhancement projects
- Coordinate volunteer efforts
- Manage improvement projects

**Volunteer Coordination Committee:**
- Recruit volunteers for activities
- Coordinate volunteer schedules
- Match volunteers with opportunities
- Recognize volunteer contributions

**Welcome Committee:**
- Welcome new families
- Orient new parents to school
- Organize buddy system
- Host new family events

## Joining the PTA Leadership

### Leadership Opportunities

**Executive Committee Elections:**
- **When:** Annual election (typically beginning of academic year)
- **Process:** Nominations, campaigning (minimal), voting
- **Term:** One academic year, can serve multiple terms
- **Requirements:** Parent of current student, commitment to PTA mission

**Committee Chairs:**
- Appointed by Executive Committee
- Interest in specific committee area
- Willingness to lead and organize
- Regular availability

**Class Representatives:**
- One representative per class or grade
- Represent parent concerns
- Communicate PTA information
- Organize class activities

### Responsibilities

**Time Commitment:**
- Executive Committee: 5-10 hours per month
- Committee Chairs: 3-5 hours per month
- Class Representatives: 2-3 hours per month
- Additional during major events

**Expectations:**
- Regular meeting attendance
- Active participation in committees
- Islamic character in all interactions
- Collaboration with school administration
- Respect for confidentiality
- Commitment to PTA mission

## PTA and School Administration

### Collaborative Relationship

**Working Together:**
- PTA supports school programs and initiatives
- Administration supports PTA activities
- Regular communication between PTA and administration
- Mutual respect and understanding
- Shared vision for school success

**Roles and Boundaries:**
- PTA is advisory and supportive body
- Final decision-making authority rests with school administration
- PTA does not involve itself in personnel matters
- PTA respects Islamic guidelines and school policies
- Administration values PTA input and collaboration

### Communication with Administration

**Regular Contact:**
- PTA President meets regularly with Principal
- Administration representative at PTA meetings
- Open communication channels
- Discussion of initiatives before implementation
- Collaboration on events and activities
