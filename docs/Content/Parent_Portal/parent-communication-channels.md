## Overview

**School Email Addresses:**

- **Admissions:** admissions@smartacademy.edu.bd
- **Accounts:** accounts@smartacademy.edu.bd
- **Principal:** principal@smartacademy.edu.bd

**Email Uses:**
- Official notices and announcements

- Informing of absences
- General inquiries

## Traditional Communication

### Telephone Communication

**Main Office:**
- **Phone:** [Number]
- **Hours:** Sunday-Thursday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- **For:** General inquiries, urgent matters, leaving messages

**When to Call:**
- Urgent matters that cannot wait
- Emergency situations
- Quick questions needing immediate answer
- Scheduling appointments

**Calling Guidelines:**
- Call during office hours
- Identify yourself and your child
- Be concise and respectful
- Leave message if no answer

**Teacher Phone Calls:**
- Teachers may call parents to:

  - Follow up on issues
  - Schedule meetings
- Calls typically made after school hours or during planning time
- If you miss call, return message promptly

**Report Cards:**

**Progress Reports:**
- Monthly or periodic updates
- Available on parent portal

## Face-to-Face Communication

### Parent-Teacher Conferences

**Scheduled Conferences:**
- **Mid-term:** After mid-term examinations
- **End of Term:** After final examinations
- **By Request:** By parent or teacher request
- **Duration:** Typically 20-30 minutes

**Purpose:**
- Discuss academic progress
- Review strengths and areas for improvement
- Set goals
- Build partnership
- Address concerns

**Scheduling:**
- Scheduled through portal or by calling main office
- Multiple time options available
- Both parents encouraged to attend
- Students may be included for part of conference (age-dependent)

### School Meetings and Events

**Types of Meetings:**
- **General Parent Meetings:** For all parents
- **Grade-Level Meetings:** Specific to child's grade
- **Orientation Meetings:** Beginning of year or new enrollment
- **Workshops:** On specific topics
- **PTA Meetings:** Monthly or as scheduled

**Events:**
- **Orientation:** New student and parent orientation
- **Open House:** Visit classrooms and meet teachers
- **Sports Day:** Annual sports events
- **Science Fair:** Student projects and presentations
- **Cultural Programs:** Islamic celebrations and events
- **Award Ceremonies:** Recognition of achievements

**Benefits of Attending:**
- Face-to-face interaction
- Better understanding of school programs
- Networking with other parents
- Direct communication with teachers and administration
- Demonstrate commitment to child's education

### Informal Communication

**Drop-Off and Pick-Up:**
- Brief interactions with teachers
- Not appropriate for extended discussions
- Schedule appointments for longer conversations
- Respect teachers' preparation time

**School Events:**
- Opportunities to meet teachers informally
- Build community relationships
- See school in action
- Celebrate student achievements

## Communication Protocols

### Contacting Teachers

**Preferred Methods:**
1. **Parent Portal Message:** For non-urgent matters
2. **Email:** For questions requiring detailed response
3. **Phone:** For urgent matters
4. **In-Person:** By appointment only

**Guidelines:**
- Use appropriate channel for situation
- Be respectful of teachers' time
- Allow reasonable response time (24-48 hours on school days)
- Avoid contacting teachers on weekends or holidays
- Come to appointments on time

### Contacting Administration

**Principal:**
- **When:** Serious concerns, policy questions, strategic matters
- **How:** Through main office for appointment or email
- **Response Time:** Within 2-3 days

**Vice Principal:**
- **When:** Academic concerns, day-to-day operations, discipline
- **How:** Email or phone, or through main office
- **Response Time:** Within 1-2 days

**Academic Coordinator:**
- **When:** Curriculum, teaching methods, academic programs
- **How:** Email or phone
- **Response Time:** Within 1-2 days

**Department Heads:**
- **When:** Subject-specific questions or concerns
- **How:** Email
- **Response Time:** Within 1-2 days

**Admissions Officer:**
- **When:** Enrollment, admission process, new registrations
- **How:** Email, phone, or in-person
- **Response Time:** Within 1 day

**Accounts Manager:**
- **When:** Fee payments, financial matters
- **How:** Email or in-person during office hours
- **Response Time:** Within 1 day (urgent matters sooner)

### Urgent Communications

**Emergency Situations:**
- Medical emergencies
- Child not picked up on time
- Serious behavioral incidents
- Security concerns
- Crisis situations

**Contact Immediately:**
- **Phone:** Main office during school hours
- **Emergency Number:** [After-hours emergency number, if available]

**For True Emergencies Only:**
- Life-threatening situations
- Medical emergencies
- Serious safety concerns

## Communication Etiquette

### Islamic Etiquette

**All Communication:**
- Begin with Assalamu Alaikum
- Use kind and respectful language
- Avoid harshness
- Be patient
- Maintain dignity of all parties
- Avoid gossip and backbiting

**Even When Disagreeing:**
- Respectful tone
- Focus on issue, not person
- Listen to understand
- Give benefit of doubt
- Seek solutions, not blame
- Forgive and move forward

### Professional Etiquette

**Email:**
- Clear subject line
- Concise and specific
- Proper greeting and closing
- Professional tone
- Proofread before sending

**Phone:**
- Identify yourself
- Be clear about purpose
- Respect time (yours and theirs)
- Take notes if needed
- Thank them for their time

**In-Person:**
- Arrive on time for appointments
- Dress appropriately and modestly
- Respect Islamic gender interaction guidelines
- Maintain Islamic demeanor
- Thank them for their time

## Communication Challenges

### Common Challenges

**Language Barriers:**
- School provides translations when possible
- Use visual aids and demonstrations
- Bring translator to meetings if needed
- Ask for clarification
- Use simple language

**Technology Access:**
- Computer access available at school
- Training provided for portal use
- Print materials available when requested
- Phone communication always available

**Busy Schedules:**
- Portal accessible 24/7
- Flexible meeting times available
- Phone conferences when in-person not possible
- Summaries provided of meetings missed

**Multiple Children:**
- Separate communications for each child
- Family meetings available
- Coordinated conferences when possible
- Sibling considerations

## Communication Best Practices Summary

### For Parents

**Do:**
- Check portal regularly
- Respond to communications promptly
- Use appropriate channel for each situation
- Be respectful and Islamic in all communications
- Ask questions when unsure
- Share important information with school
- Attend meetings and events
- Provide constructive feedback

**Don't:**
- Wait until crisis to communicate
- Use social media for concerns or complaints
- Ignore school communications
- Be rude or aggressive
- Contact teachers at inappropriate times
- Share other families' private information
- Expect immediate response to non-urgent matters

### For School

**We Commit To:**
- Communicate regularly and clearly
- Respond within reasonable time
- Be respectful and Islamic
- Keep you informed
- Listen to your concerns
- Provide multiple communication channels
- Value your feedback
- Maintain confidentiality
