## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you hire fresh graduates?**  
**A:** Yes, for entry-level positions. We provide extensive training and mentorship for beginners with the right attitude and qualifications.

**Q: Must I be Muslim to apply?**  
**A:** While Smart Academy has an Islamic ethos, we welcome qualified candidates of all backgrounds who respect and support our mission and values.

**Q: Is accommodation provided?**  
**A:** Currently no on-campus housing. However, we help find nearby accommodation and may provide housing allowance for senior positions.

**Q: What is the dress code?**  
**A:** Professional and modest. For women: hijab, loose clothing (shalwar kameez/abaya). For men: formal shirts and pants. Islamic guidelines observed.

**Q: Can I apply for multiple positions?**  
**A:** Yes, but indicate your preferences clearly. We'll consider you for positions best matching your qualifications.
