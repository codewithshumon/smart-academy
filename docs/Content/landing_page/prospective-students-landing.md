## Frequently Asked Questions

### Admissions Questions

**Q: What age can my child join?**  
**A:** We accept students from age 3 (PlayGroup) to Class 9. Age requirements:
- PlayGroup: 3 years
- KG: 4 years
- Class 1: 5-6 years
- Class 2-9: Appropriate age for grade

**Q: When does admission open?**  
**A:** Admission opens in December for the following academic year (April start). 
However, we accept mid-year transfers if seats are available.

**Q: Is there an entrance test?**  
**A:** Yes, a simple assessment to understand your child's level. For PlayGroup-KG, 
it's informal observation. For higher classes, age-appropriate tests in English, 
Math, and General Knowledge.

**Q: What if my child doesn't pass the test?**  
**A:** We don't have a "pass/fail" mentality. The test helps us place your child 
appropriately and identify support needs. We rarely reject students based on test 
scores alone.

**Q: Can we visit before applying?**  
**A:** Absolutely! We encourage campus visits. Call us to schedule a tour or attend 
our monthly open house.

---

### Academic Questions

**Q: What curriculum do you follow?**  
**A:** We follow the Bangladesh National Curriculum enhanced with:
- Islamic studies (Quran, Hadith, Fiqh)
- STEAM education
- Character development
- 21st-century skills

**Q: Is it all in English?**  
**A:** Most subjects are taught in English, but we also teach Bengali (as a subject

**Q: How much homework do students get?**

- Class 4-5: 1-1.5 hours daily
- Class 6-9: 1.5-2 hours daily

**Q: Do you offer tutoring?**  
**A:** Yes, free after-school tutoring for students who need extra help.
