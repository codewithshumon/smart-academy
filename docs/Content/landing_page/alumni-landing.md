## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I register in the alumni network?**  
**A:** Visit alumni.smartacademybd.com and create your profile using your graduation year and student ID. Contact alumni@smartacademybd.com if you need help.

**Q: Can I visit the campus anytime?**  
**A:** Yes! Alumni are always welcome. Just call ahead to coordinate timing (especially if you want to meet specific teachers).

**Q: How can I hire Smart Academy students/alumni?**  
**A:** Post job openings on our alumni portal job board or contact alumni@smartacademybd.com.

**Q: Can I sponsor a student anonymously?**  
**A:** Yes! Contact our financial aid office for anonymous scholarship options.

**Q: Do alumni get discounts for their children?**  
**A:** Yes, 20% sibling discount for children of alumni in addition to standard sibling discounts.

**Q: How do I update my contact information?**  
**A:** Log into the alumni portal and update your profile, or email alumni@smartacademybd.com.

**Q: Can alumni attend school events?**  
**A:** Yes! You'll receive invitations to open events. Some events (like sports day) are open to all alumni.
