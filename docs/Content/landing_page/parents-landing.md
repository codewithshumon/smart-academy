**A:** Call the school office before 9:00 AM or submit a leave application through the parent portal. Medical certificate required for absences over 3 days.

**Q: How often are report cards issued?**  
**A:** Report cards are issued three times per year (end of each term). Progress reports are available monthly on the portal.
