## Competition Rules

### Eligibility

**Participation**:
- All students expected to participate
- Students may enter up to 3 track events + 1 field event
- Medical exemptions require parent note
- Parent consent required for all students

### Track Event Rules

**Starting**:
- False starts result in disqualification
- Staying in assigned lane required
- Starting blocks for older students only

**Finishing**:
- Must finish under own power
- Crossing finish line correctly
- Photo finish for close races

### Field Event Rules

**Long Jump**:
- Three attempts per student
- Foul lines monitored
- Best jump measured for scoring

**High Jump**:
- Fosbury flop technique (Class 6-9)
- Scissors jump (younger students)
- Three attempts per height

**Shot Put**:
- Proper technique required
- Safety zone enforced
- Measured from inside of landing circle
