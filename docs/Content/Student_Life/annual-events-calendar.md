## Calendar Overview

### Event Philosophy

**Purposeful Events**:
- Celebrate achievements and milestones
- Build school community and spirit
- Provide opportunities for student leadership
- Extend learning beyond classroom
- Create joyful memories
- Represent Islamic values

### Event Categories

**Academic**: Intellectual competitions and showcases
**Cultural**: Celebrations of heritage and diversity
**Islamic**: Religious observances and celebrations
**Athletic**: Sports competitions and events
**Arts**: Creative expression and performance
**Social**: Community building and fun

## Academic Events

### January

**New Student Orientation** (January 8-10)
- **Who**: New admissions and their families
- **What**: School introduction, campus tour, meetings
- **Purpose**: Welcome new families, transition support

**First Monthly Tests** (January 15)
- **Who**: All students
- **What**: Subject assessments for January learning
- **Purpose**: Early academic check

### February

**Science Fair** (February 26-28)
- **Who**: Class 3-9
- **What**: Student science projects displayed and judged
- **Venue**: School auditorium and halls
- **Awards**: Ribbons and certificates

### March

**Term 1 Examinations** (March 10-18)
- **Who**: All students
- **What**: Comprehensive term assessments
- **Schedule**: Modified exam timetable

**Quran Competition** (March 30)
- **Who**: All students
- **What**: Quran recitation and memorization contest
- **Categories**: By grade and proficiency level

### April

**Spelling Bee Competition** (April 15)
- **Who**: Class 1-8
- **What**: English spelling competition
- **Rounds**: Grade-level then school-wide

### May

**Math Olympiad** (May 10)
- **Who**: Selected students Class 4-9
- **What**: Problem-solving mathematics competition
- **Format**: Individual and team challenges

**Career Day** (May 15)
- **Who**: Class 6-9
- **What**: Guest speakers from various professions
- **Purpose**: Career awareness and inspiration

### October

**Term 3 Final Examinations** (October 1-15)
- **Who**: All students
- **What**: Comprehensive final assessments

### December

**Final Examinations** (December 1-8)
- **Who**: All students
- **What**: End-of-year comprehensive exams

## Event Participation Guidelines

### Student Participation

**Expected Participation**:
- All students participate in grade-appropriate events
- Attendance at major school events expected
- Represent school positively at external events
- Demonstrate Islamic character at all events

**Voluntary Participation**:
- Some competitions by choice/qualification
- Clubs and activities based on interest
- Performance groups by audition
- Advanced teams by selection

### Parent Involvement

**Attendance Encouraged**:
- Sports Day
- Annual Cultural Program
- Annual Day
- Graduation ceremonies
- Award ceremonies

**Volunteer Opportunities**:
- Event planning and setup
- Concessions and refreshments
- Chaperoning field trips
- Sharing expertise

## Event Communication

### Notification Methods

**Advance Notice**:
- School calendar published annually
- Monthly newsletters highlight upcoming events
- Email notifications for major events
- SMS reminders for important dates
- Student portal calendar

**Event Details**:
- Specific dates, times, locations
- Participation requirements
- What students need to bring/wear
- Whether parents should attend
- Any costs (if applicable)

## Event Policies

### Attendance Policy

**Mandatory Events**:
- Sports Day
- Annual Day/Cultural Program
- Graduation ceremonies (for graduating classes)
- Major examinations

**Excused Absences**:
- Illness (with notification)
- Family emergency
- Religious observance
- Approved reasons

### Conduct Expectations

**At All Events**:
- Islamic character and manners
- Respectful behavior
- Proper uniform unless otherwise specified
- Representing Smart Academy positively
- Following event-specific rules

**For Competitive Events**:
- Good sportsmanship
- Respect for opponents
- Grace in victory and defeat
- Fair play and honesty
- Shukr and Sabr
