## Important Notes

### Weather-Related Adjustments

**Monsoon Season** (June-September):
- Heavy rain may cause early dismissal
- Indoor activities during heavy rain
- Parent notification via SMS for any changes

**Summer Months** (April-June):
- Reduced outdoor activities during peak heat
- Extra hydration breaks
- Modified PE schedule

**Winter Months** (November-February):
- Regular outdoor activities
- Comfortable learning environment
- No major schedule modifications

### Schedule Changes

**Notification Method**:
- SMS to all parents
- Email notification
- Website announcement
- Student portal update

**Emergency Closures**:
- Political situations
- Natural disasters
- Health emergencies
- Notification sent by 7:00 AM
