## About Environmental Club

### Mission

> "To develop environmentally conscious students who understand their responsibility as stewards (khalifah) of Allah's creation, taking action to protect and preserve the environment for future generations."

### Islamic Foundation

**Stewardship (Khalifah)**:
> *"And it is He who has made you successors (khalifah) upon the earth..."* — Quran 6:165

**Humans as Stewards**:
- Earth is a trust (amanah) from Allah
- We are responsible for its care
- Will be held accountable for how we treat it
- Creation praises Allah—we should protect it

**Waste Not, Want Not**:
> *"Indeed, He [Allah] does not like those who commit wastefulness."* — Quran 6:141
> *"And give the relative his right, and the needy, and the traveler, and do not be wasteful."* — Quran 17:26

**Environmental Justice in Islam**:
- Protecting animals and plants
- Conserving resources
- Avoiding harm to creation
- Ensuring access to clean resources for all

### Club Details

**Eligibility**: Class 3-9
**Meeting**: Every Friday, 3:30-4:30 PM
**Location**: Science Lab 3 and Campus Gardens
**Sponsor**: Ms. Fatima Rahman (Science Teacher)
**Dues**: None (funded through school and donations)

## Green Initiatives

### School Recycling Program

**What We Recycle**:
- Paper and cardboard
- Plastic bottles
- Metal cans
- Ink cartridges

**How It Works**:
- Recycling bins placed throughout campus
- Weekly collection by club members
- Sorting and proper disposal
- Tracking and reporting results

**Achievements**:
- 500+ kg recycled annually
- Reduced campus waste by 30%
- Raised awareness among students

### Tree Planting and Care

**Campus Trees**:
- Annual tree planting campaign
- Maintaining existing trees
- Creating green spaces
- Fruit tree orchard (future project)

**Goals**:
