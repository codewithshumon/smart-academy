## Parent Involvement

### Supporting Career Exploration

**Encourage**:
- Discussion of different careers
- Exposure to various professions
- Support for interests
- Openness to possibilities
- Balance of guidance and autonomy

**Avoid**:
- Pressuring toward specific careers
- Imposing parent preferences
- Dismissing student interests
- Focus only on "prestigious" careers
- Unrealistic expectations

### Parent-Child Conversations

**Discuss**:
- Student's interests and strengths
- Family values and priorities
- Financial realities
- Career possibilities
- Educational planning
