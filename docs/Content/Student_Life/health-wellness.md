## Health Policies and Procedures

### Illness Policy

**When to Keep Student Home**:
- Fever (100°F/37.8°C or higher) - must stay home 24 hours fever-free
- Vomiting or diarrhea - stay home 24 hours after last episode
- Contagious illness (chickenpox, flu, etc.)
- Unexplained rash (until evaluated by doctor)
- Communicable disease

**Returning to School**:
- Fever-free for 24 hours without medication
- No vomiting or diarrhea for 24 hours
- Contagious period over
- Doctor's note may be required
- Feeling well enough to participate

### Medication Policy

**Prescription Medication**:
- Written parent authorization required
- Original container with pharmacy label
- Doctor's instructions
- Stored and administered by nurse
- Parent permission for self-carry when appropriate

**Over-the-Counter Medication**:
- Parent authorization required
- Original container
- Written instructions
- Administered by nurse
- Stored appropriately

**Emergency Medication**:
- EpiPens, inhalers, diabetes supplies
- Individual health care plans
- Staff training on administration
- Emergency protocols in place

### Injury Response

**School Response**:
- Immediate first aid by trained staff
- School nurse assessment
- Parent notification
- Emergency services activation if needed
- Accident report filed

**Minor Injuries**:
- First aid provided
- Parent notified
- Student may remain in class if appropriate
- Incident report filed

**Serious Injuries**:
- Emergency response activated
- Parents notified immediately
- Emergency services called
- Student transported to hospital if needed
- Incident report filed
