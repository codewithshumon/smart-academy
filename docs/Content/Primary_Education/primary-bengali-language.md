# Primary Bengali Language Curriculum

**Smart Academy | Honoring Our Mother Tongue and National Heritage**

---

## Bengali Language Philosophy

> **"The martyrs of the Language Movement taught us that our mother tongue is worth any sacrifice. We honor their legacy by mastering Bengali while maintaining our Islamic identity."**

Bengali is our mother tongue, the language of our nation, and a crucial part of our cultural identity. At Smart Academy, Bengali instruction develops fluency in reading, writing, speaking, and listening while instilling pride in Bengali language and culture within an Islamic framework. Students learn to express themselves eloquently in Bengali, appreciate Bengali literature, and understand Bangladesh's rich cultural heritage.

**Core Principles:**
1. **Mother Tongue Proficiency** - Fluency in Bengali language
2. **Cultural Pride** - Appreciation of Bengali heritage
3. **Literature Appreciation** - Engaging with Bengali literature
4. **Islamic Integration** - Bengali language within Islamic identity
5. **National Identity** - Understanding Bangladesh through language
6. **Language Movement Legacy** - Honoring shaheed (martyrs) of February 21, 1952
