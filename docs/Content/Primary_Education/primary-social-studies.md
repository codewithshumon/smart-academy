# Primary Social Studies Curriculum

**Smart Academy | Understanding Our World Through Islamic Values**

---

## Social Studies Philosophy

> **"O mankind! We created you from a single pair of a male and a female, and made you into nations and tribes, so that you may know one another." (Quran 49:13)**

Social Studies at Smart Academy develops informed, responsible, and active citizens who understand their place in Bangladesh, the Muslim Ummah, and the global community. Through the study of history, geography, civics, economics, and culture, students gain knowledge, skills, and values necessary to participate effectively in society while maintaining Islamic principles.

**Core Principles:**
1. **Integrated Disciplines** - History, geography, civics, economics, culture
2. **Islamic Perspective** - Understanding society through Islamic values
3. **Critical Thinking** - Analyzing events, trends, and issues
4. **Global and Local** - Bangladesh, Muslim world, and global awareness
5. **Active Citizenship** - Rights, responsibilities, and participation
6. **Cultural Appreciation** - Respecting diversity within Islamic framework

---

## Social Studies Domains

Our curriculum encompasses:
1. **History** - Past events, changes, continuity
2. **Geography** - Places, regions, human-environment interaction
3. **Civics and Government** - Citizenship, governance, rights and responsibilities
4. **Economics** - Production, consumption, trade, Islamic economics
5. **Culture** - Traditions, arts, beliefs, practices
6. **Islamic Civilization** - Muslim history, contributions, contemporary issues
