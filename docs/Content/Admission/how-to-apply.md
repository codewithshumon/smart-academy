## Before You Apply

### Prepare in Advance

**Research Smart Academy:**
- Review website thoroughly: www.mysmart.academy
- Read about our programs and approach
- Understand our Islamic identity and values
- Attend an information session if possible
- Schedule a campus visit

**Determine Eligibility:**
- Check age requirements for desired grade
- Review academic requirements
- Understand our expectations
- Consider if Smart Academy aligns with your family values

**Gather Information:**
- Student's personal information
- Previous school records (if applicable)
- Parents' National ID information
- Contact information
- Emergency contact details

**Timeline Planning:**
- Application period: November - January
- Apply early for best consideration
- Allow time to gather documents
- Plan for assessment in February

## Application Methods

### Choose Your Application Method

Smart Academy offers **three ways** to apply:

### 1. Online Application (Recommended)

**Benefits:**
- Fastest processing
- Immediate acknowledgment
- Can upload documents
- Track status online
- No courier/postal delays

**Steps:**
1. Visit apply.smartacademybd.com
2. Create account with email and password
3. Fill in application form online
4. Upload required documents
5. Pay application fee online
6. Submit application
7. Receive immediate acknowledgment

**Requirements:**
- Internet access
- Scanned documents (PDF/JPG format)
- Digital payment capability (bKash, Nagad, or bank)
- Valid email address
- Mobile phone number

### 2. Paper Application

**Benefits:**
- Familiar format
- Can complete at own pace
- No technology required
- Pay in person

**Steps:**
1. Collect application form from admission office OR download from website
2. Complete form in print (black or blue ink)
3. Attach required documents
4. Bring to admission office in person
5. Pay application fee at office
6. Receive acknowledgment receipt

**Requirements:**
- Visit to admission office
- Printed application form
- Photocopies of all documents
- Cash, bank draft, or mobile payment

### 3. Mail/Courier Application

**Benefits:**
- Can apply from distance
- Complete at convenience
- Documents sent securely

**Steps:**
1. Download application form from website OR request by email
2. Print and complete application
3. Gather all required documents
4. Make bank draft for application fee (1,500 BDT)
5. Send by courier/post to admission office
6. Receive acknowledgment by email/SMS when received

**Requirements:**
- Reliable courier service
- Bank draft for payment
- All documents photocopied
- Tracking shipment recommended

## Completing the Application

### Section-by-Section Guide

### Section 1: Student Information

**Required Information:**
- Full name (as per birth certificate)
- Date of birth
- Place of birth
- Gender
- Nationality
- Religion
- Blood group (if known)
- Applying for grade

**Tips:**
- Spell name correctly as it should appear on records
- Ensure date of birth matches birth certificate
- Use complete legal name (no nicknames)

### Section 2: Contact Information

**Required Information:**
- Present address
- Permanent address (if different)
- Phone numbers (mobile and landline if available)
- Email address
- Emergency contact information

**Tips:**
- Provide working phone number where you can be reached
- Include WhatsApp number if applicable
- Email address checked regularly

### Section 3: Parents/Guardians Information

**Father's Information:**
- Full name
- National ID number
- Occupation
- Workplace/Business
- Office phone
- Mobile phone
- Email address

**Mother's Information:**
- Full name
- National ID number
- Occupation
- Workplace/Business
- Office phone
- Mobile phone
- Email address

**Guardian Information (if applicable):**
- If parents are not available, guardian information required
- Relationship to student
- Legal guardianship documentation

### Section 4: Previous Education (Class 1 and above)

**Required:**
- Previous school name and address
- Previous grade/class completed
- Year completed
- Medium of instruction (English/Bengali/Other)
- Contact information of previous school

**Tips:**
- Provide accurate school information
- Include transfer certificate if available
- Previous school may be contacted for reference

### Section 5: Academic Information

**Information Needed:**
- Subjects studied in previous grade
- Proficiency in English (Basic/Intermediate/Advanced)
- Proficiency in Arabic (None/Basic/Intermediate/Advanced)
- Quran memorization (if any)
- Any special needs or learning difficulties

### Section 6: Health Information

**Required:**
- General health (Good/Fair/Poor)
- Any chronic health conditions
- Allergies
- Dietary restrictions
- Physical disabilities
- Medications regularly taken

**Important:**
- Be honest and complete
- Medical information kept confidential
- Helps us provide appropriate support

### Section 7: Family Information

**Required:**
- Number of siblings
- Siblings attending Smart Academy (if any)
- Languages spoken at home
- Parent's educational background
- Annual household income range (for scholarship consideration)

### Section 8: Declaration

**Required:**
- Parent/guardian signature
- Date of signing
- Agreement that information provided is accurate
- Understanding of terms and conditions

## Gathering Documents

### Document Checklist

**[ ] Application Form**
- Completed in full
- Signed by parent/guardian
- All questions answered

**[ ] Student Identification**
- Birth certificate (original + 2 photocopies) OR
- Passport (original + 2 photocopies)
- For PlayGroup: Birth certificate mandatory

**[ ] Parents' Identification**
- Father's National ID (2 copies)
- Mother's National ID (2 copies)
- Passport copies for international parents

**[ ] Photographs**
- Student: 4 passport-size color photos
- Father: 2 passport-size color photos
- Mother: 2 passport-size color photos
- White background
- Taken within last 3 months

**[ ] Previous School Records (Class 1+)**
- Transfer certificate from previous school
- Last report card/transcript
- Character/conduct certificate
- Attendance record
- School contact information

**[ ] Health Documents**
- Immunization record (if available)
- Health information form completed
- Medical documentation if applicable

**[ ] Application Fee Payment**
- Payment receipt or proof of payment
- 1,500 BDT (non-refundable)
- Late application: 2,000 BDT (after January 31)

### Document Preparation Tips

**Photocopies:**
- Use good quality photocopies
- Ensure all text is readable
- Make extra copies for your records

**Photographs:**
- Use professional studio photos if possible
- Ensure clear, well-lit images
- Print on photo paper
- Cut to passport size

**Organization:**
- Organize documents in order
- Use folder or envelope to keep together
- Label with student's name
- Keep copies for your records

## Submitting Your Application

### Online Submission

**Step 1: Create Account**
- Go to apply.smartacademybd.com
- Click "Create Account"
- Enter email and create password
- Verify email address

**Step 2: Complete Application**
- Log in to your account
- Fill in all required fields
- Save progress as you go
- Review before final submission

**Step 3: Upload Documents**
- Scan or photograph documents
- Upload in correct format (PDF preferred)
- Ensure files are clear and readable
- Maximum file size: 5MB per file

**Step 4: Pay Application Fee**
- Choose payment method (bKash/Nagad/Bank)
- Complete payment online
- Upload payment receipt
- Or enter transaction reference number

**Step 5: Submit**
- Review all information
- Confirm all documents uploaded
- Submit application
- Receive acknowledgment with application number

### In-Person Submission

**Step 1: Visit Admission Office**
- Smart Academy, Narimpur, Ramganj
- Sunday-Thursday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Saturday, 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

**Step 2: Bring Application**
- Completed application form
- All required documents
- Photographs
- Payment (cash/draft/mobile transfer)

**Step 3: Submit to Staff**
- Staff will review application
- Verify all documents present
- Accept application fee payment
- Provide acknowledgment receipt

**Step 4: Receive Confirmation**
- Application number assigned
- Assessment information provided if applicable
- Next steps explained

### Mail/Courier Submission

**Step 1: Prepare Package**
- Completed application form
- All required documents
- Photographs
- Bank draft for application fee

**Step 2: Address Package**

**To:**
Admission Office
Smart Academy
Narimpur, Ramganj
Laxmipur District
Chittagong Division
Bangladesh

**Step 3: Send via Reliable Courier**
- Use registered mail or courier service
- Request tracking number
- Note tracking for your records

**Step 4: Await Confirmation**
- Acknowledgment sent when application received
- Usually within 5-7 working days of receipt
- Contact if no acknowledgment within 10 days

## After Submission

### What Happens Next

**1. Application Review (3-5 working days)**
- Application checked for completeness
- Documents verified
- Contacted if anything missing

**2. Assessment Scheduling**
- If complete, assessment scheduled
- Notification sent with date, time, and details
- Usually 1-2 weeks in advance

**3. Assessment and Interview**
- Attend on scheduled date
- Student assessment
- Parent interview
- Bring application acknowledgment

**4. Decision Notification**
- Decision sent by March 15, 2026
- Email, SMS, and formal letter
- Next steps provided

**5. Enrollment (if admitted)**
- Accept offer by deadline
- Pay required fees
- Submit enrollment documents
- Attend orientation program

### Tracking Your Application

**Online Tracking:**
- Log into your account at apply.smartacademybd.com
- Check current status
- View updates and messages
- Upload additional documents if requested

**Phone Inquiry:**
- Call admission office: +8801709-651168 (Extension 201)
- Provide application number
- Ask about status
- Allow reasonable time for processing before checking

### If Contacted for More Information

**Common Requests:**
- Missing documents
- Clarification needed
- Additional information
- Verification of information

**Response Required:**
- Respond promptly (within 3-5 working days)
- Provide requested information
- Contact if you need clarification
- Keep application number in all communications

## Application Tips

### Before Submitting

**1. Review Thoroughly**
- Check all information is accurate
- Ensure all questions answered
- Verify contact information correct
- Confirm all documents included

**2. Proofread**
- Check for spelling errors
- Verify names spelled correctly
- Ensure dates accurate
- Review phone numbers and emails

**3. Be Honest**
- Provide accurate information
- Don't exaggerate achievements
- Disclose any special needs
- Explain any gaps in education

**4. Be Complete**
- Answer all questions
- Don't leave sections blank
- Provide all requested documents
- Include all required information

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

**❌ Incomplete Information**
- Leaves processing delayed
- Requires follow-up contact
- May miss assessment deadline

**❌ Incorrect Contact Information**
- We cannot reach you
- Important communications missed
- Cannot send decision notification

**❌ Missing Documents**
- Application cannot be processed
- Causes delays
- May affect assessment scheduling

**❌ Inaccurate Information**
- Creates problems later
- May affect enrollment
- Could lead to admission rejection

**❌ Late Application**
- Misses priority consideration
- Late fee applies
- Limited seats available

### When to Apply

**Apply Early:**
- November-December ideal
- More time for processing
- Better assessment time slot options
- No late fee

**Apply By Deadline:**
- January 31, 2026 regular deadline
- Still good chance for consideration
- No late fee

**Late Application:**
- After January 31 but before February 28
- Late fee of 500 BDT applies
- Processed if time permits
- No guarantee of consideration

## Special Circumstances

### Scholarship Applications

**Additional Steps:**
1. Complete regular admission application
2. Complete separate scholarship application
3. Submit financial documentation
4. Provide evidence of circumstances
5. Submit by January 31 deadline

**Documentation Required:**
- Income verification (salary certificate, tax returns)
- Financial statements
- Evidence of special circumstances
- Any supporting documentation

### Special Needs Applications

**Additional Steps:**
1. Complete regular admission application
2. Submit medical/psychological assessments
3. Provide previous IEP if applicable
4. Meet with special education coordinator
5. Discuss support needs openly

**Important:**
- Honest discussion about child's needs
- Assessment of our ability to provide support
- Recommendation if another setting more appropriate

### International Applications

**Additional Documentation:**
- Valid passport copies
- Visa documentation
- Previous school transcripts (translated if needed)
- Transfer certificate (authenticated)
- Health insurance information

**Process:**
- Same application process
- Additional documentation required
- May require distance assessment arrangements
- Contact admission office for specific requirements
