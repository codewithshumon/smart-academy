# Sibling Discounts

## Family Discount Program

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026
**Target Audience:** Current and prospective parents
**Purpose:** Information about sibling discount benefits

---

## Overview

Smart Academy values families who choose to educate multiple children with us. Our **sibling discount program** makes quality education more affordable for families.

### Discount Structure

| Sibling Number | Discount | Applied To |
|----------------|----------|------------|
| First child | Full price | - |
| Second child | 10% off | Tuition only |
| Third child | 15% off | Tuition only |
| Fourth+ child | 20% off | Tuition only |

---

## Discount Details

### What's Discounted

**Tuition Fees:**
- All grade levels eligible
- Discount applies to tuition portion only
- Calculated from lowest grade to highest

**Example Family with 3 Children:**
- Child 1 (Class 8): Full tuition = 45,000 BDT
- Child 2 (Class 5): 10% discount = 32,000 × 0.90 = 28,800 BDT
- Child 3 (Class 2): 15% discount = 32,000 × 0.85 = 27,200 BDT
- **Total:** 100,800 BDT (vs. 109,000 full price = 8,200 savings)

### What's NOT Discounted

**Not Eligible for Discount:**
- Admission fee (one-time per student)
- Security deposit
- Additional fees (library, lab, computer, sports, activity)
- Optional fees (transport, lunch, after-school care)

---

## Eligibility

### Automatic Enrollment

**No Application Required:**
- Automatically applied when siblings enroll
- Verified by school records
- Applied to oldest child first

**Biological, Adopted, Step-Siblings:**
- All eligible if enrolled at Smart Academy
- Verification may be required for step-siblings
- Legal documentation for adopted siblings

### Guardian Siblings

**Legally Guarded Siblings:**
- Under same legal guardian
- Court documentation required
- Proof of guardianship
- Approved by administration

## Examples

### Two Siblings

**Children in Class 5 and Class 2:**

**Without Discount:**
- Class 5: 38,000 BDT
- Class 2: 32,000 BDT
- Total: 70,000 BDT

**With Sibling Discount:**
- Class 5 (older): 38,000 BDT (full price)
- Class 2 (younger): 32,000 × 0.90 = 28,800 BDT
- **Total: 66,800 BDT**
- **Savings: 3,200 BDT**

**With Sibling Discount:**

- Class 1 (youngest): 32,000 × 0.85 = 27,200 BDT
- **Total: 106,400 BDT**
- **Savings: 8,600 BDT**

## Combining Discounts

### Sibling Discount + Scholarship

**Policy:** Sibling discount OR scholarship, whichever is greater

**Example:**
- Second sibling eligible for 10% sibling discount
- Also qualifies for 30% need-based scholarship
- Receives 30% scholarship (greater amount)
- Does not combine both discounts

**Exception:**
- Merit scholarships CAN combine with sibling discount
- Both discounts applied to tuition

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to apply for sibling discount?**
A: No, it's automatically applied when both siblings are enrolled.

**Q: Does it apply to twins?**
A: Yes, twins are considered siblings and the discount applies.

**Q: What if one sibling graduates?**
A: The discount recalculates for remaining siblings. Oldest remaining child pays full price.

**Q: Does it apply to half-siblings?**
A: Yes, if they live in the same household and share at least one parent.

**Q: Can I get sibling discount if one child leaves?**
A: Discount recalculates based on remaining enrolled siblings.

**Q: Does it apply to cousins?**
A: No, only siblings (or legally adopted/guarded siblings).

**Q: What if my child joins mid-year?**
A: Discount applied from date of enrollment, prorated accordingly.
