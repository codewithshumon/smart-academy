## Overview

Admission to Smart Academy requires submission of specific documents and meeting of certain criteria. This document outlines all requirements clearly so families can prepare complete applications.

**Key Points:**
- Requirements vary by grade level
- Some documents are mandatory for all applicants
- Additional requirements may apply to special cases
- Incomplete applications cannot be processed

---

## General Requirements

### Requirements for ALL Applicants

**Every application must include:**

1. **Completed Application Form**
   - Online application submitted
   - Or paper form filled completely
   - All sections answered
   - Signature(s) of parent/guardian

2. **Application Fee**
   - Payment of 1,500 BDT (non-refundable)
   - Paid via bank draft, cash, or online payment
   - Receipt attached to application

3. **Photographs**
   - 4 copies recent passport-size photographs of student
   - 2 copies each of parents/guardians
   - Color photos with white background
   - Taken within last 3 months

4. **Proof of Identity**
   - Birth certificate OR passport copy of student
   - National ID cards of both parents (Bangladesh)
   - For international applicants: Valid passports

### Age Policy

**Standard Entry:**
- Students should be the age specified for the grade by January 1st of admission year

**Note:** Age exceptions are granted on a case-by-case basis and are not common.

## Grade-Specific Requirements

### Early Childhood (PlayGroup - KG)

**PlayGroup (Age 3+)**

**Required:**
- Completed application form
- Birth certificate (original + 2 copies)
- Photographs (4 student, 2 each parent)
- Parents' NID
- Application fee payment

**Additional:**
- Immunization record (if available)
- Emergency contact information
- Health information form

**Assessment:**
- No formal testing
- Play-based observation
- Parent interview required
- Developmental readiness check

**Nursery (Age 4+)**

**Required:**
- All PlayGroup requirements
- Previous preschool records (if attended)

**Assessment:**
- Basic readiness assessment
- Language development check
- Social development observation
- Parent interview

**Kindergarten (Age 5+)**

**Required:**
- All Nursery requirements
- Previous school records/transcripts
- Previous school contact information

**Assessment:**
- Basic academic readiness
- Letter and number recognition
- Language comprehension
- Social skills evaluation

### Primary Education (Class 1-5)

**Class 1**

**Required:**
- All KG requirements
- KG completion certificate
- KG final report card/transcripts
- Transfer certificate from previous school
- Previous school contact information

**Assessment:**
- English: Basic reading and writing
- Mathematics: Number concepts (1-20)
- Arabic: Basic Arabic alphabet
- Islamic Studies: Basic Islamic knowledge
- Interview with student and parents

**Class 2-5**

**Required:**
- Completed application form
- Birth certificate or passport
- Recent academic transcripts (last 2 years)
- Transfer certificate from previous school
- Character/conduct certificate from previous school
- Attendance record from previous school
- Parents' NID
- Photographs
- Application fee

**Assessment:**
- **Class 2-3:**
  - English (reading comprehension, writing)
  - Mathematics (grade-level concepts)
  - Arabic (reading and writing)
  - Islamic Studies basic knowledge

- **Class 4-5:**
  - English (reading, writing, grammar)
  - Mathematics (problem-solving, concepts)
  - Science (basic concepts)
  - Arabic (reading, writing, understanding)
  - Islamic Studies (Quran, Hadith, Fiqh basics)

### Secondary Education (Class 6-9)

**Class 6-9**

**Required:**
- All Primary requirements
- Detailed academic transcripts (last 3 years)
- Transfer certificate
- Character/conduct certificate
- Attendance certificate
- Previous school curriculum information
- Recommendation letter from previous teacher (optional but recommended)

**Assessment:**
- **English:** Reading comprehension, writing, grammar, vocabulary
- **Mathematics:** Grade-level concepts and problem-solving
- **Science:** Basic scientific concepts and reasoning
- **Arabic:** Reading, writing, grammar, comprehension
- **Islamic Studies:** Quran recitation, Islamic knowledge
- **General Knowledge:** Basic awareness and reasoning

**Additional Consideration:**
- Previous academic performance
- Conduct and character
- Extracurricular involvement
- Leadership experience

## Academic Requirements

### Minimum Academic Standards

**Previous Performance:**
- Minimum 60% average in previous grade
- No failing grades in core subjects
- Good conduct record
- Regular attendance (minimum 85% in previous year)

**English Language Proficiency:**
- Basic English comprehension required
- Ability to communicate in English
- Willingness to improve language skills
- Support provided for developing proficiency

**Islamic Education:**
- Previous Islamic education beneficial but not mandatory
- Willingness to participate in Islamic programs
- Respect for Islamic practices and values

### Special Considerations

**Acemic Probation:**
- Students slightly below standards may be considered
- Placed on academic monitoring
- Support provided
- Review after first term

**Learning Support:**
- Students with learning difficulties considered individually
- Assessment of ability to provide appropriate support
- Honest discussion with parents about expectations

### Mandatory Health Information

**All Students Must:**
- Complete health information form
- Disclose any medical conditions
- Provide emergency contact information
- Update school of any health changes

**Recommended:**
- Up-to-date immunizations
- Annual health check-up

**Chronic Health Conditions:**
- Detailed medical information required

- Medication administration plan

- Facility assessment

**Mental Health Concerns:**

- Support services available

## Special Categories

### Sibling Admission

**Priority Consideration:**
- Siblings of current students receive priority
- Application still must meet requirements
- Assessment still required
- Not automatic admission

**Required:**
- Indicate sibling relationship on application
- Provide sibling's name and current grade
- Early application still recommended

### Alumni Children

**Priority Consideration:**
- Children of alumni may receive priority
- Must still meet all admission requirements
- Alumni status noted in application

**Required:**
- Parent's graduation year
- Parent's name while at Smart Academy
- Verification of alumni status

### International Students

**Additional Requirements:**
- Valid student visa (if applicable)
- Passport copies
- Previous school transcripts (with English translation)
- Health insurance documentation
- Local guardian information (if applicable)

**Support Provided:**
- Orientation program
- English language support
- Cultural adjustment assistance
- International student coordinator

### Special Needs Students

**Individualized Review:**
- Meeting with special education coordinator
- Review of existing assessments
- Discussion of support needs
- Facility accommodation assessment

**Honest Approach:**
- Transparent discussion about our capabilities
- Referral to more appropriate settings if needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### About Requirements

**Q: What if I cannot provide all required documents?**
A: Contact the admission office immediately. Some documents may be submitted later with valid reason. However, missing documents delay processing.

**Q: Do you accept students without previous formal schooling?**
A: Yes, for PlayGroup and Nursery. For KG and above, previous school records are required.

**Q: My child's previous school doesn't issue transfer certificates. What should I do?**
A: Provide a letter from the previous school confirming enrollment and last date attended. We'll work with you on alternatives.

**Q: Can my child apply if they don't speak English well?**
A: Yes, we provide English language support. However, basic comprehension is needed. We assess each child individually.

**Q: Are immunizations mandatory?**
A: We recommend up-to-date immunizations but don't require them for admission. Health information is required for emergency planning.

**Q: What if my child has a learning disability?**
A: We provide support for diverse learning needs. Contact us to discuss your child's specific situation so we can determine appropriate placement.

**Q: Do you require children to be Muslim?**
A: Smart Academy is an Islamic institution. While we welcome all students, our curriculum and practices are Islamic. We give preference to Muslim applicants but consider all who respect Islamic values.

**Q: Can documents be submitted in Bengali?**
A: Yes, Bengali documents are accepted. Translations may be requested for verification purposes.

**Q: How recent should photographs be?**
A: Photographs should be taken within the last 3 months and clearly show current appearance.

**Q: Do you accept applications after the deadline?**
A: Late applications may be considered if space is available, with a late application fee. Early application is strongly recommended.
