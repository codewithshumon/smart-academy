## Enrollment Process

### After Acceptance

1. Accept offer by deadline
2. Pay admission and security fees
3. Submit enrollment documents
4. Complete medical forms
5. Arrange transportation
6. Attend orientation

### Required Documents

- Enrollment form
- Medical information form
- Transportation request (if needed)
- Emergency contact form
- Parent agreement

### Timeline

- March-April: Enrollment
- April: Orientation
- August: Class placement
- January: Classes begin
