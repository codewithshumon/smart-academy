## Payment Schedule Overview

### Academic Year Timeline

**Academic Year:** January to December
**Payment Year:** January to December (3 installments)

Smart Academy follows a **three-installment payment system** to help families manage education costs throughout the year.

### Payment Philosophy

**Flexible but Structured:**
- Payments spread across academic year
- Clear deadlines for each installment
- Options for different family needs
- Reminders sent before due dates
- Consequences for late payment

## Annual Payment Options

### Option 1: Full Annual Payment (Recommended)

**Payment Deadline:** January 31

**Benefits:**
- **5% discount** on tuition fees
- No worry about payment deadlines
- One payment, done for the year
- Simplified record-keeping
- Avoids late payment issues

**Example:**
- Class 5 Annual Tuition: 38,000 BDT
- With 5% Early Payment Discount: 36,100 BDT
- **Savings: 1,900 BDT**

**Who This Suits:**
- Families with funds available
- Want simplicity and peace of mind
- Prefer to get payments done early
- Want maximum discount

**How to Pay:**
- Single payment of full annual tuition + additional fees
- Pay by January 31 to receive discount
- Use any payment method (see Payment Methods)

### Option 2: Three Installments

**Standard payment option for most families**

**Installment Breakdown:**

| Installment | Due Date | Percentage | Approximate Amount* |
|-------------|----------|------------|---------------------|
| First | January 31 | 40% | Tuition × 0.40 |
| Second | May 31 | 30% | Tuition × 0.30 |
| Third | September 30 | 30% | Tuition × 0.30 |

*Amounts vary by grade level. See Fee Structure for exact amounts.

**Who This Suits:**
- Families who prefer spreading payments
- Those with regular income
- Want to manage cash flow
- Standard payment plan

**No Additional Fees:**
- No interest charged
- No service fees
- Standard payment schedule

### Option 3: Custom Arrangements

**Special Circumstances:**

**Available For:**
- Families with genuine financial hardship
- Irregular income patterns (business owners, freelancers)
- Special situations requiring flexibility

**Requirements:**
- Written application to finance office
- Explanation of circumstances
- Supporting documentation
- Proposed payment schedule
- Meeting with finance officer

**Approval:**
- Case-by-case basis
- Not guaranteed
- Must be approved before January 31
- May require guarantor

## Late Payment Policy

### Grace Period

**5-Day Grace Period:**
- Payments accepted until 5 days after deadline without penalty
- After 5 days, late fee applies
- Grace period applies to each installment

**Example:**
- Deadline: January 31
- Grace period ends: February 5
- Late fee applies: February 6 onward

### Late Fees

**Monthly Late Fee:**
- 500 BDT per month on overdue amount
- Calculated from deadline date
- Added to outstanding balance
- Must be paid with tuition

**Example:**
- Tuition due: 15,200 BDT (January 31)
- Paid March 1: 1 month late
- Late fee: 500 BDT
- **Total due: 15,700 BDT**

### Consequences of Chronic Late Payment

**First Instance:**
- Warning letter sent
- Late fee charged
- Reminder of payment schedule

**Second Instance:**
- Meeting requested with finance office
- Payment plan required
- Possible loss of installment privilege

**Third Instance:**
- Report card withheld
- Student may be suspended from classes
- Examination participation withheld
- Possible withdrawal

### Special Circumstances

**Genuine Hardship:**
- Contact finance office BEFORE deadline
- Explain circumstances
- Provide documentation
- Request extension or modified schedule
- Extensions considered case-by-case

**Valid Reasons May Include:**
- Medical emergency
- Job loss
- Natural disaster
- Family crisis
- Other genuine hardship

**Documentation Required:**
- Medical certificate
- Employment termination letter
- Other relevant documentation

## Frequently Asked Questions

### About Payment Schedule

**Q: Can I pay monthly instead of in installments?**
A: No, we only have 3 installments per year (January, May, September). Monthly payments not available except by special arrangement.

**Q: What if I miss the January 31 deadline?**
A: You have a 5-day grace period until February 5. After that, a 500 BDT late fee applies per month.

**Q: Can I change from installment to annual payment?**
A: Yes, you can pay the full year anytime before January 31 and receive the 5% early payment discount.

**Q: What happens if I can't pay on time due to financial problems?**
A: Contact the finance office BEFORE the deadline. Don't wait until it's overdue. We may be able to work out a modified payment plan.

**Q: Do you send payment reminders?**
A: Yes, we send 3 reminders before each deadline (30 days, 15 days, and 5 days before) via email and SMS.

**Q: Can I pay early?**
A: Yes, early payments are welcome and appreciated. No discount for early installment payment, but full annual payment by January 31 receives 5% discount.

**Q: What if I forget to pay?**
A: Late fees will apply. We send multiple reminders, but it's your responsibility to pay on time. Set up your own reminders if needed.

**Q: Can I pay multiple installments at once?**
A: Yes, you can pay future installments early if convenient. No extra discount for early installment payment.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Cash, bank draft, mobile banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket), online transfer, and credit/debit cards at office. See Payment Methods for details.

**Q: How do I get a payment receipt?**
A: Receipts are issued immediately for cash payments and within 24 hours for other methods. Online payments receive email receipts.

**Q: Can someone else pay on my behalf?**
A: Yes, ensure they include your child's name and ID number so payment is properly credited.

**Q: Do I get a refund if I overpay?**
A: Yes, credit is maintained for future payments or refund available upon request.

**Q: What if my child withdraws mid-year?**
A: Tuition is prorated based on withdrawal date. See Refund Policy for details.
