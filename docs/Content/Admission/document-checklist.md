## Document Overview

### Why Documents Matter

Complete and accurate documentation ensures:
- Smooth processing of application
- Proper student records from the start
- Compliance with educational regulations
- Emergency preparedness
- Accurate placement and support

### General Guidelines

**✓ All documents must be:**
- Clear and readable copies
- Current and valid
- Complete (no missing pages)
- Accurate and truthful
- Submitted by deadline

**✗ Do NOT submit:**
- Falsified documents
- Incomplete information
- Expired documents
- Poor quality copies
- Documents with alterations

## Mandatory Documents

### Required for ALL Applicants

#### 1. Application Form

**[ ] Completed Application Form**

**Requirements:**
- All sections filled completely
- Signed by parent/guardian
- Date of signing included
- No blank required fields
- Information accurate and truthful

**Where to Get:**
- Online at apply.smartacademybd.com
- Admission office
- Download from website

---

#### 2. Student Identification

**[ ] Birth Certificate OR Passport**

**Birth Certificate:**
- Original shown for verification
- 2 photocopies submitted
- Issued by government authority
- Name and date of birth clear
- No alterations

**OR Passport:**
- Original shown for verification
- 2 photocopies submitted
- Valid passport
- Clear photograph page
- Birth date visible

**Note:** Birth certificate preferred for PlayGroup-KG. Passport acceptable for all grades.

---

#### 3. Student Photographs

**[ ] Student Photographs (4 copies)**

**Specifications:**
- Passport size (approx. 4cm x 5cm)
- Color photographs
- White background
- Recent (within last 3 months)
- Clear, well-lit
- Front view, face clearly visible
- No hats, sunglasses (religious head covering acceptable)

**Quality:**
- Professional photos recommended
- Good print quality
- Cut to proper size
- Name written on back of each

- Clear and readable
- Current and valid
- Photo clearly visible

#### 5. Parents' Photographs

## Document Preparation

### Photocopy Guidelines

**Quality Matters:**
- Use good quality photocopier
- Ensure all text is readable
- Copy entire document (no cut-offs)
- Use clean copier glass
- Check copies before submitting

**Number of Copies:**
- Birth certificate: 2 copies
- NID cards: 2 copies each
- Photos: As specified above
- Other documents: 2 copies unless specified

### Photography Guidelines

**Student Photos:**
- Professional photographer recommended
- Studio photos preferred
- White background
- Passport size
- Recent (within 3 months)
- Face clearly visible
- No sunglasses or hats
- Religious head covering acceptable

**Parent Photos:**
- Passport size
- Color or black and white
- Recent (within 6 months)
- Clear and well-lit

### Document Organization

**Organize by Category:**
1. Application form (on top)
2. Student identification
3. Student photographs
4. Parents' identification
5. Parents' photographs
6. Payment proof
7. Academic records
8. Special category documents (if applicable)

**Use Folder or Envelope:**
- Large envelope to hold all documents
- Label with student's name
- Keep organized and protected
- Have extra copies for your records

### Keeping Records

**Keep Copies of Everything:**
- Make copies of all submitted documents
- Keep in safe place at home
- May need for reference later
- Useful for future applications

**Scanning Guidelines:**
- Scan in color if original is color
- PDF format preferred

- Name files clearly (e.g., BirthCertificate.pdf)

**Upload Order:**
1. Application form (submitted online)
2. Birth certificate or passport
3. Parents' NID cards

## Frequently Asked Questions

### About Documents

**Q: What if I don't have all documents?**
A: Contact the admission office. Some documents may be submitted later with valid reason. Missing documents delay processing.

**Q: Can I submit photocopies instead of originals?**
A: Yes, photocopies are accepted. We may need to see originals for verification at enrollment.

**Q: Do documents need to be in English?**
A: Bengali documents are accepted. We may request translations for verification if needed.

**Q: How recent should photographs be?**
A: Student photos within last 3 months, parent photos within last 6 months.

**Q: What if my child doesn't have a birth certificate?**
A: Passport can be used. If neither available, contact admission office for alternatives.

**Q: Do I need to submit originals?**
A: No, only clear photocopies. We may request to see originals for verification.

**Q: Can I submit documents after the deadline?**
A: Late document submission may delay your application. Submit all documents with application if possible.

**Q: What if my previous school won't give a transfer certificate yet?**
A: Submit a letter from the school confirming enrollment and explain the situation. Transfer certificate can be submitted later.

**Q: Are my documents safe with Smart Academy?**
A: Yes, all documents are kept confidential and stored securely. They're used only for admission purposes.
