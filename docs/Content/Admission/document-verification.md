# Document Verification

## Credential Evaluation Process

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026

---

## Overview

Smart Academy verifies all submitted documents to ensure authenticity and proper placement.

## Verification Process

### Academic Documents
- Contact previous schools
- Verify transcripts
- Confirm attendance records
- Check character references

### International Documents
- Translation verification
- Authentication check
- Curriculum evaluation
- Grade conversion

### Personal Documents
- Birth certificate verification
- Passport validation
- NID confirmation

## Timeline

- Standard verification: 1-2 weeks
- International verification: 2-4 weeks
- May delay admission decision

## Requirements

**Parents Must:**
- Provide accurate information
- Authorize school to contact previous institutions
- Submit original documents for verification
- Report any errors immediately

**Falsification:**
- Grounds for immediate rejection
- May result in legal action
- Not eligible for reapplication

## Confidentiality

- All verification handled confidentially
- Information used only for admission purposes
- Staff trained in proper verification procedures
