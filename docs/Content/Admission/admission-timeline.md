## Overview

The Smart Academy admission cycle runs from **November to April** each year, with the academic year beginning in January. This timeline outlines all important dates for the **2026-2027 academic year admission cycle**.

### Key Points

- **Application Period:** November 2025 - February 2026
- **Assessments:** February - March 2026
- **Decisions:** Late March 2026
- **Enrollment:** April 2026
- **Academic Year Begins:** January 2027 (orientation in April 2026)

## Important Deadlines

### Critical Dates

**Must Remember:**

| Date | Event | Importance |
|------|-------|------------|
| January 31, 2026 | Application Deadline | CRITICAL - Late fee after |
| February 5-28, 2026 | Assessment Period | Must attend assigned date |
| March 15, 2026 | Decision Notification | Offers sent |
| March 31, 2026 | Acceptance Deadline | Respond by this date |
| April 17, 2026 | Orientation Program | Attend if possible |
| April 30, 2026 | Final Enrollment | Complete enrollment |

### Deadline Policies

**Application Deadline:**
- January 31, 2026 at 4:00 PM
- Late applications accepted until February 28, 2026
- Late application fee: Additional 500 BDT
- Late applicants processed if time permits

**Acceptance Deadline:**
- Usually 2 weeks from offer date
- Extensions may be requested with valid reason
- Offer may be withdrawn if deadline missed without prior arrangement

**Enrollment Deadline:**
- April 30, 2026 for most enrollments
- After this date, enrollment may be forfeited
- Special circumstances considered individually

## Late Applications

### Policy

**Late Application Period:** February 1-28, 2026

**Conditions:**
- Late fee of 500 BDT applies
- Processed if time permits
- Assessment scheduled if possible
- No guarantee of consideration

**Late Decision:**
- If time permits, decision by April 15
- May be placed on waitlist
- Enrollment may be rushed

**Recommendation:**
- Apply before January 31 deadline
- Avoid late fees and complications
- Ensure full consideration
