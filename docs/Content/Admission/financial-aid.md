## Types of Financial Aid

### 1. Need-Based Grants

**Description:** Direct financial support based on family need

**Coverage:** 25%-100% of tuition fees

**Eligibility:**
- Demonstrated financial need
- Genuine hardship
- Commitment to education
- Regular attendance required

### 2. Emergency Aid

**Description:** Short-term assistance for families in crisis

**Coverage:** Temporary support (usually one term)

**For Families Facing:**
- Medical emergencies
- Job loss
- Natural disasters
- Family crises
- Unexpected hardships

### 3. Work-Study Programs

**Description:** Parents/guardians contribute skills/services in exchange for reduced fees

**Opportunities:**
- Assistance in school programs
- Sharing professional expertise
- Volunteering at events
- Community service projects
- Administrative support

**Value:** Up to 20% tuition reduction per year

### 4. Fee Deferrals

**Description:** Extended payment timeline for families with temporary cash flow issues

**Terms:**
- Maximum 2-month deferral
- Valid reason required
- Written agreement
- Small administrative fee (2%)

## Applying for Aid

### Application Process

**Step 1: Obtain Application**
- Available at admission/finance office
- Download from website
- Request by email

**Step 2: Complete Application**
- Provide all required information
- Attach documentation
- Be honest and thorough
- Sign declaration

**Step 3: Submit Application**
- Submit by deadline (January 31 for new students)
- Include all documents
- Keep copy for records

**Step 4: Meeting**
- May be scheduled with finance committee
- Discuss situation confidentially
- Clarify information
- Answer questions

**Step 5: Decision**
- Notification with admission decision
- Award amount specified
- Terms explained
- Acceptance required

### Required Documentation

**Financial Documentation:**
- Income verification (salary certificate, tax returns)
- Bank statements (3-6 months)
- List of assets and liabilities
- Documentation of special circumstances

**Family Information:**
- Number of dependents
- Other children in school
- Monthly expenses
- Special needs or expenses

**Supporting Letters:**
- Letter explaining circumstances
- Any relevant documentation
- Reference letter (if applicable)

## Aid Determination

### How Decisions Are Made

**Factors Considered:**
1. **Family Income:** Total income from all sources
2. **Family Size:** Number of dependents
3. **Assets:** Property, vehicles, savings
4. **Expenses:** Living costs, debts, medical expenses
5. **Special Circumstances:** Crises, emergencies
6. **Commitment:** Family's commitment to education

**Review Process:**
- Finance committee reviews application
- Cross-checks documentation
- Verifies information if needed
- Makes recommendation
- Principal approves final awards

### Determination Formula

**Need Assessment:**
- Basic living expenses calculated
- Education capacity assessed
- Gap determines award amount
- Standard formula applied consistently

**Example:**
- Family income: 200,000 BDT/year
- Basic living expenses: 150,000 BDT/year
- Education capacity: 50,000 BDT/year
- Tuition fees: 38,000 BDT/year
- Gap: Need assistance
- Award: 50% of tuition (19,000 BDT)

**Note:** This is simplified. Actual calculations consider many factors.

## While Receiving Aid

### Responsibilities of Recipients

**Academic Responsibilities:**
- Maintain satisfactory academic progress
- Attend classes regularly (90%+ attendance)
- Good effort and behavior
- Complete homework and assignments

**Behavioral Expectations:**
- Excellent conduct
- Respect for teachers and peers
- No disciplinary issues
- Islamic character demonstrated

**Parent Responsibilities:**
- Support child's education
- Attend parent-teacher meetings
- Communicate with school
- Report changes in financial situation
- Fulfill any work-study commitments

### Renewal Requirements

**Annual Review:**
- All aid must be renewed annually
- Submit updated financial information
- Academic progress reviewed
- Conduct evaluated
- May be adjusted based on circumstances

**Changes in Circumstances:**
- Report improvement in financial situation
- May result in reduced aid
- Report worsening situation
- May result in increased aid

### Confidentiality

**Privacy Protected:**
- All financial information confidential
- Only necessary staff involved
- Other students/parents not informed
- Dignity maintained
