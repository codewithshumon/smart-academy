# Transfer Student Admission

## Information for Transfer Students

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026
**Target Audience:** Students transferring from other schools
**Purpose:** Guide to transfer admission process

---

## Overview

Smart Academy welcomes transfer students throughout the academic year when space is available.

## Eligibility

**Requirements:**
- Previous school records
- Transfer certificate
- Good conduct record
- Minimum 90% attendance
- Meeting grade-level requirements

## Process

### Application
- Submit application with transfer documents
- Provide previous school records
- Transfer certificate required
- Character/conduct certificate

### Assessment
- Placement testing to determine appropriate grade
- Subject-based evaluation
- Language proficiency assessment
- Interview with student and parents

### Decision
- Based on availability
- Assessment results
- Previous record
- Fit with Smart Academy program

## Required Documents

- [ ] Transfer certificate from previous school
- [ ] Academic transcripts (current year + last 2 years)
- [ ] Character/conduct certificate
- [ ] Attendance record
- [ ] Course descriptions (if from different curriculum)
- [ ] Immunization records (if international)

## Timeline

- Applications accepted year-round (if space available)
- Processing: 2-3 weeks
- Enrollment: Within 1 week of acceptance

## Support

- Orientation program for transfer students
- Buddy system
- Academic support if needed
- Regular check-ins with teacher
