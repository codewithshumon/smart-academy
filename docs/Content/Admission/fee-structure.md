## Fee Structure Overview

### Philosophy of Fees at Smart Academy

**Accessible Quality Education:**
Smart Academy is committed to providing quality Islamic English medium education that is **accessible to families across economic backgrounds**. Our fee structure reflects:

**✓ Value for Money:**
- Quality education comparable to urban schools
- Modern facilities and technology
- Qualified, experienced teachers
- Comprehensive Islamic programs
- Holistic development activities

**✓ Transparency:**
- Clear, published fee structure
- No hidden charges
- All fees explained upfront
- Regular communication about any changes

**✓ Fairness:**
- Sibling discounts
- Scholarship programs (25% of students)
- Flexible payment plans
- Financial aid available

**✓ Sustainability:**
- Fees support quality education
- Competitive with local private schools
- Reinvested in school improvement
- Enable teacher retention and development

## Admission Fees

### One-Time Fees (Payable Upon Admission)

**Admission Fee: 10,000 BDT**
- One-time, non-refundable fee
- Paid only upon first admission
- Covers administrative processing
- Includes enrollment costs
- Not charged for returning students

**Security Deposit: 5,000 BDT**
- Refundable deposit
- Held while student enrolled
- Returned upon graduation or withdrawal
- Deducted for any outstanding dues or damages
- No interest paid on deposit

**Total Initial Admission Cost: 15,000 BDT**
(Admission fee + Security deposit)

**Note:** These are one-time fees paid only when student first joins Smart Academy.

## Fee Policies

### Payment Policies

**Fee Payment Responsibility:**
- Parent/guardian named in enrollment agreement
- Fees non-transferable to other students
- Both parents jointly responsible unless otherwise specified

**Late Payment:**
- Late fee: 500 BDT per month on overdue amounts
- Repeated late payment may result in:
  - Loss of installment privilege
  - Withholding of report cards
  - Suspension from classes
  - Possible withdrawal

**Non-Payment:**
- Tuition fees must be current for student to attend classes
- Report cards withheld for non-payment
- Examination participation may be withheld
- Withdrawal possible for chronic non-payment

### Fee Increases

**Annual Review:**
- Fees reviewed annually by Board of Directors
- Adjustments based on:
  - Inflation and cost increases
  - Teacher salary enhancements
  - Facility improvements
  - Program enhancements

**Notification:**
- Parents notified at least 3 months before new academic year
- New fee structure published by April for following January
- Existing students locked in for academic year

**Average Increase:**
- Typically 5-10% annually
- Reflects economic conditions
- Maintains quality standards

### Refund Policies

**Security Deposit:**
- Refunded upon:
  - Graduation from Smart Academy
  - Withdrawal with proper notice
  - Adjusted for:
    - Outstanding fees
    - Lost or damaged school property
    - Unreturned items
- **No interest** paid on deposit

**Tuition Refunds:**
- Prorated based on withdrawal date
- See Refund Policy document for complete details
- Generally:
  - Before school starts: 80% refund
  - First month: 50% refund
  - After first month: No refund

**Admission Fee:**
- Non-refundable under all circumstances
- Covers administrative processing costs

## Frequently Asked Questions

### About Fees

**Q: Are fees negotiable?**
A: No, published fees are standard. However, scholarships and financial aid are available for qualified families.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Cash, bank drafts, mobile banking (bKash, Nagad), and online bank transfers. See Payment Methods for details.

**Q: Can fees be paid in installments?**
A: Yes, fees can be paid in 3 installments. See Payment Schedule for details.

**Q: Do fees increase every year?**
A: Fees may be adjusted annually, typically 5-10%. Parents are notified at least 3 months in advance.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No, all fees are published upfront. Optional services are clearly marked as optional.

**Q: What if I can't afford the fees?**
A: Apply for our scholarship program. 25% of our students receive financial assistance. We're committed to accessibility.

**Q: Do I get a discount for paying full year upfront?**
A: Yes, 5% discount on tuition if full annual tuition paid before January 31.

**Q: What happens if I withdraw my child mid-year?**
A: Tuition is prorated based on withdrawal date. See Refund Policy for complete details.

**Q: Are fees different for transfer students?**
A: No, all students pay the same fees based on grade level.

**Q: Do you charge for textbooks?**
A: Textbooks are additional and purchased separately. Average cost: 3,000-5,000 BDT per year depending on grade.

**Q: Are there fees for exams?**
A: No, examination fees are included in tuition.

**Q: Is there a fee for the orientation program?**
A: No, orientation is included for new students.
