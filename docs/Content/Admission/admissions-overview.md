## Admission Policies

### Equal Opportunity Policy

Smart Academy admits students of any race, color, national and ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally accorded or made available to students at the school. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national and ethnic origin in administration of our educational policies, admission policies, scholarship and loan programs, and athletic and other school-administered programs.

### Priority Consideration

While we maintain a fair and open admission process, priority consideration may be given to:

1. **Siblings of current students** (subject to meeting admission requirements)
2. **Children of alumni** (subject to meeting admission requirements)
3. **Early applicants** who demonstrate strong fit with Smart Academy's mission
4. **Students transferring from similar Islamic schools** (subject to evaluation)

### Special Considerations

**Scholarship Applicants:**
- Separate scholarship application required
- Financial documentation needed
- Academic merit considered
- Islamic character evaluated

**Special Needs Students:**
- Individualized evaluation
- Meeting with special education coordinator
- Assessment of our ability to meet student needs
- Honest discussion about support capacity

**Transfer Students:**
- Evaluation of previous school records
- Placement testing to determine appropriate grade
- Credit evaluation for transfer courses
- Integration support provided

## Frequently Asked Questions

### About Admissions

**Q: How competitive is admission to Smart Academy?**
A: Admission varies by grade level. Early childhood grades (PlayGroup-KG) typically have more availability. Primary and secondary grades fill quickly, so early application is recommended.

**Q: Do you accept students mid-year?**
A: Yes, mid-year admission may be available if space permits. Contact the admission office for current availability.

**Q: What are my chances of admission if I apply for financial aid?**
A: Financial aid is awarded separately from admission decisions. We admit based on merit and fit, then offer financial aid based on documented need. 25% of our students receive aid.

**Q: Do you require students to be Muslim?**
A: Smart Academy is an Islamic institution and our curriculum is grounded in Islamic values. While we give preference to Muslim applicants, we welcome students from all backgrounds who respect Islamic practices and values.

**Q: How do you support new students adjusting to Smart Academy?**
A: We offer comprehensive orientation, buddy systems, ongoing support from teachers and counselors, and parent guidance to ensure smooth transitions.

**Q: Can international students apply?**
A: Yes, we welcome international students. Additional documentation may be required. Contact the admission office for specific requirements.

**Q: What if my child has special learning needs?**
A: We provide support for diverse learning needs. Contact us to discuss your child's specific situation so we can determine if we can provide appropriate support.
