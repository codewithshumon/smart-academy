## Payment Options Overview

### Available Payment Methods

Smart Academy offers **multiple convenient payment options** to accommodate family preferences:

**✓ Cash** - Pay at finance office
**✓ Bank Draft/Pay Order** - From any bank
**✓ Mobile Banking** - bKash, Nagad, Rocket
**✓ Online Bank Transfer** - From your bank account
**✓ Payment Cards** - Debit/Credit cards (at office only)

### Choosing a Payment Method

Consider:
- **Convenience:** What's easiest for you?
- **Record-keeping:** Do you need automatic receipts?
- **Security:** Which method feels safest?
- **Fees:** Some methods have transaction fees
- **Distance:** Can you pay online or must visit?

**Office Hours:**

## Payment Receipts

### Importance of Receipts

**Always Keep Receipts For:**
- Proof of payment
- Tax purposes
- Record-keeping
- Resolving disputes
- Enrollment verification

### Receipt Information

**Every Receipt Should Include:**
- Smart Academy letterhead
- Receipt number
- Date of payment
- Student name and ID
- Grade/Class
- Amount paid (in figures and words)
- Purpose of payment
- Payment method
- Signature and stamp
- Finance officer name

### Getting Your Receipt

**In-Person Payments:**
- Receipt issued immediately
- Verify all details before leaving
- Request correction if errors found

**Online/Mobile Payments:**
- Automatic email receipt
- SMS confirmation
- Printed receipt available on request

### Lost Receipts

**If Receipt is Lost:**
1. Contact finance office immediately
2. Provide payment details (date, amount, method)
3. Student ID and name required
4. Duplicate receipt issued after verification
5. Small fee may apply (50 BDT)

## Payment Security

### Protecting Your Payments

**Cash Payments:**
- Count money before leaving
- Get receipt immediately
- Don't leave cash unattended
- Request security for large amounts

**Online/Mobile Payments:**
- Use secure internet connection
- Verify merchant number
- Keep Transaction IDs
- Check confirmation details
- Save receipts digitally

**Bank Payments:**
- Verify account details
- Include correct reference
- Keep bank receipts
- Verify with Smart Academy

### Avoiding Fraud

**Warning Signs:**
- Requests for payment to personal accounts
- Urgency or pressure tactics
- Unusual payment methods
- Requests for secret payments
- Individuals claiming to be staff but not at office

**Always:**
- Pay directly to Smart Academy accounts
- Get official receipts
- Verify with office if uncertain
- Report suspicious requests

**Report Fraudulent Activity:**
- Finance Office: +8801709-651168 (Extension 301)
- Main Office: +8801709-651168

## Frequently Asked Questions

### About Payment Methods

**Q: What payment method do you recommend?**
A: Choose what's most convenient for you. Bank transfer or mobile banking are popular for security and convenience.

**Q: Can I pay fees in monthly installments?**
A: Fees are due in 3 installments (January, May, September). Monthly payments are not available except by special arrangement.

**Q: Are there any discounts for early payment?**
A: Yes, 5% discount on tuition if full annual tuition paid before January 31.

**Q: Do you accept international payments?**
A: Yes, via bank transfer or credit card. Contact finance office for specific instructions and exchange rate information.

**Q: What if I'm having trouble making payments?**
A: Contact the finance office immediately. We can discuss payment plans or scholarship options. Don't wait until fees are overdue.

**Q: Can someone else pay on my behalf?**
A: Yes, but ensure they provide your child's name and ID number so payment is properly credited.

**Q: How do I know my payment was received?**
A: You'll receive a receipt. For online payments, you'll also get email confirmation. You can also check your parent portal account.

**Q: What if I make an overpayment?**
A: Credit will be maintained in your account and applied to future payments. Refunds available upon request.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I overpay?**
A: Yes, refunds available for overpayments. Contact finance office with receipt details. Processing takes 5-7 working days.

**Q: Do you charge for payment processing?**
A: Smart Academy doesn't charge processing fees. However, banks and mobile financial services may charge their standard fees.
