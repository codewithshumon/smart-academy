## Uniform Policy

All students wear prescribed uniform.

### Early Childhood (PlayGroup-KG)

- Shirts: Yellow polo with school logo
- Pants/Shorts: Navy blue
- Shoes: Black comfortable shoes
- Socks: White socks

### Primary (Class 1-5)

**Boys:**
- Shirt: White button-down shirt
- Pants: Navy blue trousers
- Shoes: Black formal shoes
- Socks: White socks
- Belt: Black belt

**Girls:**
- Kameez: White kameez with school logo
- Salwar: Navy blue salwar
- Scarf: White scarf (hijab)
- Shoes: Black comfortable shoes
- Socks: White socks

### Secondary (Class 6-9)

**Boys:**
- Shirt: White button-down shirt
- Pants: Navy blue trousers
- Shoes: Black formal shoes
- Socks: White socks
- Belt: Black belt

**Girls:**
- Kameez: White kameez with school logo
- Salwar: Navy blue salwar
- Scarf: White scarf (hijab)
- Shoes: Black formal shoes
- Socks: White socks

## Purchase

Uniforms available at:
- Designated suppliers (list at orientation)
- School sample for sizing
- Purchase before academic year begins
