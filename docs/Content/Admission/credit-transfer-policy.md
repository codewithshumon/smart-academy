# Credit Transfer Policy

## Transfer Credit Evaluation

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026

---

## Overview

Students transferring from other schools may receive credit for previous coursework completed.

## Eligibility

**From Similar Schools:**
- English medium institutions
- Bangladesh curriculum or equivalent
- Accredited institutions
- Documentation provided

**Credit Considered For:**
- Core academic subjects
- Age/grade-appropriate coursework
- Satisfactory completion (passing grades)
- Comparable curriculum

## Process

### Submission
- Submit course descriptions/transcripts
- Provide syllabus if available
- Grade conversion scale
- School accreditation information

### Evaluation
- Subject matter reviewed
- Depth of content assessed
- Grade level appropriateness
- Compatibility with Smart Academy curriculum

### Decision
- Credits accepted/rejected
- Placement determination
- Possible exemption from some requirements
- May need to take placement test

## Credit Limits

**Maximum Credits:**
- Up to 100% of non-core subjects may transfer
- Core subjects (English, Math, Science) require assessment
- Religious studies evaluated individually

## Grades

**Transferred Grades:**
- Recorded on transcript as "Transfer Credit"
- Not included in Smart Academy GPA
- Listed as completed requirements

## Appeals

**If Credit Denied:**
- Can provide additional documentation
- Challenge through assessment
- Reviewed by academic committee
