## Process Overview

The Smart Academy admission process is designed to be **transparent, fair, and thorough** while being respectful of families' time. From initial inquiry to final enrollment, the process typically takes **8-12 weeks**.

### Key Features

**✓ Clear Steps:** Each stage is clearly explained
**✓ Fair Evaluation:** All applicants evaluated equally
**✓ Timely Communication:** Regular updates throughout
**✓ Family-Friendly:** Respectful of parents and students
**✓ Islamic Values:** Process guided by Islamic principles

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Initial Inquiry

**Your First Contact**

**Ways to Reach Us:**
- **Website:** www.mysmart.academy
- **Phone:** +8801709-651168 (ask for Admissions)
- **Email:** admissions@smartacademybd.com
- **In Person:** Visit our admission office
- **Social Media:** Facebook @SmartAcademyBD

**What Happens:**
- You learn about Smart Academy
- We understand your needs and expectations
- You receive admission information pack
- Questions are answered

**Recommended:**
- Review website thoroughly
- Download admission information
- Prepare questions to ask
- Consider if Smart Academy aligns with your values

### Step 2: Campus Visit (Recommended but Not Required)

**Schedule Your Visit:**
- Call or email to arrange appointment
- Best times: Sunday-Thursday, 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
- Allow 1-2 hours for complete visit

**What You'll Experience:**
- Meet admission staff
- Tour campus facilities
- See classrooms in action
- Observe school environment
- Meet principal or coordinator (if available)
- Ask questions

**What to Look For:**
- Quality of facilities
- Teaching approach and classroom atmosphere
- Student behavior and engagement
- Islamic environment
- Overall feeling of the school

**What to Bring:**
- List of prepared questions
- Notebook for taking notes
- Your child (if possible)

### Step 3: Application Submission

**Obtain Application Form:**

**Options:**
- **Online:** Apply at admissions.smartacademybd.com
- **In Person:** Pick up form at admission office
- **Download:** Print form from website

**Complete the Application:**
- Fill in all required information
- Ensure accuracy and completeness
- Attach all required documents
- Sign the application

**Submit Application:**

**Submission Methods:**
- **Online:** Upload documents and submit
- **In Person:** Bring to admission office
- **Mail/Courier:** Send to Smart Academy address

**Include:**
- Completed application form
- Application fee payment (1,500 BDT)
- Required documents (see Document Checklist)
- Photographs

**Confirmation:**
- You'll receive application acknowledgment
- Application number assigned
- Assessment date communicated (if applicable)

### Step 4: Application Review

**What We Do:**
- Review your application for completeness
- Verify all documents are included
- Check minimum requirements met
- Contact you if anything missing

**If Incomplete:**
- We'll contact you within 3-5 working days
- Specify what's missing
- Give deadline for submission
- Application may be delayed

**If Complete:**
- Application moves forward
- Assessment/interview scheduled
- You'll receive date, time, and details

### Step 5: Admission Assessment

**Age-Appropriate Assessment:**

**PlayGroup-KG:**
- Date: Usually March 2026
- Duration: 30-45 minutes
- Format: Play-based observation
- Parent interview: 15-20 minutes

**Class 1-3:**
- Date: February-March 2026
- Duration: 1-2 hours
- Format: Age-appropriate testing
- Parent interview: 20-30 minutes

**Class 4-5:**
- Date: February-March 2026
- Duration: 2-3 hours
- Format: Subject-based assessment
- Student and parent interviews

**Class 6-9:**
- Date: February-March 2026
- Duration: 3-4 hours
- Format: Comprehensive testing
- Student interview: 30 minutes
- Parent interview: 30 minutes

**On Assessment Day:**
- Arrive 15 minutes early
- Bring application acknowledgment
- Student well-rested and fed
- Parents prepared for interview
- Relax and do your best

### Step 6: Interview

**Student Interview:**

**Purpose:**
- Assess communication skills
- Understand student's personality
- Gauge interest in learning
- Evaluate Islamic character
- Observe behavior and manners

**Typical Questions:**
- Tell us about yourself
- What do you like about school?
- What are your favorite subjects?
- What do you do in free time?
- Why do you want to join Smart Academy?

**Parent Interview:**

**Purpose:**
- Discuss expectations
- Explain school policies and approach
- Assess family commitment
- Answer parent questions
- Ensure mutual understanding

**Typical Topics:**
- Your educational goals for your child
- Understanding of Smart Academy's Islamic approach
- Support you can provide at home
- Questions and concerns
- Expectations from school

### Step 7: Evaluation and Decision

**What Happens:**
- Admissions committee reviews complete application
- All factors considered holistically
- Decision made based on criteria
- Comparison with other applicants

**Timeline:**
- Evaluation takes 1-2 weeks after assessment
- Decision made by committee
- Final approval by principal

**Factors Considered:**
- Academic potential and performance
- Islamic character and values
- Conduct and behavior
- Family commitment and partnership
- Seat availability

### Step 8: Notification of Decision

**How You'll Be Notified:**
- Email: Primary communication method
- Phone call: For important decisions
- SMS: Notification of sent email
- Postal mail: Formal offer letter

**Timeline:**
- Within 2 weeks of assessment
- Usually by end of March 2026

**Decision Categories:**

**1. Offer of Admission**
- Congratulations!
- Instructions for accepting offer
- Deadline to respond (usually 2 weeks)
- Enrollment information provided

**2. Waitlist**
- Your child is qualified but space is limited
- No guarantee of admission
- Will contact if space opens
- Usually notified by May-June if space available

**3. Not Admitted**
- Regret to inform
- Decision is final
- No detailed feedback provided
- May reapply next year

**4. Conditional Offer**
- Admission with conditions
- Usually academic probation
- Specific requirements outlined
- Review period specified

### Step 9: Accepting the Offer

**If Admitted:**

**Steps to Take:**
1. **Accept Offer:**
   - Sign acceptance form
   - Return by deadline specified

2. **Pay Fees:**
   - Admission fee: 10,000 BDT (one-time)
   - Security deposit: 5,000 BDT (refundable)
   - First installment of tuition
   - Payment methods available

3. **Submit Documents:**
   - Enrollment form
   - Additional required documents
   - Medical information form
   - Transportation request (if needed)

4. **Receive Confirmation:**
   - Enrollment confirmation letter
   - Student ID assigned
   - Class placement information (later)
   - Orientation invitation

**Deadline:**
- Usually 2 weeks from offer date
- Extensions may be requested with valid reason
- Offer may be withdrawn if deadline missed

### Step 10: Enrollment and Orientation

**Enrollment:**
- Officially enrolled when fees paid and documents submitted
- Records created in student information system
- Student ID assigned
- Added to class lists

**Orientation Program:**
- **Date:** Usually April 2026
- **Duration:** Half-day program
- **Purpose:** Prepare for new academic year

**Orientation Includes:**
- Welcome session
- School policies and procedures
- Meet teachers and staff
- Tour of campus
- Purchase uniforms and supplies
- Important dates and calendar
- Question and answer session

**Both Parents and Student Attend:**
- Important information shared
- Questions answered
- Smooth transition supported

## Frequently Asked Questions

### About the Process

**Q: How long does the admission process take?**
A: From application to decision, typically 8-12 weeks. Early applicants may hear sooner.

**Q: Can we check the status of our application?**
A: Yes, contact the admission office. Allow reasonable time for processing before checking.

**Q: What if we miss the application deadline?**
A: Late applications may be accepted with a late fee if space is available. Contact us immediately.

**Q: Do you have rolling admission?**
A: We have specific admission periods but may accept qualified students mid-year if space permits.

**Q: How will we be notified of the decision?**
A: Primarily by email, followed by formal letter. Phone calls may be made for important decisions.

**Q: What if we're placed on the waitlist?**
A: You'll be contacted if space opens up, usually by May-June. No guarantee of admission.

**Q: Can we appeal an admission decision?**
A: Appeals only considered for significant new information. Decision is usually final.

**Q: Do you give preference to early applicants?**
A: Early application allows thorough review but doesn't guarantee advantage over qualified later applicants.

**Q: What if we can't come for assessment on the scheduled date?**
A: Contact us immediately. Alternative dates may be available but not guaranteed.

**Q: How many students are admitted each year?**
A: Varies by grade level. Early childhood has more seats. Upper grades depend on withdrawals.
