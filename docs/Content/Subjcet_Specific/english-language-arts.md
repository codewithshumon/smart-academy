## Program Overview

### Introduction to English Language Arts at Smart Academy

English Language Arts (ELA) at Smart Academy is the cornerstone of our trilingual education system, serving as the primary medium of instruction across all grade levels from PlayGroup through Class 9. Our comprehensive ELA program develops proficiency in reading, writing, speaking, and listening while integrating Islamic values, critical thinking, and character development.

As Bangladesh increasingly participates in the global economy and international community, English proficiency has become essential for academic success, career opportunities, and cultural exchange. Smart Academy recognizes this reality while maintaining strong commitment to Bengali as our national language and Arabic as the language of the Quran.

Our ELA program goes beyond basic language acquisition to develop:
- **Critical Literacy**: Analyzing, evaluating, and creating texts with discernment
- **Communication Excellence**: Expressing ideas clearly, persuasively, and appropriately
- **Cultural Competence**: Understanding diverse perspectives while maintaining Islamic identity
- **Academic Language**: Mastering the sophisticated language of scholarship
- **Creative Expression**: Using language artfully and imaginatively
- **Ethical Communication**: Speaking and writing with honesty, respect, and integrity

### Program Goals

By the time students complete Class 9 at Smart Academy, they will:

1. **Read Proficiently**: Comprehend complex texts across genres and disciplines
2. **Write Effectively**: Compose clear, organized, and purposeful writing for various audiences
3. **Speak Confidently**: Communicate ideas orally with clarity and poise
4. **Listen Actively**: Understand and respond thoughtfully to spoken language
5. **Think Critically**: Analyze texts, arguments, and ideas with sophistication
6. **Create Meaningfully**: Produce original writing and presentations that reflect understanding
7. **Integrate Islamic Values**: Apply Islamic ethical principles in all communication
8. **Navigate Multiple Literacies**: Succeed with print, digital, and media texts
9. **Appreciate Literature**: Engage deeply with diverse literary traditions
10. **Prepare for Future Success**: Possess language skills for higher education and careers

### Scope and Sequence

Our ELA curriculum is carefully sequenced across developmental stages:

**Early Childhood (Ages 3-6)**
- Oral language development
- Phonological awareness
- Letter recognition and phonics
- Early reading experiences
- Emergent writing
- Listening comprehension

**Primary Education (Ages 6-11)**
- Systematic phonics instruction
- Reading fluency development
- Comprehension strategies
- Writing process instruction
- Grammar and mechanics
- Vocabulary expansion
- Literature appreciation
- Speaking and listening skills

**Secondary Education (Ages 11-15)**
- Advanced reading comprehension
- Literary analysis
- Sophisticated writing (argument, analysis, narrative)
- Advanced grammar and syntax
- Research and citation
- Public speaking and debate
- Media literacy
- College and career preparation

## Success Indicators

### Expected Outcomes by Grade Level

**Early Childhood (PlayGroup - KG)**
- Oral language proficiency in English
- Phonological awareness
- Letter recognition and sounds
- Emergent reading skills
- Early writing attempts
- Love of books and stories

**Primary (Class 1-5)**
- Reading fluency and comprehension at grade level or above
- Writing proficiency across genres
- Strong grammar and mechanics foundation
- Expanding vocabulary
- Effective speaking and listening
- Positive attitude toward reading and writing

**Secondary (Class 6-9)**
- Advanced literacy skills
- Sophisticated analysis and interpretation
- Excellent writing across purposes
- Strong communication skills
- College and career readiness
- Excellent examination results

### Benchmarks for Excellence

**Reading Benchmarks**

**Class 1**: Level E-G by end of year
**Class 2**: Level H-J by end of year
**Class 3**: Level K-M by end of year
**Class 4**: Level N-P by end of year
**Class 5**: Level Q-S by end of year
**Class 6**: Level T-V by end of year
**Class 7**: Level W-Y by end of year
**Class 8**: Level Z by end of year
**Class 9**: Beyond Level Z (college-level texts)

**Writing Benchmarks**

**Primary (Class 1-5)**
- Write organized compositions appropriate to grade
- Apply grade-level grammar and mechanics
- Develop ideas with details
- Demonstrate writing process

**Secondary (Class 6-9)**
- Produce multi-paragraph essays
- Develop sophisticated arguments
- Demonstrate voice and style
- Research and cite sources properly
- Edit to high standards

**Examination Benchmarks**

**Primary Scholarship Exam (PSC - Class 5)**
- Target: 100% pass rate
- Average: 80%+ scores
- Many students achieving A+ (80+)

**Junior School Certificate (JSC - Class 8)**
- Target: 100% pass rate
- Average: 85%+ scores
- Majority of students achieving A/A+

**Secondary School Certificate (SSC - Class 10)**
- Target: 100% pass rate
- Average: 90%+ scores
- Many students achieving A+ (80+)

English Language Arts education at Smart Academy provides students with the literacy skills essential for academic success, career achievement, and lifelong learning.
