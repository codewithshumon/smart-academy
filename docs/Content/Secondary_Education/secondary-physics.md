## Class 9 Physics Curriculum

### Chapter 1: Physical World & Measurement
- Physics and its scope, branches, importance
- Physical quantities: Base and derived, SI units
- Measurement instruments: Ruler, vernier calipers, screw gauge
- Accuracy, precision, significant figures, errors
- Dimensional analysis and applications

### Chapter 2: Motion
- Distance, displacement, speed, velocity, acceleration
- Equations of motion: v=u+at, s=ut+½at², v²=u²+2as
- Motion graphs: Distance-time, velocity-time
- Projectile motion, circular motion basics
- Applications and problem-solving

### Chapter 3: Force & Motion (Dynamics)
- Newton's Three Laws of Motion
- Force, mass, weight (W=mg)
- Friction: Types, coefficients, applications
- Momentum (p=mv), impulse, conservation
- Applications in daily life

### Chapter 4: Work, Energy, Power
- Work: W = F × s × cosθ
- Kinetic energy: KE = ½mv²
- Potential energy: PE = mgh
- Conservation of energy
- Power: P = W/t = Fv
- Simple machines: Mechanical advantage, efficiency

### Chapter 5: Properties of Matter
- States of matter, density (ρ=m/V)
- Pressure: P=F/A, liquid pressure P=ρgh
- Pascal's principle, Archimedes' principle
- Elasticity, Hooke's law: F=kx
- Applications: Hydraulics, floatation

### Chapter 6: Heat & Thermometry
- Temperature vs. heat
- Temperature scales: Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin
- Specific heat capacity: Q=mcΔT
- Thermal expansion: Linear, area, volume
- Change of state, latent heat: Q=mL
- Heat transfer: Conduction, convection, radiation

### Chapter 7: Wave Motion & Sound
- Wave types: Transverse, longitudinal
- Wave parameters: λ, f, T, amplitude
- Wave equation: v=fλ
- Sound: Production, propagation, speed
- Reflection (echo), refraction, resonance
- Loudness, pitch, quality
- Applications: SONAR, ultrasound

### Chapter 8: Light & Optics
- Nature of light, speed of light
- Reflection: Laws, mirrors (plane, spherical)
- Mirror formula: 1/f = 1/v + 1/u
- Refraction: Laws, Snell's law, refractive index
- Total internal reflection, critical angle
- Lenses: Types, image formation, lens formula
- Dispersion, spectrum, colors
- Optical instruments: Microscope, telescope

## Class 10 Physics Curriculum

### Chapter 9: Current Electricity
- Electric current: I=Q/t
- Potential difference: V=W/Q
- Resistance: Ohm's law R=V/I
- Resistivity: R=ρL/A
- Series and parallel resistor combinations
- Electric power: P=VI=I²R=V²/R
- Heating effect: H=I²Rt
- Domestic electricity, safety

### Chapter 10: Magnetism
- Magnetic properties, field, field lines
- Magnetic effect of current, electromagnets
- Force on current-carrying conductor: F=BIL
- Fleming's left-hand rule
- Electromagnetic induction, Faraday's laws
- Fleming's right-hand rule, Lenz's law
- Electric motor and generator principles
- Transformer: Np/Ns = Vp/Vs = Is/Ip

### Chapter 11: Modern Physics
- Atomic structure: Thomson, Rutherford, Bohr models
- Atomic number, mass number
- Radioactivity: α, β, γ radiation
- Half-life, decay equations
- Nuclear fission and fusion
- E=mc² (mass-energy equivalence)
- Applications and safety
- Semiconductors (basic introduction)

## SSC Examination Strategies

### MCQ Strategy (30 marks, 30-35 minutes):
- Answer easy questions first
- Eliminate obviously wrong options
- Use calculations when needed
- Intelligent guessing if unsure
- Review if time permits

### Creative Questions (45 marks, 100-105 minutes):
- Read stem carefully
- Understand what each part asks
- Show all working steps
- Write formulas before calculation
- Include units in final answer
- Draw diagrams where helpful
- Attempt all parts for partial credit

### Practical Exam (25 marks):
- Practice all experiments thoroughly
- Fast and accurate measurements
- Complete, neat laboratory report
- Prepare for viva voce questions
- Marks breakdown:
  - Performance: 10 marks
  - Report: 10 marks
  - Viva: 5 marks

## Important Formulas Summary

**Motion:**
- v = u + at
- s = ut + ½at²
- v² = u² + 2as

**Force & Energy:**
- F = ma
- p = mv
- W = F × s
- KE = ½mv²
- PE = mgh
- P = W/t

**Matter:**
- ρ = m/V
- P = F/A
- P = ρgh (liquid)
- F = kx (Hooke's)

**Heat:**
- Q = mcΔT
- Q = mL

**Waves:**
- v = fλ

**Electricity:**
- V = IR
- P = VI = I²R = V²/R
- Rs = R₁ + R₂ + ...
- 1/Rp = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + ...

**Magnetism:**
- F = BIL
- Np/Ns = Vp/Vs

**Optics:**
- n = c/v
- 1/f = 1/v ± 1/u
