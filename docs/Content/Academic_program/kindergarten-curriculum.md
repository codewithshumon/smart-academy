# Kindergarten Curriculum (Ages 5-6 Years)

**Smart Academy | Ready for Tomorrow**

---

## Program Overview

### Welcome to Kindergarten!

Kindergarten at Smart Academy represents the crucial final year of early childhood education before children transition to formal schooling in Class 1. Our program for 5-6 year-olds combines rigorous academic preparation with continued play-based learning, ensuring children are confident, competent, and ready for the challenges ahead while maintaining their love of learning and strong Islamic foundation.

### Age Range
- **Minimum Age**: 5 years old by December 31
- **Maximum Age**: 6 years old
- **Program Duration**: 10 months (September - June)

### Class Structure
- **Class Size**: 20-22 students per class
- **Teacher-Student Ratio**: 1:10-12 (1 lead teacher)
- **Schedule**: Full-day only (6.5 hours)
- **Classes**: 2-3 Kindergarten sections

---

### Kindergarten Readiness Focus

## Core Curriculum

### 1. Islamic Studies (40-45 minutes daily)

**Quranic Education - Nazra Program:**

*Advanced Reading:*
- Fluent reading of simple Quranic verses
- Complete Harakat (Fatha, Kasra, Damma, Sukoon)
- Tanween understanding
- Shad and Madd introduction
- Joining 3-4 letter words
- Simple passages from Juz Amma

*Tajweed Basics:*
- Makharij (letter pronunciation points)
- Basic Tajweed rules
- Correct articulation
- Rhythm and pausing

*Memorization (Hifz):*
Students complete last 10 Surahs of Juz Amma:
- Al-Bayyinah to An-Nas
- Selected du'as and Ayat
- Daily practice and review
- Individual progress tracking
- For advanced: begin additional Surahs

**Arabic Language:**
- Arabic alphabet mastery (reading & writing)
- Joining letters in all positions
- 200+ Arabic vocabulary words
- Simple sentences (noun+verb)
- Numbers 1-100 in Arabic
- Days of week, months
- Colors, animals, objects
- Quranic Arabic phrases

**Islamic Studies Curriculum:**

*Aqeedah (Beliefs):*
- Six Pillars of Iman (detailed)
- Allah's 99 names (20 names with meanings)
- Prophets and their messages (10 prophets)
- Books of Allah
- Angels and their duties
- Day of Judgment concept
- Qadr (Divine Decree)

*Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence):*
- Five Pillars of Islam (detailed)
- Salah: How to pray complete prayer
- Wudu: Complete steps and rules
- Purification (Taharah)
- Fasting: Rules and benefits
- Zakah: Concept of charity
- Hajj: Introduction to pilgrimage

*Seerah (Prophet's Biography):*
- Complete life overview
- Early life in Makkah
- Revelation and early Islam
- Migration to Madinah
- Key battles and their lessons
- Conquest of Makkah
- Farewell sermon
- The Prophet as role model

*Hadith Studies:*
- Selected Hadith with meanings
- 40 Hadith memorization program
- Understanding and application
- Stories from Hadith

*Akhlaq (Islamic Character):*
Daily emphasis on:
- Truthfulness in all situations
- Trustworthiness and honesty
- Kindness to all creation
- Respect for elders
- Mercy and compassion
- Patience in difficulty
- Gratitude for blessings
- Humility and modesty
- Justice and fairness
- Generosity and charity

*Islamic History:*
- Sahabah (Companions) stories
- Four Rightly Guided Caliphs
- Women in early Islam
- Islamic Golden Age introduction
- Muslim contributions to science

**Practical Islamic Living:**
- Daily five prayers (awareness and practice)
- Proper mosque etiquette
- Quran respect and handling
- Islamic holidays and their meanings
- Family Islamic practices
- Du'as for daily situations

### 2. English Language Arts (60 minutes daily)

**Phonics & Word Study:**
- All letter sounds mastered
- Consonant blends (bl, cr, st, etc.)
- Consonant digraphs (sh, ch, th, wh)
- Short vowels in CVC words
- Long vowels (CVCe pattern)
- Sight words (100+ words)
- Word families (-at, -ig, -op, etc.)
- Beginning phonics rules

**Reading Skills:**
- Decoding strategies
- Reading simple sentences fluently
- Beginning chapter books (with support)
- Comprehension strategies:
  - Predicting
  - Questioning
  - Visualizing
  - Making connections
  - Summarizing
- Fiction vs. non-fiction
- Story elements (characters, setting, plot)
- Main idea and details
- Sequencing events
- Drawing inferences

**Writing Development:**
- Correct letter formation (all letters)
- Proper spacing between words
- Capital letters and periods
- Writing complete sentences
- Beginning creative writing
- Descriptive writing
- Personal narratives
- Simple poetry
- Labels and captions
- Lists and instructions
- Journal writing

**Oral Language:**
- Speaking in complex sentences
- Presenting to class
- Descriptive language
- Asking and answering questions
- Retelling stories in order
- Following multi-step directions
- Participating in discussions
- Expressing opinions with reasons
- Active listening skills

**Handwriting:**
- Proper pencil grip
- Correct posture
- Letter formation precision
- Writing on lines
- Appropriate size and spacing
- Beginning cursive awareness

**Literature Study:**
- Daily read-aloud (chapter books)
- Independent reading time
- Class library use
- Book discussions
- Author studies
- Genre exploration
- Poetry appreciation
- Islamic literature

### 3. Mathematics (50 minutes daily)

**Geometry:**

**Measurement:**

- Temperature concepts

**Data & Graphing:**
- Collect and organize data
- Picture graphs

- Tally charts

**Patterns & Algebra:**

**Problem-Solving:**
- Multiple strategies

- Act it out

- Logical reasoning
- Explain thinking
- Check for reasonableness

## Kindergarten Readiness Benchmarks

### End-of-Year Expectations

**Islamic Development:**
✓ Fluent Quran reading of simple passages
✓ 20+ Surahs memorized
✓ Participate in 5 daily prayers
✓ Strong Islamic character demonstrated
✓ Understanding of basic Fiqh
✓ Arabic reading and writing

**Reading:**
✓ Read level E-G books independently
✓ Decode CVC and CVCe words
✓ Know 100+ sight words
✓ Comprehend simple texts
✓ Read fluently (appropriate speed)

**Writing:**
✓ Write complete sentences
✓ Use capital letters and periods
✓ Spell phonetically
✓ Write simple stories
✓ Legible handwriting

**Mathematics:**
✓ Add/subtract fluently within 10
✓ Solve word problems within 20
✓ Count to 100 by 1s, 10s
✓ Understand place value (tens/ones)
✓ Tell time to hour/half-hour
✓ Identify coins and values

**Social-Emotional:**
✓ Self-regulation mastery
✓ Conflict resolution independently
✓ Work collaboratively
✓ Demonstrate perseverance
✓ Show academic confidence
✓ Leadership abilities
