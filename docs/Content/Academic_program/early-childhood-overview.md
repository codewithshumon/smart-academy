**Smart Academy | Building Strong Foundations for Lifelong Learning**

## Program Introduction

Welcome to Smart Academy's Early Childhood Education (ECE) program, where we believe that the early years from ages 3 to 6 represent the most critical developmental period in a child's life. Our comprehensive ECE program combines Islamic values with modern, research-based early childhood practices to create an environment where young learners thrive academically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

- Preparing children for kindergarten and beyond
- Building lifelong love of learning

## Program Structure

### Three Distinct Levels

Our ECE program serves children ages 3-6 across three progressive levels:

#### **PlayGroup (Ages 3-4 years)**
- **Focus**: Socialization, basic routines, sensory exploration
- **Class Size**: 15-18 students per class
- **Teacher Ratio**: 1:6-8 (1 lead teacher + 1 assistant)
- **Duration**: Half-day (3.5 hours) or Full-day (6 hours)
- **Emphasis**: Learning through play, developing independence

#### **Nursery (Ages 4-5 years)**
- **Focus**: Early literacy, numeracy foundations, creativity
- **Class Size**: 18-20 students per class
- **Teacher Ratio**: 1:8-10 (1 lead teacher + 1 assistant)
- **Duration**: Half-day (3.5 hours) or Full-day (6 hours)
- **Emphasis**: Structured learning activities, skill development

#### **Kindergarten (Ages 5-6 years)**
- **Focus**: School readiness, academic foundations, critical thinking
- **Class Size**: 20-22 students per class
- **Teacher Ratio**: 1:10-12 (1 lead teacher)
- **Duration**: Full-day (6 hours)
- **Emphasis**: Preparation for Class 1, independence, problem-solving
