# Nursery Curriculum (Ages 4-5 Years)

**Smart Academy | Building Skills Through Exploration**

---

## Program Overview

### Welcome to Nursery!

Nursery at Smart Academy serves children ages 4-5 years during a critical period of cognitive growth, social development, and skill acquisition. Building upon the PlayGroup foundation, our Nursery program introduces more structured learning while maintaining a playful, exploratory approach that honors children's natural curiosity and developmental needs.

### Age Range
- **Minimum Age**: 4 years old by December 31
- **Maximum Age**: 5 years old
- **Program Duration**: 10 months (September - June)

### Class Structure
- **Class Size**: 18-20 students per class
- **Teacher-Student Ratio**: 1:8-10 (1 lead teacher + 1 assistant)
- **Schedule Options**: Half-day (3.5 hours) or Full-day (6 hours)
- **Classes**: 2-3 Nursery sections

## Core Subject Areas

### 1. Islamic Studies (30-35 minutes daily)

**Quranic Education:**

*Nazra (Reading):*
- All Arabic letters (Alif to Ya)
- Letter recognition and sounds
- Joining letters (2-3 letter words)
- Short Harakat (Fatha, Kasra, Damma)
- Simple Quranic words

*Memorization (Hifz):*
- Surah Al-Fatiha (complete)
- Last 5 Surahs of Juz Amma:
  - Al-Fil
  - Quraish
  - Al-Ma'un
  - Al-Kawthar
  - Al-Kafirun
- Selected du'as

**Arabic Language:**
- Arabic alphabet mastery
- Simple Arabic words (100+)
- Basic greetings and phrases
- Islamic terminology
- Arabic number recognition (1-10)
- Simple Arabic songs

**Islamic Knowledge:**

*Aqeedah (Beliefs):*
- Six pillars of Iman (basic)
- Allah's names (5-10 names)
- Prophets' names (5 main prophets)
- Angels and their roles
- Qadr (Allah's will)

*Fiqh (Practices):*
- Five pillars of Islam
- Wudu steps (demonstration)
- Prayer movements and meaning
- Fasting concept (Ramadan)
- Charity (Sadaqah) importance

*Seerah (Prophet's Life):*
- Birth and childhood
- Receiving revelation
- Hijrah to Madinah
- Key events (simplified)
- Character traits to emulate

*Akhlaq (Character):*
- Truthfulness (Sidq)
- Honesty (Amanah)
- Kindness (Ihsan)
- Respect (Adab)
- Patience (Sabr)
- Gratitude (Shukr)
- Humility
- Generosity
- Cleanliness

**Daily Islamic Practices:**
- Morning du'as and adhkar
- Bismillah before activities
- Du'a before/after eating
- Bathroom du'as
- Sleeping and waking du'as
- Observing prayer times
- Participation in Dhuhr prayer

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How is Nursery different from PlayGroup?**
A: More structured learning, longer attention span activities, academic skill building, and more independence expectations.

**Q: Will my child learn to read?**
A: Children will learn pre-reading skills and many will begin reading simple words. Full reading develops in Kindergarten.

**Q: How much Quran will my child learn?**
A: All Arabic letters, joining basics, and memorization of 10+ short Surahs. Individual progress varies.

**Q: Is homework given?**
A: Optional practice packets are sent home weekly. Daily reading together is strongly encouraged.

**Q: How can I support learning at home?**
A: Read daily, practice letters/numbers, listen to Quran together, and do suggested activities from newsletters.

**Q: What if my child needs extra help?**
A: We provide differentiated instruction and communicate early if concerns arise. Referrals available if needed.

**Q: Will my child be ready for Kindergarten?**
A: Yes! Our program specifically prepares children for Kindergarten success academically, socially, and Islamically.
