# PlayGroup Curriculum (Ages 3-4 Years)

**Smart Academy | First Steps in Learning**

---

## Program Overview

### Welcome to PlayGroup!

PlayGroup at Smart Academy is designed specifically for children aged 3-4 years, recognizing this as a crucial period of rapid development, exploration, and socialization. Our program provides a gentle, nurturing introduction to school life where children learn through play, develop independence, and begin building strong Islamic foundations.

###

 Age Range
- **Minimum Age**: 3 years old by December 31
- **Maximum Age**: 4 years old
- **Ideal Start**: September (beginning of academic year)
- **Rolling Admission**: Available based on capacity

### Class Structure
- **Class Size**: 15-18 students
- **Teacher-Student Ratio**: 1:6-8 (1 lead teacher + 1 assistant)
- **Schedule Options**: Half-day (3.5 hours) or Full-day (6 hours)
- **Program Duration**: 10 months (September - June)

## Developmental Focus

### Key Developmental Goals

At this age, children are developing rapidly across all domains. Our PlayGroup program focuses on:

**Social-Emotional Development**
- Separating from parents comfortably
- Building trust with teachers and peers
- Expressing emotions appropriately
- Following simple classroom routines
- Playing cooperatively with others
- Developing self-help skills

**Physical Development**
- Gross motor: running, jumping, climbing
- Fine motor: grasping, manipulating objects
- Self-care: toileting, handwashing, dressing
- Coordination and balance
- Sensory exploration

**Cognitive Development**
- Curiosity and exploration
- Cause-and-effect understanding
- Simple problem-solving
- Memory development
- Attention span building
- Symbolic thinking

**Language Development**
- Vocabulary expansion
- Listening skills
- Basic conversation skills
- Following simple directions
- Expressing needs and wants
- Exposure to multiple languages

**Spiritual Development**
- Introduction to Allah and Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)
- Simple prayers and du'as
- Islamic manners (Adab)
- Love for Quran
- Basic Islamic concepts

## Curriculum Framework

### Learning Through Play

Our PlayGroup curriculum is entirely play-based, recognizing that play is the primary mode of learning for 3-4 year olds:

**Free Play (40% of day)**
- Child-initiated exploration
- Learning centers
- Social interaction
- Independent discovery

**Guided Play (30% of day)**
- Teacher-facilitated activities
- Skill-building games
- Thematic projects
- Small group work

**Structured Activities (20% of day)**
- Circle time
- Story time
- Music and movement
- Prayer time

**Routines & Transitions (10% of day)**
- Snack time
- Bathroom breaks
- Clean-up
- Arrival and dismissal

## Daily Learning Activities

### 1. Islamic Studies (15-20 minutes daily)

**Quranic Introduction:**
- Listening to Quran recitation
- Learning Arabic letters through songs
- Memorizing very short Surahs:
  - Surah Al-Fatiha (introduction only)
  - Surah Al-Ikhlas
  - Surah Al-Falaq
  - Surah An-Nas
- Simple Quranic Arabic words

**Basic Islamic Concepts:**
- Allah is our Creator
- Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) is Allah's messenger
- Stories of Prophets (very simple versions)
- Islamic greetings: Assalamu Alaikum
- Simple du'as:
  - Before eating
  - Before sleeping
  - When waking up
  - Entering/leaving bathroom

**Islamic Manners (Adab):**
- Saying Bismillah before activities
- Using right hand for eating
- Respecting elders
- Sharing with friends
- Being kind to animals
- Cleanliness

**Prayer Introduction:**
- Observing others pray
- Simple prayer movements (as play)
- Wudu demonstration
- Praying in congregation (observation)

### 2. English Language Arts (30 minutes daily)

**Listening Skills:**
- Story time (daily)
- Following simple instructions
- Listening games
- Sound discrimination
- Music and rhymes

**Speaking Skills:**
- Show and tell
- Circle time discussions
- Expressing needs and wants

- Singing songs and nursery rhymes

**Pre-Reading Skills:**
- Book handling
- Page turning
- Picture recognition
- Story sequencing
- Environmental print awareness

**Pre-Writing Skills:**
- Scribbling and mark-making
- Using various writing tools
- Hand strengthening activities
- Tracing simple shapes
- Drawing pictures

**Phonics Introduction:**
- Listening to letter sounds
- Rhyming words
- Beginning sounds recognition

### 3. Mathematics (20-25 minutes daily)

**Numbers & Counting:**
- Rote counting 1-10

- Number recognition 1-5

**Shapes & Colors:**

**Patterns & Sorting:**

- Sorting by one attribute (color, size)

**Spatial Concepts:**
- Positional words: in, on, under, over

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does my child need to be toilet trained?**
A: Yes, children should be fully toilet trained before starting PlayGroup. We will support occasional accidents.

**Q: What if my child cries during drop-off?**
A: Separation anxiety is normal at this age. We have strategies to help, and it usually improves within a few weeks.

**Q: Will my child learn to read in PlayGroup?**
A: PlayGroup focuses on pre-reading skills. Formal reading instruction begins in Nursery/KG. We build strong foundations.

**Q: How much homework will there be?**
A: No formal homework. We may send optional activities to reinforce learning at home.

**Q: What should my child bring to school?**
A: Backpack, water bottle, extra clothes, and any comfort item for rest time (full-day program).

**Q: Can I visit the classroom?**
A: After the initial adjustment period, parents are welcome to visit by appointment. We also have observation windows.

**Q: How do you handle discipline?**
A: We use positive guidance, redirection, natural consequences, and teaching appropriate behaviors. Islamic values guide our approach.

**Q: What if my child has special dietary needs?**
A: Please inform us of all allergies and dietary restrictions. We accommodate all needs and respect halal requirements.
