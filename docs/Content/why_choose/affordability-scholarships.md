## Scholarship Opportunities

### Don't Let Cost Stop You!

**25% of students receive financial assistance**

### 1. Need-Based Scholarship

**Eligibility:**
- Family income below threshold
- Unable to afford full fees
- Commitment to academic performance
- Bangladeshi citizenship

**Coverage:** 25-100% of tuition

**Application Process:**
1. Complete financial aid form
2. Submit income proof (salary slip, tax return, affidavit)
3. Provide recommendation letters (2)
4. Interview with scholarship committee
5. Decision within 2 weeks

**Timeline:** Apply during admission process

**Renewal:** Annual review based on:
- Financial situation
- Academic performance (minimum 70%)
- Behavioral standards
- Attendance (minimum 90%)

**Current Recipients:** 95 students

---

### 2. Merit-Based Scholarship

**Eligibility:**
- Outstanding academic performance (85%+ previous grades)
- Exemplary character
- Leadership potential
- Contribution to school community

**Coverage:** 25-50% of tuition

**Application:** Automatic consideration based on:
- Academic records
- Entrance assessment
- Teacher recommendations
- Interview performance

**Renewal Criteria:**
- Maintain 85%+ grades
- Exemplary behavior
- Active participation
- Leadership demonstration

**Current Recipients:** 20 students

---

### 3. Sibling Discount

**Automatic Benefits:**
- **2nd Child:** 10% discount
- **3rd Child:** 15% discount
- **4th+ Child:** 20% discount

**Conditions:**
- All children must be enrolled simultaneously
- Discount applies to all enrolled siblings
- Automatic application
- No separate application needed

**Example:**
- **Family with 3 children:**
  - Child 1: Full tuition
  - Child 2: 10% discount
  - Child 3: 15% discount
  - **Total Savings:** 8.75% on combined tuition

**Current Families:** 40 families benefiting

---

### 4. Hifz Scholarship

**Eligibility:**
- Enrolling in Hifz program
- Commitment to complete memorization
- Good academic potential
- Parent support and commitment

**Coverage:** 
- **Full tuition**
- **Monthly stipend** (BDT 2,000)
- **Free books**
- **Uniform allowance**

**Conditions:**
- Maintain Hifz program progress
- Complete memorization within 5 years
- 75%+ in academic subjects
- Exemplary behavior

**Commitment Required:**
- Daily attendance in Hifz classes
- Home practice
- Parent involvement
- Regular progress meetings

**Current Recipients:** 10 students

---

### 5. Orphan Support Program

**Eligibility:** 
- Orphaned children (one or both parents deceased)
- Documentation required

**Coverage:** Up to 100% of ALL fees including:
- Full tuition waiver
- Free uniform (2 sets/year)
- Free textbooks and materials
- Free meals (lunch program)
- Free transportation
- Free co-curricular activities

**Additional Support:**
- Counseling services
- Mentorship program
- Special care and attention
- Community support

**Sponsorship:** Through Smart Foundation and dedicated donors

**Application:**
- Death certificate(s)
- Guardian information
- Financial situation assessment
- Interview

**Current Recipients:** 15 students

---

### 6. Teacher's Children Scholarship

**Eligibility:** Children of full-time Smart Academy teachers

**Coverage:** 50% tuition discount

**Benefits:**
- Reduced tuition
- Insider knowledge of education
- Stronger school-home connection

**Current Recipients:** 8 students

## How to Apply for Financial Aid

### Step-by-Step Process

**Step 1: Initial Application**
- Submit admission application
- Indicate financial aid interest
- Receive financial aid forms

**Step 2: Complete Documentation**
- Financial aid application
- Income proof (salary slips, tax returns, affidavits)
- Bank statements (3 months)
- Property/asset documentation
- 2 recommendation letters
- Family composition details

**Step 3: Submission**
- Submit to admissions office
- Include all required documents
- Ensure completeness

**Step 4: Review**
- Scholarship committee reviews
- May request additional information
- May schedule interview
- Evaluation typically 2 weeks

**Step 5: Decision**
- Notification of decision
- Award letter if approved
- Details of scholarship terms
- Acceptance/decline deadline

**Step 6: Enrollment**
- Accept scholarship offer
- Complete admission process
- Sign scholarship agreement
- Begin studies!

## FAQs

**Q: Can I apply for multiple scholarships?**
A: Yes! Need-based and merit-based can combine. Sibling discount applies automatically.

**Q: What if my financial situation changes?**
A: Contact us immediately. We can review and adjust if necessary.

**Q: Is scholarship renewable?**
A: Yes, most scholarships renewable annually based on criteria.

**Q: How confidential is financial information?**
A: Completely confidential. Only scholarship committee accesses it.

**Q: Can I get scholarship mid-year?**
A: Typically awarded at admission, but emergency assistance available.

**Q: What if I can't afford even with scholarship?**
A: Let's talk. We work to find solutions for truly deserving students.
