## Our Islamic Education Philosophy

**Core Principles:**
1. Islam as foundation, not addition
2. Authentic sources (Quran & Sunnah)
3. Integration, not compartmentalization
4. Living faith, not mere knowledge
5. Modern context, timeless values

## Comprehensive Islamic Curriculum

### Daily Structure:
- **8:00 AM:** Quran recitation and Islamic reminder
- **10:30 AM:** Morning Dhikr
- **12:30 PM:** Dhuhr prayer (congregation)
- **3:30 PM:** Asr prayer before dismissal

### Core Components:

**1. Quranic Studies (Daily)**
- Nazra with Tajweed
- Hifz program available
- Tafseer (interpretation)
- Quranic Arabic

**2. Arabic Language (5 periods/week)**
- Classical Arabic for Quran
- Modern Arabic for communication
- Islamic literature
- Calligraphy

**3. Islamic Studies (5 periods/week)**
- Aqeedah (beliefs)
- Fiqh (jurisprudence)
- Hadith
- Seerah (Prophet's biography)
- Islamic history

**4. Akhlaq (Integrated Daily)**
- Truthfulness, kindness, respect
- Patience, gratitude, humility
- Justice, forgiveness

## Quranic Education Program

### Goal: Every student reading Quran fluently by Class 5

**Progression:**
- **PlayGroup-KG:** Arabic letters, short Surahs
- **Class 1-3:** Nazra begins, Tajweed basics
- **Class 4-5:** Nazra completion, Tajweed mastery
- **Class 6-9:** Tafseer, continued memorization

### Hifz Program:
- 15 Huffaz completed (2020-2025)
- 45 students currently in program
- Specialized teachers
- Structured curriculum
- Scholarship opportunities
