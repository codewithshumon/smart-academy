## What is STEAM?

STEAM integrates:
- **S**cience
- **T**echnology  
- **E**ngineering
- **A**rts
- **M**athematics

**Emphasis:**
- Interdisciplinary connections
- Real-world problem-solving
- Hands-on experimentation
- Creative thinking
- Collaborative learning

---

### Benefits:

**1. Critical Thinking & Problem-Solving**

**2. Collaboration & Communication**
- Teamwork on projects

**3. Technology Integration**
- Digital literacy

**4. Creativity & Innovation**

**5. Real-World Relevance**

## National Context

**Bangladesh Challenges:**
- Limited STEM resources
- Large class sizes (45:1 average)
- Minimal technology access in rural areas
- Rote memorization emphasis
- Urban-rural education divide

**Smart Academy's Response:**
- 3 fully equipped science labs
- 65 modern computers
- Robotics and coding resources
- Small classes (max 25 students)
- STEAM-trained faculty
