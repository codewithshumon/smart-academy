## Campus Safety Protocols

**Document Version:** 1.0

## Introduction

At Smart Academy, student safety is our top priority. We have implemented comprehensive security measures, strict protocols, and continuous monitoring to ensure every child learns in a completely safe environment.

**Commitment:** Parents can have complete peace of mind knowing their children are protected in every way possible.

---

## Physical Security Systems

### 1. Surveillance System

**CCTV Coverage:**
- **50+ high-resolution cameras**
- Strategic placement across entire campus
- 24/7 recording
- Monitored by security personnel
- Cloud backup storage
- 30-day retention

**Coverage Areas:**
- All entrances and exits
- Hallways and corridors
- Playgrounds and outdoor areas
- Parking areas
- Building perimeters
- Common areas

**Monitoring:**
- Real-time monitoring room
- Trained security personnel
- Alert systems for suspicious activity
- Regular review of footage
- Evidence for incidents if needed

---

### 2. Access Control

**Entry/Exit Management:**
- **Single controlled entry** point during school hours
- Security personnel at entrance
- Visitor registration system
- ID verification required
- Sign-in/sign-out procedures
- Emergency exits marked and monitored

**Biometric Attendance:**
- Student fingerprint system
- Accurate attendance tracking
- Parent notification of arrival/departure
- Staff attendance monitored
- No proxy attendance possible

**Visitor Management:**
- All visitors must register
- Government ID required
- Purpose of visit documented
- Visitor badge issued
- Escort to destination
- Sign-out upon departure

- Regular training
- Emergency response trained
- Clear protocols

**Responsibilities:**
- Entrance monitoring
- Campus patrols
- Incident response

- Emergency coordination
- Student safety oversight
