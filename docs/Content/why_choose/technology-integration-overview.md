# Technology Integration Overview

## Our Technology Vision

Smart Academy leverages technology to:
- Enhance learning experiences
- Develop digital literacy
- Bridge geographic barriers
- Personalize education
- Prepare students for future

**Commitment:** 100% smart classrooms, every student develops digital literacy.

## Infrastructure Overview

### Network & Connectivity:
- 100 Mbps dedicated internet
- Full campus WiFi
- Content filtering
- Cyber security protocols
- 24/7 availability

### Hardware:
**Computer Labs:** 65 modern computers (3:1 ratio)
**Classrooms:** 30 interactive whiteboards, 30 projectors
**Mobile Devices:** 25 tablets, 20 laptops
**Servers:** Local and cloud backup

### Software:
- Microsoft Office, Google Workspace
- Math platforms (Khan Academy, IXL)
- Science simulations
- Programming environments (Scratch, Python)
- Islamic education apps
- Learning Management System
