# Faculty Qualifications

## Teacher Requirements and Credentials

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026

---

## Overview

Smart Academy maintains high standards for faculty qualifications to ensure quality education that integrates academic excellence with Islamic values. All teaching staff meet or exceed established qualification requirements.

## General Qualification Requirements

### Minimum Educational Qualifications

**All Teaching Staff Must Possess:**

**Academic Degrees:**
- Bachelor's degree in relevant subject area from recognized institution
- For secondary level: Master's degree preferred
- Minimum GPA: 2.75 (on 4.0 scale) or equivalent

**Teaching Certification:**
- Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) or
- Diploma in Education (D.Ed) or
- Recognized teaching certification program
- For Islamic Studies: Alim/Dawra Hadith or equivalent Islamic scholarship

**Language Proficiency:**
- English: IELTS 6.0+ or equivalent for English medium subjects
- Bengali: Native proficiency for Bengali subjects
- Arabic: Proficiency certification for Arabic and Islamic Studies

### Experience Requirements

**Preferred Experience:**
- Minimum 2 years teaching experience
- Experience in English medium institution preferred
- Experience with Islamic school environment (preferred)

**For Leadership Positions:**
- Minimum 5 years teaching experience
- 2+ years in administrative or coordination role
- Demonstrated leadership capabilities

## Subject-Specific Qualifications

### Early Childhood Education (PlayGroup-KG)

**Required Qualifications:**
- Bachelor's degree in any discipline + Early Childhood Education (ECE) certification
- Diploma in Primary Education (preferred)
- Training in child development and psychology

**Essential Skills:**
- Understanding of early childhood development
- Patience and affection for young children
- Creative teaching methods
- Classroom management for young learners

### Primary Section (Class 1-5)

**Required Qualifications:**
- Bachelor's degree + B.Ed or teaching certification
- For Class 3-5: Subject specialization preferred
- Training in primary education methodologies

**Preferred:**
- Experience with activity-based learning
- Mathematics and Language teaching certifications
- Understanding of multiple intelligences

### Secondary Section (Class 6-9)

**English Language & Literature:**
- MA in English or BA (Honors) in English + B.Ed
- English language teaching certification (CELTA/TEFL preferred)
- Literature specialization for literature classes

**Mathematics:**
- M.Sc/B.Sc (Honors) in Mathematics + B.Ed
- Strong conceptual understanding
- Problem-solving expertise

**Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology):**
- M.Sc/B.Sc (Honors) in relevant science + B.Ed
- Laboratory training and safety certification
- Practical science teaching experience

**Information & Communication Technology (ICT):**
- B.Sc in Computer Science or relevant field
- Industry experience (preferred)
- Programming and software skills
- Educational technology training

**Bengali Language & Literature:**
- MA in Bengali or BA (Honors) + B.Ed
- Strong command of Bengali language
- Literature specialization

**Social Studies (History, Geography, Bangladesh Studies):**
- MA/BA (Honors) in relevant social science + B.Ed
- Understanding of local and global contexts
- Research and project-based learning skills

### Islamic Studies Department

**Qualifications:**

**Quranic Studies:**
- Hifz (complete memorization of Quran) preferred
- Tajweed certification
- Qirat qualification (preferred)
- Understanding of Tafsir methodology

**Hadith & Fiqh:**
- Dawra Hadith or equivalent Islamic scholarship
- Understanding of major Fiqh schools
- Ability to apply Fiqh to contemporary issues
- Arabic language proficiency

**Islamic History:**
- Alim degree or equivalent
- Knowledge of Islamic civilization and contributions
- Understanding of contemporary Islamic world

**Arabic Language:**
- Arabic language certification from recognized institution
- Fluency in reading, writing, and speaking Arabic
- Understanding of Quranic Arabic grammar

**Islamic Studies Teacher Requirements:**
- Strong personal Islamic character and practice
- Ability to teach practical application of Islamic principles
- Understanding of Islamic pedagogy
- Commitment to ongoing Islamic learning

## Administrative Staff Qualifications

### School Leadership

**Principal:**
- Master's degree in Education Administration or relevant field
- Minimum 10 years educational experience
- 5+ years in leadership position
- Educational leadership certification
- Islamic education background (preferred)

**Vice Principal:**
- Master's degree in Education or relevant field
- Minimum 7 years teaching experience
- 3+ years in administrative role
- Leadership and management training

**Academic Coordinator:**
- Master's degree in Education or subject specialization
- Minimum 5 years teaching experience
- Curriculum development training
- Teacher mentoring experience

### Support Services

**Librarian:**
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Library Science
- Digital library management training
- Information literacy certification

**Counselor:**
- Master's degree in Psychology, Counseling, or Social Work
- School counseling certification
- Understanding of child and adolescent psychology
- Islamic counseling training (preferred)

**Learning Support Specialist:**
- Special Education certification or degree
- Experience with diverse learning needs
- Training in learning disabilities and interventions
- Understanding of gifted education

**STEAM Coordinator:**
- Background in Science, Technology, or Arts education
- Project-based learning training
- Innovation and creativity workshops
- Cross-curricular integration experience

**Early Childhood:**
- Early childhood development workshops

**Islamic Studies:**
- Islamic pedagogy workshops
- Contemporary Fiqh issues
- Character education methods
- Quran teaching methodologies

## Recruitment and Verification

### Recruitment Process

**Selection Criteria:**
1. Initial qualification screening
2. Written demonstration of subject knowledge
3. Teaching demonstration (mock class)
4. Interview with administration
5. Islamic character reference check
6. Background verification

### Document Verification

**All Faculty Must Submit:**
- Original academic certificates and transcripts
- Teaching certification documentation
- Previous employment verification
- Character references (including Islamic character)
- National ID and proof of identity
- Police clearance (for child safety)

### Probationary Period

**New Teachers:**
- 6-month probationary period
- Regular observation and feedback
- Mentorship program participation
- Performance evaluation at 3 and 6 months
- Confirmation based on performance assessment

## Continuing Professional Development

### Professional Growth Requirements

**All Faculty:**
- Annual professional development plan
- Participation in school-based training
- Attendance at external workshops/conferences
- Peer observation and collaborative planning
- Action research or curriculum projects

### Leadership Development

**For Aspiring Leaders:**
- Leadership training programs
- Administrative skill development

- Project management training

### Advanced Qualifications

**Encouraged for All Staff:**
- Master's degree in relevant field
- Additional subject certifications
- Specialized training (e.g., gifted education, learning support)
- Research and publication
- Conference presentations

Smart Academy's qualification requirements align with:
- Bangladesh Ministry of Education standards
