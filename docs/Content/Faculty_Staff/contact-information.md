## Communication Guidelines

### Email Communication

**Email Format:**
- **Teachers:** [firstname].[lastname]@smartacademy.edu.bd
- **Departments:** [department]@smartacademy.edu.bd

**Email Etiquette:**
- Include student name and class/ID in subject line
- State purpose clearly and concisely
- Provide contact information
- Allow 24-48 hours for response on school days
- Follow up after 3 school days if no response

**When to Email:**
- Non-urgent questions and concerns
- Requests for appointments
- Sharing information or documents
- Follow-up to previous conversations

**When NOT to Email:**
- Emergencies (call instead)
- Same-day requests (may not be seen in time)
- Sensitive or complex issues (better to meet in person)

### Phone Communication

**Main Phone:** [Number]
**Office Hours:** Sunday-Thursday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

- Urgent matters during school hours

## Parent Communication Protocol

### Step-by-Step Process

**For Academic or Behavioral Concerns:**

1. **First:** Contact the relevant teacher
   - Email or schedule appointment
   - Discuss concern directly
   - Most issues resolved at this level

2. **Second:** If unresolved, contact Department Head or Academic Coordinator
   - For academic issues: Academic Coordinator
   - For subject-specific: Department Head

3. **Third:** If still unresolved, contact Vice Principal
   - For serious or ongoing issues
   - When previous steps haven't resolved concern

4. **Fourth:** Contact Principal
   - For very serious matters
   - When all other avenues exhausted

**For Urgent Matters:**
- Contact main office immediately
- Speak with Administrator or Vice Principal
- Principal may be contacted for urgent serious matters

### Parent-Teacher Communication

**Best Practices:**
- **Timing:** Avoid during teaching hours (8:30 AM - 2:30 PM)
- **Method:** Email to schedule, then meet in person or phone
- **Preparation:** Have specific questions or concerns clearly stated
- **Follow-up:** Send thank you email after meeting if appropriate

**Regular Communication:**
- **Monthly:** Progress updates from teachers
- **Termly:** Parent-teacher conferences
- **As needed:** For concerns or achievements
