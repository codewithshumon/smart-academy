## Communication Protocols

### Preferred Contact Methods

**For Inquiries:**
- **General Questions:** Main office in-person or phone
- **Academic Matters:** Email to relevant department
- **Student Issues:** Phone call or in-person meeting
- **Fee Payments:** In-person at accounts office
- **Emergencies:** Phone call to main office

**Email Etiquette:**
- Include student name and ID/grade
- Clearly state purpose of email
- Provide contact information
- Allow 24-48 hours for response
- Follow up if no response within 3 school days

### Meeting Protocols

**Arrival:**
- Check in at main office
- Wait in designated area
- Be on time for appointment
- Respect time limits

**During Meeting:**
- Maintain Islamic etiquette (Salaam, respect)
- Be concise and clear
- Listen actively
- Take notes if needed
- Follow Islamic guidelines for gender interaction

**After Meeting:**
- Follow through on any action items
- Send thank you if appropriate
- Provide additional information if requested

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I meet with a teacher without an appointment?**
A: Teachers are often available after school for brief conversations, but appointments are recommended to ensure availability.

**Q: What if I need to see someone urgently?**
A: For urgent matters during school hours, come to the main office or call. We will accommodate as best we can.

**Q: Are administrators available on Fridays?**
A: No, the school is closed on Fridays (Islamic weekend).

**Q: Can I pay fees after 4:00 PM?**
A: No, the accounts office closes at 3:00 PM. Please arrange to come during office hours.

**Q: How do I schedule a meeting with the Principal?**
A: Contact the main office to schedule. Principal meetings are by appointment only with advance notice.

**Q: What if I arrive late for my appointment?**
A: Your appointment may need to be rescheduled. Please arrive on time or contact if you'll be late.
