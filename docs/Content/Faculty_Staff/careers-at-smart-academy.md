## Application and Hiring Process

### How to Apply

**1. Find Opening:**
- Check current openings (see current-openings.md)
- Review position requirements carefully
- Ensure qualifications match

**2. Submit Application:**
- Follow application process (see application-process.md)
- Submit all required documents
- Complete application form

**3. Selection Process:**
- Application screening
- Written demonstration (for teaching positions)
- Teaching demonstration (for teaching positions)
- Interview with administration
- Reference and background checks

**4. Offer and Onboarding:**
- Employment offer
- Contract acceptance
- Orientation and onboarding
- Mentorship assignment

### Timeline

**Typical Hiring Timeline:**
- **Application Submission:** By deadline
- **Screening:** 1-2 weeks after deadline
- **Interviews/Demonstrations:** 2-3 weeks after screening
- **Decision:** Within 1 week of interviews
- **Onboarding:** Before academic year begins

**Peak Hiring Times:**
- **March-May:** For academic year starting in April
- **November-December:** For January positions (if applicable)
