## Faculty Credentials Summary

### Educational Qualifications

**Minimum Requirements for Smart Academy Teachers:**
- Bachelor's degree in relevant subject
- Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) or teaching certification
- For Islamic Studies: Formal Islamic education (Alim/Dawra Hadith)
- Minimum 2 years teaching experience (preferred)

### Professional Development

**All Faculty Members:**
- Complete annual professional development hours
- Participate in regular training workshops
- Engage in peer observation and mentoring
- Contribute to curriculum development

- First aid and emergency response
- Modern teaching methodologies
- Assessment and evaluation techniques
- Technology integration
