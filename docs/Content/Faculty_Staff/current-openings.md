## How to Apply

### Application Process

1. **Review Position Requirements**
   - Ensure you meet minimum qualifications
   - Understand responsibilities and requirements

2. **Prepare Application Materials**
   - Update CV/resume
   - Write cover letter explaining interest and qualifications
   - Gather required documents (see application-process.md)

3. **Submit Application**
   - Email to: careers@smartacademy.edu.bd
   - Subject line: Position Title - Your Name
   - Include all required documents

4. **Selection Process**
   - Screening of applications
   - Written test (for teaching positions)
   - Teaching demonstration (for teaching positions)
   - Interview
   - Reference checks

### Required Documents

- CV/Resume
- Cover letter
- Copies of academic certificates
- Copies of teaching certification (if applicable)
- Experience certificates (if applicable
- References (contact information for 2-3 professional references)
