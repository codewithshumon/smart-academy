## Policy Overview

### Purpose Statement

Smart Academy is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our students, parents, staff, and website users. This policy explains:

- What information we collect
- How we use that information
- How we protect your information
- Your rights regarding your information
- How to contact us about privacy matters

### Commitment

We pledge to:
- Collect only necessary information
- Use information only for stated purposes
- Protect information with appropriate security
- Respect privacy rights
- Comply with applicable laws

### Legal Framework

This policy complies with:
- Bangladesh Digital Security Act 2018
- Bangladesh Information and Communication Technology Act
- Children's protection regulations
- International best practices for data protection

## Data Protection

### Security Measures

**We Protect Information Through:**
- Secure servers
- Encryption of sensitive data
- Regular security updates
- Access controls (user authentication)
- Regular backups
- Physical security of records
- Staff training on data protection

### Access Controls

**Limited Access:**
- Only authorized staff can access sensitive information
- Role-based access (see only what needed)
- Password protection
- Session time-outs
- Secure login for parent/student portals

### Data Storage

**Information Stored:**
- On secure servers
- In locked file cabinets (paper records)
- In cloud-based systems with security
- According to retention schedules

### Retention

**Records Kept:**
- Academic records: Until student graduates + 5 years
- Health records: Until student turns 25
- Financial records: 7 years
- Employment records: Per labor law
- Website data: As needed for operations

## Information Sharing

### When Information May Be Shared

**With Consent:**
- With parent permission for specific purposes
- For educational research (anonymized)
- For school publications (with consent)

**Without Consent (when required):**
- Legal requirements
- Emergency situations
- To protect safety and welfare
- To educational authorities
- For accreditation purposes

### Third Parties

**We May Share Information With:**
- Education authorities
- Examination boards
- Government agencies (when required)
- Service providers (with data protection agreements)
- Medical professionals (with parent permission)
- Transfer schools (upon student withdrawal)

**We Do NOT Sell:**
- Personal information to anyone
- Student data for marketing purposes
- Information to third parties except as stated

## Parental Rights

### Right to Access

**Parents May Request:**
- Access to their child's educational records
- Copies of reports and documents
- Explanation of any information in records
- Amendment of incorrect information

### Right to Consent

**Parent Consent Required For:**
- Release of directory information
- Photographs used in publications
- Website publication of student work or photos
- Sharing information with outside agencies (except as required by law)
- Student participation in certain activities

### Right to Opt Out

**Parents May Decline:**
- Directory information release
- Photo/video publication
- Website publication of student information
- Participation in surveys (unless required)

### Directory Information

**Includes:** name, address, telephone number, date and place of birth, participation in activities, dates of attendance

**Used For:** school publications, directories, yearbook, announcements

**Opt Out Available:** Parents may request directory information not be shared
