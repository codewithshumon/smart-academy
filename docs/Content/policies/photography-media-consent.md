## Policy Overview

### Purpose

Smart Academy regularly takes photographs and videos of school activities and events. This policy establishes:

- When consent is required
- How images may be used
- Parent and student rights
- Islamic guidelines for images
- Procedures for obtaining consent

### Our Commitment

We pledge to:
- Obtain appropriate consent before use
- Use images respectfully
- Protect student privacy
- Follow Islamic guidelines
- Allow parents to opt-out
- Use images for educational and promotional purposes only

## Consent Requirements

### Primary Consent

**Required For:**
- Publication of student photos on website
- Use in marketing materials
- Use in social media
- Publication in news media
- Any public use of identifiable images

**Initial Consent Obtained Through:**
- Admission registration form
- Annual media consent form
- Specific event consent when needed

### Annual Media Consent Form

**Part of Enrollment Package:**
- Parents indicate consent preferences
- Separate consent for different uses
- Can be updated anytime
- Reviewed and signed annually

### Specific Event Consent

**Required For:**
- Public performances where media may be present
- Off-campus activities where photos may be taken
- Media events or press coverage
- Third-party photography

### Student Self-Generated Content

**Student-Created Media:**
- Student work (photos, videos) shared with consent
- Student presentations recorded with consent
- Student work displayed with consent

## Permitted Uses

### School Publications

**With Consent, Photos May Be Used In:**
- School website
- School social media accounts
- School newsletters
- Annual reports
- Brochures and prospectuses
- Yearbook
- Advertisements (with consent)

### Educational Purposes

**Allowed For:**
- Classroom activities
- Student projects
- Educational displays
- Assessment portfolios
- Progress documentation
- Student recognition

### News Media

**With Specific Consent:**
- Interview with student
- Photographs featuring student
- Video of student activities
- Coverage of special achievements

**Process:**
- Parent permission obtained in advance
- Media representative escorted
- Limited to approved areas
- Student not identified by full name without consent

### School Events

**At Public Events:**
- Parents and visitors may take photos/videos
- Must be for personal use only
- Must not be posted online without consent
- Must not be used commercially
- Must respect privacy of other students

---

## Prohibited Uses

### Never Permitted

**Without Explicit Consent:**
- Sale of student images
- Use in political campaigns
- Use in advertisements for third-party products
- Use in adult content
- Use in contexts that would embarrass or harm student
- Sale to stock photo agencies

### Prohibited Contexts

**We Will Not Use Photos In:**
- Compromising or embarrassing situations
- Inappropriate contexts
- Ways that could identify student's location for safety reasons
- Contexts inconsistent with Islamic values

### Social Media by Others

**Parents and Visitors Must Not:**
- Post photos of other students without consent
- Tag students other than their own children
- Post photos in ways that compromise safety
- Share photos publicly without permission

---

## Islamic Considerations

### Modesty and Privacy

**In Accordance With Islamic Values:**
- Images used modestly and respectfully
- No images that contradict Islamic teachings
- Respect for hijab and modest dress
- No inappropriate poses or contexts
- Privacy and dignity maintained

### Image Creation

**Guidelines:**
- Students encouraged to maintain Islamic dress standards
- Photos taken in appropriate contexts
- No photography in prayer areas (except during events)
- No photography in bathroom areas
- Respect for privacy during physical activities

### Gender Considerations

**For Female Students:**
- Extra care for privacy and modesty
- No photos that compromise hijab/privacy
- Female staff for female student activities when appropriate
- Special care with media at events

## Consent Options

### Opt-In Choices

**Parents May Choose To:**
- Allow all media use
- Allow website use only
- Allow print publications only
- Allow internal use only
- Decline all media use

### Partial Consent

**May Allow:**
- Group photos (no individual identification)
- Photos without name attached
- Photos of achievements without personal details
- Back-of-head or silhouette images
- Photos of work rather than student

### Withdrawing Consent

**Parents May:**
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Request removal of specific images
- Update consent preferences annually
- Opt-out of specific uses

**Process:**
- Written request to administration
- Removal within 10 business days
- Removal from future use only
- Existing print/distributed media cannot be recalled

## Student Privacy Rights

### Students' Right to Privacy

**Older Students (Class 6-9):**
- May have input on consent decisions
- May request photos not be taken
- May request removal of specific images
- May decline to be interviewed

### Opting Out

**Student May Request:**
- Not to be photographed for specific activities
- Not to be in individual photos
- Not to be identified by name in publications
- Their work not be shared publicly

## Staff Responsibilities

### All Staff Must:**
- Follow this policy strictly
- Respect parent preferences
- Check consent list before using images
- Protect student privacy
- Report any violations
- Use school equipment only for authorized purposes

### Photography by Staff

**Guidelines:**
- Use school camera/phone only when authorized
- Follow consent preferences
- Take photos in appropriate settings
- Store images securely
- Delete images when no longer needed
- Never share photos on personal social media
