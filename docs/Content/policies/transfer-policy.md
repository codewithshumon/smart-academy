## Policy Overview

### Purpose

This policy establishes procedures for:
- Students transferring from Smart Academy
- Students transferring to Smart Academy
- Credit recognition and transfer
- Academic placement of transfer students
- Record transfer between schools

### Transfer Philosophy

Smart Academy recognizes that:
- Families may need to move for various reasons
- Educational continuity is important
- Smooth transitions benefit students
- Proper documentation protects student interests

### Scope

This policy applies to:
- All transfers between grades
- Mid-year transfers
- Transfers at natural break points
- Both domestic and international transfers

## Transfer Out of Smart Academy

### Initiating Transfer

**Parent Should:**
1. Notify school in writing as early as possible
2. Provide reason for transfer (optional but helpful)
3. Provide new school information
4. Request record transfer

### Notice Period

**Preferred Notice:**
- **30 days notice** before withdrawal
- Allows for proper documentation
- Minimizes academic disruption
- Facilitates proper clearance

### Documentation Provided

**School Will Provide:**
1. **Transfer Certificate**
   - Dates of attendance
   - Grade completed or in progress
   - Character certificate if requested
   - School seal and signature

2. **Academic Records**
   - Report cards/transcripts
   - Attendance record
   - Assessment results
   - Standardized test scores

3. **Health Records**
   - Immunization records
   - Health information
   - Vision/hearing screening results
   - Special health needs documentation

4. **Additional Documents (if requested)**
   - Course descriptions
   - Syllabi information
   - Credit information
   - Extracurricular participation record

### Clearance Process

**Before Transfer Certificate Issued:**
- All school fees paid
- Library books returned
- School property returned
- No outstanding dues
- Hold on records if fees owed

### Timing of Transfer

**Recommended Times:**
- End of academic year (December)
- End of term (May)
- Natural breaks in academic calendar

**Mid-Term Transfer:**
- Academic credit may be affected
- Assessment for placement needed at new school
- Parent responsible for academic consequences

## Transfer Into Smart Academy

### Application Process

**Transfer Students Follow:**
- Standard admission process
- Additional documentation from previous school
- Assessment for placement
- Interview with administration

### Required Documents

**In Addition to Admission Documents:**

1. **Transfer Certificate**
   - From previous school
   - Shows last grade completed
   - Shows current grade if mid-year
   - Signed and sealed by previous school

2. **Academic Transcripts**
   - Report cards for current year
   - Report cards for previous year
   - Standardized test scores
   - Attendance record

3. **Health Records**
   - Immunization records
   - Health information
   - Special needs documentation if applicable

4. **Character Certificate** (if available)
   - From previous school
   - Behavior and conduct record

### Placement Assessment

**Purpose:**
- Determine appropriate grade placement
- Identify any academic gaps
- Identify any needs for support
- Ensure student can succeed at Smart Academy

**Assessment Includes:**
- English language skills
- Mathematics skills
- Bengali language skills (if applicable)
- Arabic language skills (for placement)
- Islamic studies knowledge
- General academic abilities

### Grade Placement

**General Policy:**
- Place according to previous grade completed
- Age-appropriate placement considered
- Assessment results considered
- Academic needs considered
- Parent input considered

**Placement Options:**
- **Grade level placement** (most common)
- **Conditional placement** (with support)
- **Trial placement** (to assess fit)
- **Grade acceleration** (rare, requires assessment)

### Probationary Admission

**May Be Applied When:**
- Significant gaps in knowledge
- Uncertainty about ability to succeed
- Behavioral concerns
- Previous academic difficulties
- Need for observation period

**Conditions:**
- Academic support required
- Regular progress monitoring
- Specific timeline for review
- Final placement decision at review

## Credit Transfer

### Credit Recognition

**For Secondary Students (Class 6-9):**

**Smart Academy Recognizes:**
- Credits from recognized schools
- Subjects with comparable curriculum
- Adequate instruction time
- Satisfactory completion

**Evaluation Criteria:**
1. **School Recognition**
   - Government recognized
   - International school accreditation
   - Education board affiliation

2. **Subject Content**
   - Comparable curriculum
   - Similar learning outcomes
   - Adequate instructional time

3. **Student Performance**
   - Passing grade achieved
   - Satisfactory mastery demonstrated
   - Assessment if needed

### Credit Transfer Process

**For Credit to Be Transferred:**
1. Official transcript submitted
2. Course description provided (if needed)
3. Credit evaluation completed
4. Parent informed of decision
5. Student record updated

### Limitations on Transfer

**May Require Retaking if:**
- Significant curriculum differences
- Inadequate preparation
- Failing grade
- Incomplete course

**Partial Credit:**
- May be awarded for partially completed courses
- Assessment for mastery
- Additional work may be required

## Academic Placement

### Placement Testing

**Purpose:**
- Determine appropriate grade level
- Identify academic strengths and weaknesses
- Determine need for support services
- Plan for any remediation needed

**Testing May Include:**
- English placement test
- Mathematics placement test
- Bengali language test
- Arabic language placement (for Islamic studies)
- General achievement test

### Grade-Level Placement

**Factors Considered:**
- Age of student
- Previous grade completed
- Assessment results
- Previous school records
- Parent recommendation
- Student maturity
- Special needs if applicable

### Special Considerations

**For Students with Special Needs:**
- Individualized assessment
- Review of existing IEP if available
- Meeting with special needs coordinator
- Development of support plan
- Placement determined by team

**For English Language Learners:**
- English proficiency assessed
- Language support determined
- Grade placement may differ by subject
- Additional support provided

**For Gifted Students:**
- Assessment may indicate higher grade level
- Consideration of social-emotional needs
- Subject acceleration considered
- Enrichment provided

## Record Transfer

### Sending Records

**Smart Academy Will Forward:**
- All academic records
- Health records
- Attendance records
- Special education records if applicable
- Disciplinary records if requested

**To:**
- Receiving school
- With parent/guardian permission
- Upon official request
- In timely manner

### Receiving Records

**Smart Academy Will:**
- Request records from previous school
- Maintain records in student file
- Use records for placement
- Keep records confidential
- Transfer records when student leaves

### Records Security

**All Records:**
- Maintained securely
- Confidentiality maintained
- Only accessed by authorized staff
- Retained according to policy
- Destroyed when no longer needed

## Inter-School Transfers

### Domestic Transfers

**Within Bangladesh:**
- Transfer certificate required
- Education Board notification if required
- Previous school records required
- Placement testing if grade standards vary

### International Transfers

**From Abroad:**
- Visa documentation required
- Passport copy required
- Records translated if needed
- Equivalency determination
- Placement testing as needed

**To Abroad:**
- Letter for student visa if required
- Complete academic records
- Character certificate if requested
- Immunization records
- School profile if requested

## Withdrawal from Transfer

### Trial Period

**For Transfer Students:**
- First term may be trial period
- Progress monitored closely
- Support provided as needed
- Review at end of trial period

**If Not Successful:**
- Meeting with parents
- Options discussed
- Additional support provided
- Alternative placement considered

### Support for Transfer Students

**School Provides:**
- Orientation program
- Peer buddy system
- Academic support as needed
- English language support if needed
- Regular check-ins
- Counseling if needed
