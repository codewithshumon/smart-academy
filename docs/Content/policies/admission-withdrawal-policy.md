## Policy Overview

### Purpose

This policy establishes clear procedures for:
- Admitting new students to Smart Academy
- Ensuring fair and equitable access
- Maintaining appropriate class sizes
- Planning for adequate resources
- Managing student withdrawals and transfers

### Admission Philosophy

Smart Academy welcomes students who:
- Are committed to Islamic education
- Value academic excellence
- Respect our school's mission and values
- Will benefit from our program
- Will contribute positively to our community

### Non-Discrimination

Smart Academy admits students of any:
- Race, color, or ethnicity
- National origin
- Socioeconomic status (with scholarship support)
- Background or previous school

**While maintaining Islamic values and religious instruction.**

## Admission Process

### Step 1: Inquiry

**Parent Action:**
- Submit admission inquiry
- Receive information package
- Visit school website
- Attend open house if available

### Step 2: Application

**Parent Submits:**
- Completed application form
- Application fee (non-refundable)
- Required documents (see below)
- Photographs of student

### Step 3: Assessment

**Student Completes:**
- Age-appropriate assessment
- PlayGroup-KG: Developmental observation
- Class 1-5: Academic assessment
- Class 6-9: Placement testing

### Step 4: Interview

**Meeting Includes:**
- Parent(s) and student
- Principal or Vice Principal
- Discussion of:
  - Family expectations
  - School policies
  - Student's educational goals
  - Islamic commitment

### Step 5: Decision

**Notification:**
- Parents notified of decision
- Typically within 7 days of completion of all steps
- Written notification provided
- Deposit required to secure seat

### Step 6: Enrollment

**To Complete Enrollment:**
- Pay admission and security deposit fees
- Submit all remaining documents
- Complete enrollment forms
- Receive admission packet
- Attend orientation

## Admission Testing and Interview

### Early Childhood (PlayGroup - KG)

**Assessment Format:**
- Play-based observation
- Developmental screening
- Parent interview
- No formal testing

**Observed Areas:**
- Social skills
- Language development
- Motor skills
- Separation from parent
- Following directions

### Primary (Class 1-5)

**Assessment Includes:**
- English and Bengali language skills
- Mathematics skills
- Reading readiness (Class 1)
- General knowledge
- Writing sample

**Format:**
- Age-appropriate testing
- Approximately 1 hour
- Parent interview separate

### Secondary (Class 6-9)

**Assessment Includes:**
- English (reading, writing, grammar)
- Mathematics (computation, concepts, problem-solving)
- Science (basic concepts)
- Bengali language skills
- General knowledge

**Format:**
- Written assessment
- Approximately 2 hours
- Interview with Principal

### Special Considerations

**For Students with Special Needs:**
- Assessment accommodations provided
- Individualized evaluation
- Meeting with special needs coordinator
- Discussion of support options

## Admission Decision

### Decision Criteria

**Admission Based On:**
- Assessment results
- Previous school records
- Interview observations
- Space availability
- Commitment to school's values
- Date of application (when qualified)

### Decision Categories

**Admission Offered:**
- Student meets all criteria
- Space available in grade
- Family accepts school policies

**Wait List:**
- Student qualified but no space
- Will be contacted if space opens
- No guarantee of placement

**Provisional Admission:**
- Student admitted on probationary status
- Specific conditions must be met
- Review after specified period
- May be for academic or behavioral reasons

**Admission Denied:**
- Student does not meet criteria
- Space not available and wait list full
- School cannot meet student's needs
- Family unwilling to comply with policies

### Priority Admissions

**Priority Given To:**
- Siblings of current students
- Children of staff (if qualified)
- Students transferring from similar Islamic schools
- Early applications (when qualified)

**No Discrimination Based On:**
- Ethnicity, race, or color
- National origin
- Socioeconomic status (within scholarship availability)

## Withdrawal Procedures

### Voluntary Withdrawal

**Parent Must:**
1. Notify school in writing of intention to withdraw
2. Provide reason for withdrawal (optional but helpful)
3. Complete withdrawal form
4. Obtain clearance from:
   - Library (no books owed)
   - Laboratory (if applicable)
   - Finance (no dues)
   - Class teacher
5. Return school property
6. Pay any outstanding fees
7. Collect transfer certificate

**Notice Period:**
- **30 days notice** preferred
- Notice must be given in writing
- Fees charged up to notice period
- See Refund Policy for fee details

### Withdrawal Timing

**Best Times to Withdraw:**
- End of academic year (December)
- End of Term 1 (May)
- Between grades

**Challenging Times:**
- During term (academic impact)
- Before examinations

### Documents Provided

**Upon Withdrawal, School Provides:**
- Transfer certificate
- Progress report to date
- Immunization record
- Report cards issued to date
- School record (upon request to new school)

### Outstanding Obligations

**Before Withdrawal Complete:**
- All fees paid or arrangements made
- School property returned
- Library books returned
- Any damages paid for
- Athletic equipment returned

## Transfer Procedures

### Transfer to Another School

**Student Transferring:**
- Parent follows withdrawal procedures
- School provides transfer certificate
- School provides academic records
- School provides health records
- Forwarding school contacted if requested

### Transfer to Smart Academy

**Mid-Year Admission:**
- Space permitting
- Assessment for placement
- Records from previous school required
- Trial period may be applied

**Credit Transfer:**
- Previous school credits evaluated
- Placement determined by assessment and records
- See Credit Transfer Policy for details

### Transfer Within Bangladesh

**Requirements:**
- Transfer certificate from previous school
- Acknowledgment from Education Board if required
- Previous academic records
- Immunization records

### Transfer from Abroad

**Additional Requirements:**
- Valid visa or residency documentation
- Previous school records translated if needed
- Equivalency of education evaluated
- Placement determined by assessment
