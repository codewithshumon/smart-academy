## Policy Overview

### Purpose

This policy establishes clear guidelines for:
- Fee refunds upon student withdrawal
- Cancellation of enrollment
- Refund of advance payments
- Handling of special circumstances
- Fair and transparent financial practices

### Principles

**Our Approach:**

### Scope

This policy applies to:
- All tuition fees

- Security deposits

## Tuition Refunds

### Withdrawal Before Academic Year Begins

**Before January 1st:**
- Full refund of tuition paid
- 100% of tuition refunded
- Admission fee NOT refunded
- Security deposit refunded

**January 1st - Start of Classes:**
- 90% of tuition refunded
- 10% retained as administrative fee
- Admission fee NOT refunded
- Security deposit refunded

### Withdrawal During Academic Year

**Prorated Refund Schedule:**

**January-February (First 2 months):**
- 80% of remaining tuition refunded
- 20% retained as penalty

**March-April (Months 3-4):**
- 60% of remaining tuition refunded
- 40% retained

**May-June (Months 5-6):**
- 40% of remaining tuition refunded
- 60% retained

**July-December (Months 7-12):**
- No refund of tuition
- Full year tuition still due

**Calculation Method:**
```
Refund = (Monthly tuition × months remaining) × Refund percentage
```

**Example:**
- Annual tuition: 38,000 BDT
- Monthly equivalent: 3,167 BDT
- Withdrawal April 15 (after 3.5 months)
- Months remaining: 8.5
- Refund percentage: 60% (March-April period)
- Calculation: (3,167 × 8.5) × 0.60 = 16,151 BDT refund

### Refund Conditions

**Requirements for Refund:**
- Written notice of withdrawal
- Clearance from library (no books owed)
- Clearance from transport (no dues)
- No outstanding fees
- Return of school property (if applicable)

## Fee Cancellations

### Cancellation Before Enrollment

**Before Admission Confirmed:**
- Application fee NOT refunded (1,500 BDT)
- All other fees refunded 100%

**After Admission Confirmed, Before Classes Begin:**
- Admission fee NOT refunded (10,000 BDT)
- Security deposit refunded
- 50% of tuition refunded if before January 1st
- 25% of tuition refunded if January 1st - start of classes

### Cancellation of Special Programs

**Before Program Begins:**
- Full refund of program fee

**After Program Begins:**
- Prorated refund based on sessions completed
- 25% cancellation fee applied

## Refund Process

### Initiating Refund

**Steps:**
1. Submit written notice of withdrawal
2. Complete withdrawal form
3. Obtain clearances from:
   - Library
   - Transport
   - Finance office
   - Class teacher
4. Return school property
5. Pay any outstanding dues
6. Submit refund request

### Processing Time

**Standard Processing:**
- **Tuition refunds:** 20-30 working days
- **Security deposit:** 30 working days
- **Overpayments:** 10-15 working days

**May Take Longer If:**
- Outstanding dues to be calculated
- Property to be returned/assessed
- Complex circumstances
- Missing documentation

### Receiving Refund

**Options:**
- **Check:** Mailed to parent address
- **Bank Transfer:** To designated account
- **Cash:** Can be collected at finance office

**Information Needed:**
- Bank account details (for transfer)
- Mailing address (for check)
- Identification when collecting cash

## Special Circumstances

### Medical Withdrawal

**If Student Withdraws for Medical Reasons:**
- Full prorated refund of tuition
- Medical certificate required
- Doctor's recommendation for withdrawal
- Reviewed on case-by-case basis
- Admission fee still not refunded

### Relocation

**If Family Moves Away:**
- Prorated refund as above
- Proof of relocation required
- Transfer certificate needed
- Admission fee still not refunded

### Family Crisis

**For Serious Family Circumstances:**
- Considered on case-by-case basis
- Documentation of crisis
- May result in additional consideration
- Reviewed by administration

### School Cancellation

**If School Cancels Program:**
- Full refund of all fees
- Including admission fee
- Including security deposit
- Notification provided as early as possible

## Security Deposit

### Refund of Security Deposit

**Security Deposit (5,000 BDT) Refunded Upon:**
- Graduation from Smart Academy
- Withdrawal with proper notice
- Transfer to another school
- Completion of education

**Security Deposit Forfeited If:**
- Student expelled for serious disciplinary reasons
- Outstanding dues not cleared
- School property damaged or lost
- Proper withdrawal procedures not followed

### Deductions

**May Be Deducted From Security Deposit:**
- Outstanding tuition fees
- Lost or damaged library books
- Lost or damaged school property
- Unpaid additional fees
- Other charges as applicable

## Non-Refundable Items

### Completely Non-Refundable

**Admission Fee (10,000 BDT):**
- Non-refundable under all circumstances
- Covers administrative processing costs
- One-time fee paid at admission

**Application Fee (1,500 BDT):**
- Non-refundable under all circumstances
- Covers application processing

### Partially Non-Refundable

**Additional Fees:**
- **Library fee:** Prorated 50% refund if withdrawal in first term
- **Laboratory fee:** No refund after first month
- **Computer lab fee:** Prorated 50% refund if withdrawal in first term
- **Sports fee:** No refund after first month
- **Activity fee:** No refund after first month

## Fee Adjustments

### Overpayment Refunds

**If You Overpay:**
- Credit maintained for future payments
- Or refund issued upon request
- No charge for overpayment refund

### Duplicate Payments

**Accidental Double Payment:**
- Full refund of duplicate payment
- Processed within 10 working days
- No fees charged

### Advance Payment Refunds

**If Paying in Advance for Multiple Years:**
- Full refund if student withdraws
- Prorated as per tuition refund policy
- No penalty for advance payment

### Scholarship Adjustments

**If Scholarship Awarded After Payment:**
- Credit applied to future fees
- Or refund issued if preference
- Adjusted within 30 days of award

## Transportation Fees

### Withdrawal from Transport Service

**Notice Required:**
- One month notice preferred
- Prorated refund given for unused portion

**No Refund For:**
- Current month if notice given mid-month
- Months already completed

### Refund Process

**Transport Refunds:**
- Processed separately from tuition refunds
- May take additional time
- Coordinate with transport office

## Extracurricular Activity Fees

### Activity-Specific Fees

**Before Activity Begins:**
- Full refund

**After Activity Begins:**
- No refund unless special circumstances
- Pro-rated if program cancelled
- Considered case-by-case

### Competition Fees

**For External Competitions:**
- May include non-refundable portions
- Travel, accommodation fees
- Non-refundable once booked

## Special Program Fees

### Summer School

**Withdrawal Before Start:**
- Full refund less 10% administrative fee

**Withdrawal After Start:**
- Prorated refund based on sessions attended
- 25% cancellation fee
- No refund after 50% completion

### Tutorial Programs

**Before Program Begins:**
- Full refund less 10% administrative fee

**After Program Begins:**
- Prorated refund based on sessions completed
- May be non-refundable for intensive programs
