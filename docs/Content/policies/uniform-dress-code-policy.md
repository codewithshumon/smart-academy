## Policy Overview

### Purpose

Smart Academy's uniform policy promotes:
- **Islamic Modesty (Haya)**: Appropriate dress for all students
- **Equality**: No visible economic differences
- **School Identity**: Pride and belonging
- **Practicality**: Simplified morning routine
- **Focus**: Learning, not appearance

### Philosophy

**We Believe That Uniform:**
- Creates sense of belonging and equality
- Reduces focus on clothing and appearance
- Promotes school pride
- Prepares students for professional dress expectations
- Supports Islamic modesty principles

## Islamic Foundation

### Modesty Requirements

**Quranic Guidance:**
> "Tell the believing men to lower their gaze and guard their modesty. That is purer for them." (Quran 24:30)

> "And tell the believing women to lower their gaze and guard their modesty..." (Quran 24:31)

### Islamic Dress Principles

**For Boys:**
- Cover awrah (from navel to knee)
- Wear loose, non-transparent clothing
- Avoid tight or revealing clothing
- Maintain neat, clean appearance

**For Girls:**
- Cover entire body except face and hands
- Wear loose, non-transparent clothing
- Hijab/head covering (required Class 3+)
- Avoid tight or form-fitting clothing
- Avoid excessive adornment

## Uniform Requirements

### Early Childhood (PlayGroup - KG)

#### PlayGroup & Nursery

**Purpose:** Comfort, ease of movement, independence

**Boys:**
- Colored polo shirt with school logo (light blue, yellow, or green)
- Navy blue shorts or elastic-waist trousers
- Comfortable closed-toe shoes (velcro preferred)
- White socks

**Girls:**
- Colored polo shirt with school logo (light blue, pink, or yellow)
- Navy blue shorts, leggings, or easy salwar-kameez
- Comfortable closed-toe shoes (velcro preferred)
- White socks

**Notes:**
- Colors may be rotated daily
- Easy for children to manage independently
- Comfortable for active play

#### Kindergarten

**Boys:**
- White polo shirt with school logo
- Navy blue elastic-waist shorts or trousers
- Black sneakers or shoes
- White socks

**Girls:**
- Simple navy blue kameez with white trim
- White salwar (comfortable elastic waist)
- White scarf/dupatta (optional, encouraged)
- Black sneakers or shoes
- White socks

### Primary Education (Class 1-5)

#### Class 1-2

**Boys:**
- White button-down shirt or polo shirt with school logo
- Navy blue trousers (short or long depending on season)
- Black belt
- Black shoes
- White socks

**Girls:**
- Navy blue kameez with white trim
- White salwar/trousers
- White or navy scarf/dupatta
- Black shoes
- White socks

#### Class 3-5

**Boys:**
- White button-down shirt with school logo
- Navy blue trousers (long only)
- School tie (navy with white stripes)
- Black belt
- Black dress shoes
- White socks

**Girls:**
- Navy blue kameez with white embroidery
- White salwar
- White scarf/dupatta (required)
- Black shoes
- White socks

**Hijab Policy (Class 3+):**
- Hijab/scarf is required during school hours
- Should be white, navy, or black
- Should cover hair and neck adequately

### Secondary Education (Class 6-9)

**Boys:**
- White button-down shirt with school logo
- Navy blue formal trousers
- School tie (navy with school emblem)
- Black belt
- Black formal shoes
- White socks

**Summer Option (April-October):**
- White short-sleeve shirt allowed
- Ties optional during hottest months

**Girls:**
- Navy blue kameez (knee-length or longer)
- White salwar (loose, modest)
- White or navy scarf/hijab (required)
- Black shoes (modest, closed-toe)
- White socks

**Hijab Requirements:**
- Must cover hair completely
- White, navy, or black only
- Should not be transparent
- Safety modifications for labs/PE as needed

## Grooming Standards

### Hair

**Boys:**
- Clean, neatly groomed
- Trimmed regularly
- Cut in simple, modest style
- Not extending below collar
- No extreme styles or colors
- No artificial hair dye

**Girls:**
- Clean, neatly groomed
- Tied back neatly if long
- Simple hair accessories (navy, white, black)
- No extreme styles or colors
- No artificial hair dye

### Personal Hygiene

**Daily Practices:**
- Regular bathing/showers
- Use of deodorant/antiperspirant
- Clean nails (short and simple)
- Brushed teeth
- Clean, pressed uniform
- Good general hygiene

### Face and Body

**Makeup Policy:**
- **PlayGroup-Class 8**: No makeup allowed
- **Class 9**: Minimal, natural makeup permitted (light foundation only)
- No eye makeup (eyeliner, mascara, eye shadow)
- No lipstick or colored lip gloss (clear gloss allowed)
- No fake nails or nail polish (clear only)

**Jewelry Policy:**
- **Allowed**: Simple wrist watch (no smart watches)
- **Girls**: Small stud earrings (one per ear, simple design)
- **Not Allowed**: Multiple earrings, hoops, dangling earrings
- **Not Allowed**: Necklaces, bracelets, rings (except simple band)
- **Not Allowed**: Anklets, toe rings
- **No visible piercings** other than earlobes

## Dress Down Days

### Casual Dress Days

**Occasional Non-Uniform Days:**
- Announced in advance
- Purpose: Special events, rewards, fundraising
- Still must follow Islamic modesty guidelines

**Guidelines for Casual Dress:**
- Modest tops covering shoulders and chest
- Long bottoms (jeans, trousers, long skirts)
- No tight or revealing clothing
- No offensive images or text on clothing
- Closed-toe shoes
- Hijab still required for Muslim girls

**Not Allowed Even on Casual Days:**
- Shorts, short skirts, or sleeveless tops
- Transparent clothing
- Tight or form-fitting clothing
- Clothing with inappropriate messages
- Excessive jewelry or makeup

## Enforcement

### Daily Checks

**Morning Arrival:**
- Homeroom teachers check uniform compliance
- Students with issues noted and addressed
- Minor problems corrected quickly
- Parents contacted for repeated issues

### Levels of Non-Compliance

**Minor Issues:**
- Untucked shirt
- Missing belt
- Wrong color socks
- Shoes not properly tied
- Jewelry violations

**Response:**
- First offense: Verbal warning, immediate correction
- Second offense: Note home to parents
- Third offense: Parent contact, warning letter
- Continued: Office referral, disciplinary action

**Major Issues:**
- Missing significant uniform items
- Non-uniform clothing without permission
- Repeated violations after warnings
- Extreme non-compliance
