## Policy Overview

### Purpose

Smart Academy welcomes visitors while maintaining a safe, secure learning environment. This policy ensures:

- **Safety**: Protection of students and staff
- **Security**: Controlled access to campus
- **Welcome**: Appropriate hospitality for guests
- **Accountability**: All visitors identified and tracked
- **Minimal Disruption**: Learning environment protected

### Philosophy

We balance:
- **Openness** with security
- **Hospitality** with safety
- **Community engagement** with student protection
- **Accessibility** with supervision

### Scope

This policy applies to:
- All visitors to campus
- All parents during school hours
- All vendors and contractors
- All external guests and speakers

## Visitor Categories

### Category 1: Parents/Guardians

**Definition:** Parents or legal guardians of current students

**Access:**
- May visit for specific purposes
- Must follow visitor procedures
- May have limited classroom access
- Need advance appointment for classroom visits

### Category 2: Family Members

**Definition:** Grandparents, siblings, other relatives

**Access:**
- With parent permission or accompaniment
- Must follow visitor procedures
- Limited to specific areas

### Category 3: Official Visitors

**Definition:** Education officials, inspectors, government officials

**Access:**
- Arranged in advance
- Hosted by administration
- Given appropriate access
- May require proof of identity

### Category 4: Vendors and Contractors

**Definition:** Delivery persons, repair workers, maintenance staff

**Access:**
- Access to specific areas only
- Supervised by staff
- Scheduled when possible
- Sign in/out required

### Category 5: Guests and Speakers

**Definition:** Assembly speakers, special guests, volunteers

**Access:**
- Arranged in advance
- Hosted by staff
- Given specific access based on purpose
- Supervised during visit

## Visitor Procedures

### Arrival

**All Visitors Must:**
1. Enter through **MAIN ENTRANCE ONLY**
2. Proceed directly to **MAIN OFFICE**
3. **SIGN IN** at reception
4. **SHOW PHOTO IDENTIFICATION**
5. State purpose of visit
6. Person being visited notified
7. **VISITOR BADGE** issued
8. Log entry created

### Sign-In Process

**Information Required:**
- Full name
- Photo ID
- Purpose of visit
- Person/department visiting
- Time in
- Contact phone number
- Relationship to school (if any)

### Visitor Badge

**Badge Must:**
- Be worn visibly at all times
- Be returned to office before leaving
- Be surrendered upon request
- Not be transferred to another person

### Departure

**Before Leaving, Visitors Must:**
1. Return to main office
2. **SIGN OUT** in visitor log
3. Return visitor badge
4. Collect any belongings left with office
5. Exit through main entrance

### Emergency Procedures

**If Visitor Refuses to Comply:**
1. Staff will request compliance
2. Administration will be notified
3. Security measures activated if needed
4. Law enforcement may be contacted

## Visitor Guidelines

### Expected Behavior

**All Visitors Must:**
- Follow school policies and rules
- Dress modestly and appropriately
- Respect Islamic environment
- Speak respectfully to all
- Not interrupt learning
- Follow staff directions
- Stay in authorized areas
- Maintain confidentiality

### Photography and Recording

**Generally Not Permitted:**
- Photos or videos of students
- Recording of classes or activities
- Audio recording of conversations
- **EXCEPTION:** With explicit permission from administration and parent consent

**May Be Permitted For:**
- School events where parents are welcome
- Your own child's activities (with permission)
- Official school photography with consent
- Specific authorized purposes

### Confidentiality

**Visitors Must:**
- Maintain confidentiality of all student information
- Not discuss students outside school
- Not share photos on social media
- Respect privacy of all community members

### Smoking, Tobacco, and Drugs

**Prohibited on Campus:**
- Smoking of any kind
- Vaping or e-cigarettes
- Tobacco products
- Alcohol or drugs
- Violations reported to administration

## Restricted Areas

### Areas Requiring Special Permission

**Limited/No Access:**
- **Classrooms** (during instruction) — by appointment only
- **Restroom facilities** (student) — use visitor restrooms
- **Staff room** — by invitation only
- **Kitchen** — authorized persons only
- **Storage areas** — authorized persons only
- **Playground/recess** — by permission only
- **Prayer areas** — Muslim visitors may join prayers

### Student Areas

**General Rule:**
- Visitors do not enter student areas unaccompanied
- Students do not leave class for visitors unless emergency
- Visitors wait in office or designated waiting area
- Student retrieved by staff if meeting with visitor

### Off-Limits Areas

**Completely Off-Limits:**
- Any area deemed unsafe
- Areas under construction
- Confidential record storage
- Financial areas
- Staff personal belongings

## Security Measures

### Access Control

**Main Entrance:**
- Primary access point for all visitors
- Monitored by office staff
- Video monitoring may be in use
- buzzer entry system

**Other Entrances:**
- Kept locked during school hours
- Emergency exit only
- Alarmed during certain hours
- Staff access only

### Visitor Screening

**Office Staff Will:**
- Verify identity of all visitors
- Confirm purpose of visit
- Contact person being visited
- Issue visitor badge
- Maintain visitor log
- Monitor visitor on campus if needed

### Unannounced Visitors

**Handling:**
- Purpose determined
- Appropriateness assessed
- Access granted or denied
- Appointment may be required
- Alternative arrangements offered

## Parent Visitors

### Scheduled Visits

**Parents May Schedule:**
- Parent-teacher conferences
- Classroom observations (with prior approval)
- Volunteering opportunities
- School events
- Meetings with administration

### Unscheduled Visits

**Parents Wishing to Visit:**
- May drop off items (lunch, homework, etc.)
- May leave message for child
- May wait for child at dismissal
- **NOT** permitted to go to classroom without appointment
- **NOT** permitted to interrupt instruction

### Conferences and Meetings

**Scheduled Meetings:**
- Arranged in advance
- mutually convenient time
- Specific purpose identified
- Limited to scheduled time
- Professional and respectful

### Volunteering

**Parent Volunteers:**
- Complete volunteer process
- Sign in as volunteer
- Wear volunteer badge
- Follow staff directions
- Maintain confidentiality
- Complete required training if applicable

### Cafeteria Visits

**Parents May:**
- Have lunch with child on special occasions
- Arrange with teacher in advance
- Follow cafeteria rules
- Not visit unannounced

## Vendor and Contractor Access

### Delivery Personnel

**Procedures:**
- Enter through main entrance
- Sign in at receiving area
- Deliver to designated area
- Exit immediately after delivery
- Short visit, minimal disruption

### Maintenance and Repair

**Contractors Must:**
- Be scheduled in advance when possible
- Sign in at main office
- Show identification
- Be escorted to work area
- Stay in authorized area only
- Sign out when leaving

### Long-Term Contractors

**Extended Access:**
- Background check may be required
- Photo ID badge issued
- Regular sign-in/sign-out
- Supervised by designated staff
- Access limited to work areas

## Unwelcome Visitors

### Definitions

**Unwelcome Visitors:**
- Persons on school's banned list
- Non-custodial parents without permission
- Persons with restraining orders
- Persons behaving inappropriately
- Persons under the influence of drugs/alcohol
- Persons posing safety risk

### Non-Custodial Parents

**Procedures:**
- School follows court orders and custody agreements
- Both parents may have rights unless restricted
- Legal documentation required for restrictions
- School protects child's safety and wellbeing
- Administration determines access based on legal documents

### Banned Individuals

**Process:**
- Individuals may be banned by administration
- Legal grounds required
- Written notice provided
- Appeal process available
- Cooperation with law enforcement

### Trespassing

**If Unauthorized Person Refuses to Leave:**
1. Staff will request they leave
2. Administration will be notified
3. Local law enforcement may be called
4. Students secured if needed
5. Parents notified if appropriate

### Expected Conduct

**All Visitors Must:**

- Use appropriate language

- Not interfere with school operations
- Not photograph or record without permission
