## Policy Overview

### Purpose

Smart Academy provides internet access to support educational goals. This policy ensures:

- **Educational Focus**: Internet used for learning purposes
- **Student Safety**: Protection from online dangers
- **Ethical Use**: Responsible digital citizenship
- **Resource Protection**: School network and equipment protected
- **Islamic Values**: Online behavior aligned with Islamic teachings

### Philosophy

We believe the internet is a powerful tool that can:
- Enhance learning and research
- Connect students to the world
- Provide access to valuable resources
- Develop digital literacy skills

But requires:
- Clear boundaries and guidelines
- Active supervision and monitoring
- Education about risks and responsibilities
- Balance with other learning activities

### Scope

This policy applies to:
- All internet use on school devices
- All internet use on personal devices at school
- All use of school network
- All online activities related to school

- Research and learning

## Online Safety

### Personal Safety Rules

**Students Will:**
- Never share personal information online
- Never agree to meet someone met online
- Tell an adult about uncomfortable online situations
- Keep passwords private and secure
- Log out of accounts when finished
- Think before posting anything

### Personal Information NOT to Share

**Never share:**
- Full name (use first name only at school)
- Home address
- Phone number
- Birth date
- School name (with location)
- Photos with identifying information
- Passwords
- Parent's information

### Recognizing Dangers

**Students Will Learn to Recognize:**
- Inappropriate content
- Predatory behavior
- Scams and tricks
- Cyberbullying
- Identity theft attempts
- Malware and viruses

### What To Do If...

**You encounter something inappropriate:**
- Close the window immediately
- Tell a teacher right away
- Don't show it to others
- Don't worry about getting in trouble

**Someone makes you uncomfortable online:**
- Stop communicating
- Save evidence (if possible)
- Tell an adult immediately
- Block the person

## Prohibited Activities

### Strictly Prohibited

**Students Will NOT:**

**1. Access Inappropriate Content**
- Pornographic or sexually explicit material
- Violent or graphic content
- Hate speech or extremist content
- Gambling sites
- Drug or alcohol related content
- Any content contrary to Islamic values

**2. Illegal Activities**
- Hacking or unauthorized access
- Downloading pirated material
- Copyright infringement
- Any activity that violates law

**3. Harmful Activities**
- Cyberbullying or harassment
- Spreading rumors or lies
- Impersonating others
- Invasion of privacy
- Intentionally spreading viruses or malware

**4. Wasteful Activities**
- Gaming (unless educational)
- Social media (unless educational)
- Streaming video/music (unless educational)
- Excessive personal use

**5. Network Misuse**
- Attempting to bypass filters
- Using proxy sites or VPNs
- Sharing login information
- Damaging school equipment
- Wasting bandwidth

### Academic Dishonesty

**Prohibited Online Activities:**
- Plagiarism (copying from internet)
- Using AI to write assignments without permission
- Cheating using online resources
- Buying or selling assignments
- Using unauthorized help during online assessments

## Privacy and Security

### Passwords

**Password Rules:**
- Keep passwords secret
- Don't share with friends
- Don't write in obvious places
- Use strong passwords
- Change passwords if compromised

### Account Security

**Students Will:**
- Log out when finished
- Not share accounts
- Report suspicious activity
- Not access others' accounts
- Protect personal information

### Monitoring

**School Monitors:**
- All internet activity on school network
- Email communications on school systems
- Use of school devices
- Compliance with acceptable use

**Students Have No Expectation of Privacy:**
- When using school devices
- When using school network
- For school-related accounts
- Parents may have access to student accounts

## Copyright and Intellectual Property

### Respecting Copyright

**Students Will:**
- Cite sources when using information
- Get permission before using images/media
- Understand what is fair use
- Not claim others' work as their own
- Respect intellectual property rights

### Plagiarism

**Online Plagiarism Includes:**
- Copying and pasting text
- Using images without permission
- Downloading essays
- Using others' ideas without credit
- Presenting online content as own work

**Consequences:**
- Same as other forms of cheating
- See Academic Honesty Policy

### Creative Commons

**Students May Learn To:**
- Find properly licensed content
- Use Creative Commons materials
- Understand different license types
- Cite online sources correctly
- Create and share their own content

---

## Monitoring and Enforcement

### Monitoring Systems

**School Uses:**
- Content filtering
- Activity monitoring
- Website blocking
- Safe search enforcement
- Regular review of usage

### Violation Consequences

**Level I: Minor Violations**
- Warning and education
- Loss of internet privileges temporarily
- Parent notification for repeated issues
- Reflection activity

**Level II: Moderate Violations**
- Loss of internet privileges for extended period
- Parent conference
- Behavior contract
- Disciplinary action

**Level III: Serious Violations**
- Permanent loss of internet privileges
- Suspension
- Expulsion consideration for severe cases
- Report to authorities if illegal

### Appeals Process

**If access is restricted:**
- Student may discuss with teacher
- Parent may request review
- Administration makes final decision
- Educational access maintained as possible

## Digital Citizenship Education

### Curriculum Components

**Students Learn:**
- Digital footprint and online reputation
- Online safety and security
- Information literacy
- Cyberbullying prevention and response
- Copyright and fair use
- Healthy technology habits
- Islamic values in online behavior

### By Grade Level

**Early Childhood (PlayGroup - KG):**
- Introduction to technology
- Basic safety rules
- Teacher-guided exploration only
- No independent internet use

**Primary (Class 1-5):**
- Basic internet safety
- Appropriate websites
- Cyberbullying awareness
- Beginning research skills
- Parental involvement emphasized

**Secondary (Class 6-9):**
- Digital citizenship comprehensive education
- Online safety in depth
- Social media responsibility
- Critical evaluation of online information
- Preparing for independent internet use

### Islamic Values Online

**Students Learn to Apply:**

**1. Haya (Modesty)**
- Appropriate online behavior
- Modest communication
- Respecting others' privacy

**2. 'Adl (Justice)**
- Fair treatment of others online
- Not spreading falsehood
- Giving credit to sources

**3. Amanah (Trustworthiness)**
- Honesty online
- Protecting others' information
- Being reliable in online interactions

**4. Sabr (Patience)**
- Not responding to provocation
- Taking time before posting
- Avoiding online arguments

**5. Rahma (Compassion)**
- Kindness online
- Helping others
- Not causing harm
