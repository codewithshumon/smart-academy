## Policy Overview

### Purpose

Smart Academy provides safe, reliable transportation for students. This policy establishes:

- Clear expectations for student behavior
- Safety procedures for all
- Guidelines for parents and transport staff
- Fee structure and payment
- Emergency procedures

### Service Goals

**We Provide:**
- Safe transportation to and from school
- Reliable service with consistent timing
- Trained and supervised staff
- Well-maintained vehicles
- Clear communication with parents

---

## Transportation Services

### Service Area

**Coverage:**
- Approximately 15km radius from school
- Specific routes determined annually
- Based on demand and feasibility
- Updated before each academic year

### Service Schedule

**Daily Service:**
- Morning pickup: Starting 6:30 AM (route-dependent)
- Afternoon departure: After school ends (approximately 4:00 PM)
- After extracurricular activities: Late departure available

**Days:**
- Sunday through Thursday (school days)
- No service on Fridays, Saturdays, holidays
- No service during school breaks unless special arrangement

### Fleet

**Vehicles:**
- School buses and vans
- Regular maintenance schedule
- Annual safety certification
- Air-conditioned where available
- Smart Academy branding

## Bus Routes and Stops

### Route Planning

**Considerations:**
- Student locations (subject to change)
- Road conditions and traffic
- Travel time limits (maximum 1 hour each way)
- Safety of pickup points
- Accessibility for all students

### Bus Stops

**Designated Stops:**
- Safe locations away from main roads
- Well-lit and visible
- Accessible to students
- Consistent from day to day
- May change based on needs

**Stop Changes:**
- May be adjusted at start of year
- May be adjusted during year for safety
- Parent request considered but not guaranteed
- One week notice for major changes

### Pickup and Drop-Off

**Morning Pickup:**
- Student should be at stop 5 minutes before scheduled time
- Driver will wait maximum 3 minutes
- Students should board quickly and orderly
- Parent should be visible for younger students

**Afternoon Drop-Off:**
- Student will be dropped only at designated stop
- Parent/guardian or authorized person must be present for younger students
- Driver will wait maximum 3 minutes
- Student returned to school if no one present

### Door-to-Door Service

**Not Available:**
- Due to time and route constraints
- Students must come to designated stops
- Exceptions only with special needs approval

## Student Conduct

### On the Bus

**Students Must:**
- Remain seated while bus is moving
- Use seat belt if available
- Keep aisles clear
- Talk quietly
- Respect driver and supervisor
- Keep hands and head inside bus
- Not eat or drink (unless allowed)
- Not damage bus property

### Prohibited Behaviors

**Strictly Prohibited:**
- Standing or moving around while bus is moving
- Throwing objects inside or out of bus
- Bullying or harassment of other students
- Profanity, shouting, or loud noises
- Damage to bus property
- Interfering with driver operation
- Use of electronic devices
- Taking photos/videos of other students

### Consequences

**For Misbehavior:**
- Warning for minor infractions
- Parent contact for repeated issues
- Loss of bus privileges for serious or repeated misbehavior
- Parents must arrange alternative transportation

### Privilege Suspension

**May Result In:**
- Temporary suspension (3 days to full term)
- Permanent suspension in severe cases
- No refund of transportation fees
- Parent responsible for alternative transportation

## Transportation Fees

### Fee Structure

**Annual/Monthly Fee:**
- Based on distance from school
- Number of routes determined annually
- Fees published before each academic year
- Payment due with monthly tuition

**Distance-Based Categories:**
- **Up to 3 km:** Category A
- **3-6 km:** Category B
- **6-10 km:** Category C
- **10+ km:** Category D

**See Fee Structure Document for current fees.**

### Payment

**Payment Schedule:**
- Due monthly with tuition
- Or paid annually in advance
- No prorated monthly fees
- Full month charged even if not used entire month

### Refund Policy

**No Refund For:**
- Days student absent from school
- Days student not using bus
- Temporary suspension from bus
- Mid-month withdrawal (full month charged)

**Partial Refund For:**
- Service cancellation before term begins
- Route cancellation by school

## Safety Procedures

### Driver Responsibilities

**Bus Drivers Must:**
- Have valid driver's license
- Have good driving record
- Follow all traffic laws
- Follow designated routes
- Stop at designated stops only
- Maintain discipline and safety
- Conduct pre-trip inspection
- Follow emergency procedures

### Bus Supervisor

**Each Bus Has:**
- Adult supervisor (if available)
- Responsible for student management
- Liaison with school and parents
- Reports incidents to administration

**Responsibilities:**
- Maintain order on bus
- Ensure students seated properly
- Report discipline issues
- Assist students on/off bus
- Emergency communication if needed

### Emergency Procedures

**Breakdown:**
- Driver contacts school immediately
- Backup bus sent if possible
- Parents notified of delay
- Students kept safe on bus
- Alternative arrangements made

**Accident:**
- Emergency services called immediately
- School and parents notified
- Students evacuated to safe location
- School staff sent to location
- Complete incident report filed

**Medical Emergency:**
- Driver/supervisor trained in first aid
- Emergency services called if needed
- School and parents notified immediately
- Student cared for until help arrives

### Student Release

**Who May Pick Up:**
- Parent or guardian
- Authorized person designated by parent
- Siblings only if approved and age 16+
- Photo ID may be required

### Late Pickups

**If Parent Not Present:**
- Driver waits maximum 3 minutes at stop
- Student returned to school
- Parent contacted
- Parent must pick up from school
- Late pickup fee may apply

## Parent Responsibilities

### Pickup Requirements

**At Designated Stop:**
- Parent/guardian or authorized person must be present
- Be on time (3-minute grace period)
- Contact school if delayed
- Ensure someone is available for pickup

### Changes

**Temporary Changes:**
- Different pickup person
- Address change
- Emergency pickup
- Contact school office by email or phone

**Permanent Changes:**
- New address requires new route
- One week notice for changes
- May affect fee structure
- Subject to space availability

### Communication

**Keep School Informed Of:**
- Address changes
- Emergency contact changes
- Absences from bus service
- Special circumstances
- Concerns about service or other students

## Lost Items

### On Bus

**If Student Leaves Something:**
- Report to driver/supervisor
- Check lost and found at school
- Check bus the next day

## Weather Conditions

### Rainy Weather

**Service Continues:**
- Rain is not a reason to cancel
- Students should be prepared with rain gear
- Parents should still be at stops
- Extra caution used

### Severe Weather

**Service May Be Suspended:**
- Cyclone warnings
- Severe flooding
- Dangerous road conditions
- Parent notification via SMS/phone

### Delays

**Communication:**
- SMS notification to parents
- Phone call for significant delays
- School website updated
- Social media announcement

### Accommodations

**Available For:**
- Students with mobility challenges

- Students requiring special equipment

**Process:**
- Request accommodation at enrollment
- Provide documentation of need
- School determines feasibility
