## Policy Overview

### Purpose

This policy establishes procedures for:
- Reporting lost items
- Turning in found items
- Storing and displaying lost and found items
- Returning items to owners
- Disposing of unclaimed items

### Goals

**We Aim To:**
- Return lost items to owners
- Teach honesty and responsibility
- Maintain organized lost and found system
- Dispose of unclaimed items appropriately
- Prevent loss through education

---

## Reporting Lost Items

### Student Responsibility

**If Item Lost:**
- Report to teacher immediately
- Check lost and found promptly
- Provide description of item
- Check back regularly

### Items to Report

**Students Should Report:**
- Clothing items (uniforms, jackets, etc.)
- School supplies (books, bags, etc.)
- Personal items (watches, jewelry, etc.)
- Electronic devices (if brought without permission)
- Money (small amounts only)

### Reporting Process

**Student Should:**
1. Tell teacher or parent
2. Describe what was lost
3. Say where it was last seen
4. Provide identifying information
5. Check lost and found regularly

### Parent Communication

**If Valuable Item Lost:**
- Contact school office
- Provide description
- Student name and class
- Date and location lost
- Contact information

---

## Finding Items

### Student Responsibility

**If Student Finds Item:**
- Turn it in to teacher immediately
- Not keep it for themselves
- Not give it to another student
- Explain where it was found

### Honesty Encouraged

**We Teach:**
- Honesty is Islamic value
- Returning lost items is obligatory
- Keeping lost items is wrong
- Reward for honest behavior

### What to Do With Found Item

**Steps:**
1. Pick up item if safe and appropriate
2. Bring to teacher or office
3. Describe where found
4. Provide identifying features
5. Receive credit for honesty

## Lost and Found Procedures

### Location

**Lost and Found Located:**
- Main office
- Each classroom has small lost and found box
- Items moved to main office regularly
- Displayed for easy viewing

### Organization

**Items Organized By:**
- Type (clothing, books, accessories, etc.)
- Grade level when possible
- Date found
- Description

### Logging System

**For Items Turned In:**
- Date and time
- Location found
- Description of item
- Name of finder (optional, for recognition)
- Storage location

### Display

**Visible Items:**
- Clothing items hung on rack
- Small items in display boxes
- Labeled by grade level
- Accessible during school hours

### Storage

**Items Stored:**
- In secure storage area
- Organized by category
- Retained for specified period
- Disposed of appropriately

---

## Claiming Items

### Identification Required

**To Claim Item:**
- Describe item accurately
- Show ID if needed
- Provide proof of ownership if needed
- Sign log when claiming

### Claiming Process

**For Students:**
- Identify item with description
- Verify ownership
- Teacher may assist with identification
- Sign claim log
- Item returned

**For Parents:**
- Come to main office
- Describe lost item
- Provide student information
- Sign claim log
- Item returned if matched

### Claiming Period

**Items Kept For:**
- Full term (4 months)
- Items found near end of term kept to start of next term
- Valuable items kept longer
- Unclaimed items then disposed

---

## Unclaimed Items

### Disposal Process

**Clothing Items:**
- Kept for one term
- If not claimed, washed
- Donated to charity
- Or added to uniform exchange

**Books and Supplies:**
- Kept for one term
- Given to class library if appropriate
- Donated if not claimed
- Recycled if not usable

**Personal Items:**
- Kept for one term
- If valuable, held longer
- Dispose of appropriately
- Not returned to student

### Notification Before Disposal

**At End of Term:**
- Notice sent to parents
- List of unclaimed items displayed
- One week notice given
- After one week, items disposed

### Charitable Donation

**Unclaimed Items May Be Donated To:**
- Local charities
- Clothing drives
- School uniform exchange
- Community service programs

---

## Valuable Items

### Special Handling

**Valuable Items Include:**
- Electronics (phones, watches, etc.)
- Jewelry
- Significant amounts of money
- Special personal items

**These Items:**
- Kept in secure storage
- Returned only to owner or parent
- Kept for full academic year
- May be turned over to administration

### Electronic Devices

**If Found:**
- Turned in to office immediately
- Stored securely
- Logged with description
- Parent contacted
- Returned to parent (not student)

**Note:** Electronic devices should not be brought to school. See Mobile Device Policy.

---

## Specific Items

### Uniforms and Clothing

**Labeled Uniforms:**
- Easily returned when labeled
- Checked for name label
- Returned to student
- Unlabeled items go to lost and found

### Books and Supplies

**School Books:**
- Name and class written inside
- Easily identified
- Returned to student
- Damaged books assessed for replacement cost

### Personal Items

**Toys and Accessories:**
- Kept for one term
- If not claimed, disposed of appropriately
- Considered donation if in good condition

---

## Prevention

### Labeling

**Parents Should:**
- Label all clothing items
- Label school supplies
- Label bags and lunchboxes
- Label water bottles
- Update labels as child grows

### Personal Responsibility

**Students Taught To:**
- Keep track of belongings
- Put items away properly
- Check area before leaving
- Take only what belongs to them
- Report lost items promptly

### Regular Checks

**Students Should:**
- Check for belongings before leaving classroom
- Check locker/desk before leaving
- Check playground/recess areas
- Check bus before exiting

### Parent Support

**Parents Can Help By:**
- Labeling all items clearly
- Checking child's backpack regularly
- Teaching child to care for belongings
- Replacing labels when they fade
- Checking lost and found regularly

---

## Staff Responsibilities

### Teachers

**Will:**
- Promote honesty about found items
- Turn in items to office promptly
- Remind students to check for belongings
- Provide opportunities to check lost and found
- Model responsible behavior

### Office Staff

**Will:**
- Maintain organized lost and found
- Log all items
- Display items appropriately
- Return items to owners
- Dispose of unclaimed items according to policy

## Statement of Understanding

### Student Pledge

> "I understand that I am responsible for my belongings. If I lose something, I will report it to my teacher. If I find something that doesn't belong to me, I will turn it in. I understand that honesty is important and keeping lost items is wrong."

### Parent Acknowledgment

> "I understand the lost and found policy. I will teach my child to be responsible for their belongings. I will label all items clearly. I will check lost and found if my child loses something. I understand that unclaimed items may be donated after one term."
