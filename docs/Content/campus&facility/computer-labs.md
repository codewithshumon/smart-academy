# Computer Laboratories

## IT Facilities and Equipment

Smart Academy provides two state-of-the-art computer laboratories equipped with modern technology to develop digital literacy and prepare students for the technology-driven future.

---

## Laboratory Overview

### Two Dedicated Computer Labs

**Lab 1 (Primary Section)**
- **Computers:** 30 workstations
- **Target:** PlayGroup - Class 5
- **Software:** Age-appropriate educational programs
- **Focus:** Digital literacy fundamentals

**Lab 2 (Secondary Section)**
- **Computers:** 35 workstations
- **Target:** Class 6-9
- **Software:** Advanced programming and productivity tools
- **Focus:** Coding, design, research skills

**Total Capacity:** 65 students per session  
**Student-Computer Ratio:** 3:1  
**Operating Hours:** 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM (extended for projects)

### Computer Systems

**Standard Configuration:**
- **Processor:** Intel Core i5 or equivalent

- **Storage:** 256GB SSD
- **Display:** 21-22 inch LED monitors
- **OS:** Windows 10/11 Education Edition
- **Peripherals:** Keyboard, mouse, headphones

- **Firewall:** Enterprise-grade protection

### Power & Climate Control
