### 1. Electrical Systems

- **UPS Systems:** For computers and critical equipment
- **LED Lighting:** Energy efficient throughout
- **Power Points:** Adequate in all rooms

### 2. Water & Sanitation
- **Water Supply:** Municipal + deep tube well
- **Water Treatment:** Filtration and purification systems
- **Drinking Water:** Filtered water stations throughout
