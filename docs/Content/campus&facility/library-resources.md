# Library & Learning Resources

## Knowledge Hub of Smart Academy

Our library is the heart of academic life at Smart Academy, housing over 5,000 books across multiple languages and subjects. More than just a collection of books, our library is a vibrant learning center that fosters reading culture, supports research, and provides a peaceful space for study and reflection.

---

## Library Overview

**Total Area:** 2,500 sq. ft. (Two floors)  
**Collection Size:** 5,000+ books  
**Seating Capacity:** 80 students  
**Operating Hours:** 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM (Sunday-Thursday)

### Mission
To cultivate lifelong learners by providing access to quality resources, promoting reading culture, and supporting academic excellence in an Islamic educational environment.

## Library Rules & Etiquette

**Expected Behavior:**
- Silence in quiet zones
- Respectful of others
- Careful with books
- Return on time
- No food or drinks
- Mobile phones on silent

**Book Care:**
- Handle gently
- No writing or marking
- Keep clean and dry
- Report damage
- Use bookmarks

**Consequences:**
- Overdue fines (minimal, educational)
- Lost book replacement
- Damaged book repair costs
- Suspension of privileges (severe cases)
