# Prayer & Islamic Learning Facilities

## Spiritual Heart of Smart Academy

Our prayer facilities and Islamic learning spaces provide students with a peaceful environment to practice their faith, learn Quran, and develop Islamic character in accordance with the Sunnah.

## Daily Prayer Schedule

### Five Daily Prayers

**Fajr:** Before school (for boarding students - future)
**Dhuhr:** 12:30 PM - 20 minutes
**Asr:** After school - 15 minutes
**Maghrib:** After school hours
**Isha:** After school hours

### Jumu'ah (Friday Prayer)
- Boys: Congregational prayer at school
- Duration: 45 minutes (12:00-12:45 PM)
- Khutbah (sermon) by qualified imam
- Girls: Dhuhr prayer or home

### Special Prayers
- Eid prayers (entire community)
- Taraweeh during Ramadan
- Janazah prayers (when needed)
- Special occasions

## Prayer Etiquette & Guidelines

### Before Prayer
- Perform wudu properly
- Remove shoes
- Check direction (Qibla)
- Turn off mobile phones
- Arrive on time

### During Prayer
- Silence and focus
- Follow the imam
- Complete movements
- Respect others' space
- No talking

### After Prayer
- Recite du'as
- Brief dhikr
- Orderly exit
- Return prayer mats (if used)
- Leave space clean
