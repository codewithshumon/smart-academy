# Campus Overview

## Welcome to Smart Academy Campus

Smart Academy is situated on a sprawling **2.5-acre campus** in Narimpur, Ramganj, Lakshmipur, providing a modern, safe, and inspiring learning environment in a rural setting. Our thoughtfully designed campus combines state-of-the-art facilities with natural green spaces, creating an ideal atmosphere for holistic education that integrates Islamic values, STEAM learning, and character development.

---

## Campus Location

**Address:**  
Smart Academy  
Narimpur, Ramganj  
Lakshmipur District, Bangladesh

**Geographic Context:**
- Located in a rural area, bridging the urban-rural educational divide
- Easily accessible from Ramganj and surrounding villages
- 15km service radius for transportation
- Peaceful environment away from urban distractions
- Surrounded by natural greenery and agricultural landscape
