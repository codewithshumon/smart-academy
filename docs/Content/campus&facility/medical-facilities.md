# Medical & Health Facilities

## Student Health & Wellness Center

Smart Academy maintains comprehensive medical facilities with a full-time nurse and visiting doctor to ensure the health, safety, and wellbeing of all students.

---

## Medical Room Overview

**Location:** Ground floor, near main entrance  
**Area:** 300 sq. ft.  
**Staff:** Full-time nurse (8 AM - 3:30 PM)  
**Visiting Doctor:** Twice weekly (Tue & Thu)  
**Emergency Contact:** 24/7 access

### Mission
To provide immediate medical care, promote student health, prevent illness, and ensure a safe learning environment for all students.

## Emergency Procedures

### Emergency Response Protocol

**Level 1 - Minor Injuries:**
1. Nurse provides first aid
2. Student returns to class
3. Parent informed via note
4. Record maintained

**Level 2 - Moderate Concerns:**
1. Nurse assessment
2. Contact parents immediately
3. Parent decides: pickup or monitor
4. Follow-up next day

**Level 3 - Serious Emergency:**
1. Immediate first aid
2. Call emergency services
3. Contact parents urgently
4. Accompany to hospital
5. Staff member with student
6. Document all actions
