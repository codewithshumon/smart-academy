# Transportation Services

## Safe, Reliable, GPS-Tracked School Buses

Smart Academy operates a fleet of modern, GPS-tracked school buses providing safe and convenient transportation for students within a 15km radius of the campus.

---

## Transportation Overview

**Fleet Size:** 8 GPS-tracked buses  
**Service Radius:** 15km from campus  
**Students Served:** 250+ daily  
**Operating Days:** Sunday - Thursday  
**Routes:** Multiple routes covering Narimpur, Ramganj, and surrounding areas

### Mission
To provide safe, punctual, and comfortable transportation that allows students from surrounding villages to access quality education at Smart Academy.

---

## Bus Fleet Specifications

### Modern School Buses

**Bus Types:**
- **Large Buses (4 units):** 40-seat capacity
- **Medium Buses (3 units):** 30-seat capacity
- **Small Bus (1 unit):** 20-seat capacity

**Vehicle Features:**
- Comfortable padded seats

- First aid kit onboard
- Fire extinguisher
- Emergency exits clearly marked
- GPS tracking device

**Safety Features:**
- Regular maintenance checks
- Daily pre-trip inspections
- Roadworthy certificates
- Insurance coverage
- Emergency equipment
- Reflective tape for visibility
- Working lights and indicators

## Safety Protocols

### Driver Requirements

**Qualifications:**
- Valid driver's license (minimum 5 years)
- Clean driving record
- Background verification
- Police clearance
- Medical fitness certificate
- First aid training
- Child protection training

**Ongoing Training:**
- Defensive driving courses
- Emergency response
- Student management
- Islamic behavior and character
- Annual refresher training

### Bus Attendants

**On Every Bus:**
- Female attendants on all buses
- Supervise students
- Attendance tracking
- Behavior management
- First aid trained
- Emergency response

**Responsibilities:**
- Student safety
- Orderly boarding/exiting
- Communication with parents
- Report any issues
- Maintain discipline

### Safety Rules for Students

**Before Boarding:**
- Wait at designated stop
- Stand back from road
- Board only when bus stops
- Show ID/pass

**On the Bus:**
- Sit immediately
- Wear seat belt
- Stay seated while moving
- Keep aisles clear
- No eating/drinking
- No loud noise
- Respect driver and attendant
- No bullying

**When Exiting:**
- Wait for complete stop
- Exit in orderly manner
- Cross road safely
- Go directly home
