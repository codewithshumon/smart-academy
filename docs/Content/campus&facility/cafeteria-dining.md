# Cafeteria & Dining Facilities

## Nutritious, Halal, Affordable Meals

Smart Academy's cafeteria provides healthy, delicious, and 100% halal meals in a clean, welcoming environment that supports students' physical growth and academic performance.

---

## Cafeteria Overview

**Area:** 1,800 sq. ft.  
**Seating Capacity:** 200 students  
**Service Hours:** 7:30 AM - 2:30 PM  
**Operating Days:** Sunday - Thursday

### Mission
To provide nutritious, affordable, halal meals that support student health and learning while teaching healthy eating habits and Islamic food ethics.

## Rules & Etiquette

### Expected Behavior

**Before Eating:**
- Wash hands
- Say Bismillah
- Queue properly
- Choose food quickly

**While Eating:**
- Sit at tables
- Use utensils properly
- Talk quietly
- No food fights
- Clean up spills

**After Eating:**
- Say Alhamdulillah
- Clear your plate
- Dispose waste properly
- Return trays
- Wash hands

### Consequences
- Warnings for misbehavior
- Parent notification
- Cafeteria suspension (serious cases)
- Character education
