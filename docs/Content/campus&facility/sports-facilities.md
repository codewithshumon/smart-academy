# Sports & Recreation Facilities

## Building Healthy Bodies and Teamwork

Smart Academy provides comprehensive sports facilities that promote physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition while respecting Islamic values of modesty and character development.

**Area:** 2,500 sq. ft.

- Marked boundaries for multiple sports
