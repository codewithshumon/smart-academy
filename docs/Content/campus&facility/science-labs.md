# Science Laboratories

## World-Class Science Education Facilities

Smart Academy operates four fully-equipped science laboratories providing hands-on experimental learning. Our labs bridge theory and practice, fostering scientific curiosity and critical thinking.

---

## Laboratory Overview

### Four Specialized Labs

1. **Physics Laboratory** - Mechanics, electricity, optics
2. **Chemistry Laboratory** - Chemical reactions and analysis
3. **Biology Laboratory** - Life sciences and microscopy
4. **Combined Science Lab (Primary)** - Age-appropriate experiments
5. **Robotics & Innovation Lab** - STEAM integration

**Total Lab Space:** 4,000 sq. ft.  
**Weekly Lab Sessions:** 50+ hours across all grades

---

## Physics Laboratory

**Area:** 800 sq. ft. | **Stations:** 10 students

### Equipment
- Mechanics: Pendulum sets, inclined planes, pulleys
- Electricity: Circuit boards, multimeters, sensors
- Optics: Lenses, mirrors, prisms, light boxes
- Modern Physics: Digital sensors, data loggers

### Safety Features
- Fire extinguishers, first aid, safety goggles
- Emergency protocols, proper ventilation

---

## Chemistry Laboratory

**Area:** 800 sq. ft. | **Fume Hoods:** 2 units

### Equipment
- Complete glassware sets
- Bunsen burners (12), gas connections
- Digital balances, pH meters
- Safety showers, eye wash stations
- Organized chemical storage with MSDS

### Safety Protocols
- Separate acid storage
- Spill kits, protective equipment
- Proper ventilation and waste disposal

---

## Biology Laboratory

**Area:** 800 sq. ft. | **Microscopes:** 15 student + 1 demonstration

### Equipment
- Microscopy: 200+ prepared slides, staining materials

- Models: Skeleton, organs, DNA, anatomical charts
- Live specimens: Aquarium, terrarium, incubator

### Special Features
- Digital microscope with projection
- Specimen preparation area

## Laboratory Safety

### General Rules
- Wear PPE (goggles, lab coats)
- Follow procedures exactly
- Report spills immediately
- Wash hands after experiments

### Emergency Procedures
- Fire: Extinguisher, evacuate if needed
- Spills: Contain, use spill kit
- Injuries: First aid, notify nurse
